### Specifications

**General**: The sink is designed as a self-rimming type and is recessed for a seamless appearance.

**Design Features**: It includes a highlighted finish that protects against heavy use and minimizes sound with a sound-deadening coating.

**Optional Accessories**: Compatible accessories include LKRB715, LKRB1614, LKERBSS, DB1420, and cutting tools CBT2213, SC1407. All products adhere to ANSI A112.19.3M standards set by the International Association of Plumbing and Mechanical Officials, following the Uniform Plumbing Code (UPC).
