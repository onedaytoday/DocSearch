The Ice KG system is designed for efficient catering operations, accommodating various temperature ranges from ambient to sub-zero. Installation is straightforward, requiring only a water supply and hoses. The unit features easy cleaning capabilities using AISI-grade food-safe materials and ABS, ensuring simple operation and maintenance, with convenient front access. Environmentally friendly with a cooling output of 21°C, it performs optimally with a 15°C supply.
