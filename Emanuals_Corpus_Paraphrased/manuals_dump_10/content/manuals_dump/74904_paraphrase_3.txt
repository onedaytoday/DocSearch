**Food Gear Overview**

Food Gear is utilized in supermarkets for handling various foods and cooked meats. This system features three main components designed for efficiency and ease of maintenance. The gear is robust and can operate smoothly without Teflon. It incorporates a top-mounted cover that facilitates operations while providing safety during cleaning. The design allows for easy cleaning once the unit is turned off, ensuring permanent protection during both operation and maintenance. It is specifically tailored for slicing, offering IP42 protection standards and is certified by CE and NSF.
