**EDVR-120 Overview**

The ELMO Corp EDVR-120 operates as a VCR, available in configurations with and without recording capabilities. It integrates advanced software that facilitates network connectivity. The EDVR-120 allows for interfacing with the ESD-CC1, another ELMO solution. Key features include:

- VCR recording functionality
- High refresh rates with recordings at various FPS
- Efficient search and playback of recorded material, including title protection and archiving through a 1.44MB floppy disk
- Interruption recovery via RS-232
- Remote access and control through Ethernet and RS-485

**EDVR-120 Specifications:**

- Supports NTSC with BNC connections
- Optional IDE format with M-JPEG archiving
- High frame rate recording and motion detection capabilities
- Comprehensive search functions, including character search on screen
- N.O. or N.C. recording options
- Recovery of operation interruptions

**Physical Specifications:**

- AC operation at 240V, allowed temperature range from 45°C to 113°F
- Mountable as a 2U device
