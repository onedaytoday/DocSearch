**Weather-Resistant Night QNW4000 Overview**

Introducing the new Weather-Resistant CCD featuring a durable housing and an integrated mechanical Day/Night function for optimal performance in varying light conditions. The QNW4000 delivers a resolution of 480 TV lines in black and white, operational 24/7.

**Specifications:**

- **Model:** QNW4000
- **Power Supply:** AC 24V
- **Power Consumption:** Approximately 5.0W
- **Image Sensor:** CCD (768(H) x 494(V))
- **Format:** NTSC
- **Sync Options:** Line-lock / Internal
- **Resolution:** 
  - Horizontal: 480 TVL
  - Vertical: 350 TVL
- **Minimum Illumination:** More than (F1.4)
- **Features:** Day/Night functionality, Auto White Balance (ATW) (factory preset OFF)
- **Output:** Composite VBS via RCA connection
- **Scanning Technique:** Effective TV scanning
- **Signal-to-Noise Ratio:** Not specified
- **Automatic Gain Control:** Included
