**Ice KG Overview**

The Ice KG is designed for versatile use in catering, accommodating a range of operating hours. It offers straightforward installation with accessible water supply and hoses. The unit features easy cleaning capabilities, constructed from AISI food-grade ABS, prioritizing user-friendly operation and maintenance, with front access for convenience.

**Technical Specifications**

The LEGEND CWI Cold Water system operates efficiently at temperatures of 21°C for ambient supply and 15°C for cooling. The electrical models vary, including RIMC067SA, RIMC067SW, RIMC085SA, RIMC085SW, FGC65A42, FGC90A, and FGC90W, with configurations for single-phase electric supply. Each model is designed for specific height installations, ensuring adaptability in diverse settings.
