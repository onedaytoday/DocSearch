### Product Overview

**Touchmonitor Specifications**  
This 1739L LCD touchmonitor features:

- SXGA resolution with active TFT technology, ensuring high-quality graphics performance.
- Compatibility with various video standards including VGA, SVGA, XGA, and SXGA (non-interlaced).
- Support for VESA DPMS and DDC2B standards.
- An advanced On-Screen Display (OSD) for easy user interaction.

---

### Installation and Setup

**Unpacking the Touchmonitor**  
Upon receiving the touchmonitor, make sure the following items are included: LCD unit, USB cable, QuickStart guide, and software CD. 

**Connecting the Touchmonitor**  
Ensure that both the monitor and PC are powered off before connecting. For USB connections, use the appropriate port on the monitor and PC. Tighten screws to secure connections. Power the unit with a 12V DC adapter.

---

### Operating Instructions

**Using the OSD**  
Access the OSD to display various adjustment options including brightness, contrast, and positioning. Ensure the touchmonitor's performance settings are aligned with factory specifications for optimal usage.

**OSD Functions**  
- **MENU**: Opens or exits the OSD.
- **UP/DOWN**: Adjust values or navigate through items in the menu.
- **AUTO/SEL**: Automatically configures settings based on input.

**Energy Efficiency**  
The monitor complies with VESA Display Power Management Signaling (DPMS) standards to reduce power consumption.

### Paraphrased Text

**A-27 SXGA LCD Orientation**  
The A-27 has an SXGA LCD with vertical alignment. The term "SXGA" relates to the input compatibility of the LCD. When the input exceeds the LCD's specifications, it can result in artifacts affecting the display quality, including character rendering. 

**Touchmonitor Safety Information**  
This section covers important safety details for the touchmonitor, including installation and operational guidance found in Chapter Installation and Chapter Operation. To minimize the risk of electric shock, always power down the touchmonitor before cleaning it. The power and interface connectors are located at the rear and top of the device. If the touchmonitor malfunctions, do not attempt repairs without professional assistance.

**Touchmonitor Maintenance**  
For optimal touchscreen performance, apply cleaners sparingly, avoiding direct contact with the touchscreen. Note that the device contains mercury within the backlight and complies with EU directives regarding disposal; it should be taken to a waste recovery facility.
