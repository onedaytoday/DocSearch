### Features of the XBM30

The XBM30 delivers exceptional performance for specialty breads and other fundamental functions. It's designed to be used with the recommended dough types. The item is equipped with a universal planetary mechanism that provides protection against overloads. Additionally, the planetary whisk ensures effective mixing while facilitating easy cleaning.
