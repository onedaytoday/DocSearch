**EL2010 User Manual**

**IMPORTANT SAFETY INSTRUCTIONS**  
Ensure to read all guidelines before operating this vacuum cleaner. 

**WARNING:** To minimize risks of fire or electric shock:  
- Always use the specified charger (model KSAS3R51800010VUD).  
- Avoid wrapping the cord around sharp edges or exposing it to heat sources.  
- Do not mutilate or incinerate batteries; they may explode at high temperatures.  
- Keep batteries and terminals free from dust and debris. 

**Important Note:** Use this vacuum cleaner only in residential settings. For recycling information, visit www.electrolux.com.

**COMPONENTS AND ACCESSORIES**  
Features include:  
- On/Off Switch  
- Locking Mechanism  
- Charging Station   
- Brush/Crevice Release Buttons  

**UNPACKING AND CHECKING CONTENTS**  
Carefully remove all items from the box and check for completeness. 

**CHARGING STATION**  
Position the charging station away from direct sunlight or heat sources. Ensure the unit is properly connected to the outlet and that the charging light indicates successful charging. 

**EMPTYING AND CLEANING**  
- Regularly empty and clean the dust container (recommended after each use).  
- Release the relevant buttons to access components for cleaning.  
- Ensure the vacuum operates smoothly without obstructions in hoses or wheels. 

**TROUBLESHOOTING**  
If the vacuum shuts off unexpectedly:  
1. Ensure the brush roll is disengaged and allow the unit to cool.  
2. Check for obstructions in the hose or charging station.  
3. Confirm the unit is charged.
