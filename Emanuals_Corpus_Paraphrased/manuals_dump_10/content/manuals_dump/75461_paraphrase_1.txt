**S90 Pass-Through Island (Model WGGWABQQOO)**

The S90 Pass-Through Island features four BTU inserts, designed for versatile installations that operate at high performance levels. Each unit is equipped with internal thermocouples for precise temperature control, ensuring efficient cooking capabilities. The top section can connect to adjacent units, creating a cohesive cooking space. 

Both a two-year service warranty and a one-year installation warranty are provided. For further details, visit www.electroluxusa.com/professional or contact SW Telephone. 

This model supports both high-intensity cooking and customer needs, offering an option for easy front and internal cleaning. The unit also incorporates a round plinth for aesthetic purposes, with an option for a portioning shelf to enhance functionality. 

When specifying the S90 Pass-Through Island, please include the LPG NPT information, cavity height, and depth, and ensure appropriate heat insulation is fitted if necessary. Electrolux reserves the right to modify specifications without prior notice.
