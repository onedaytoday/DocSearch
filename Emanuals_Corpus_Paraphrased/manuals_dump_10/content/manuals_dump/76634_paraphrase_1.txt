### RANGE HOOD Instructions

**IMPORTANT SAFETY INSTRUCTIONS FOR RESIDENTIAL USE ONLY**

**READ AND SAVE THESE INSTRUCTIONS**

Before proceeding, please read the entire set of instructions. 

**IMPORTANT NOTES:**
- Inspectors and installers must review these instructions with the owner.
- Owners should keep these instructions for future reference, especially during cleaning and household cooking processes.

**WARNINGS:**
- To minimize the risk of fire or electric shock, follow these precautions:
  - Disconnect the power before servicing or cleaning the hood.
  - Ensure the service panel is securely locked.
  
**GREASE FIRE SAFETY:**
- To reduce the risk of grease fires on the stovetop:
  - Keep the cooking surface clean and clear of grease buildup.
  - Always monitor cooking at high heat.
  - Use appropriate cookware and maintain proper heat settings.

**IN CASE OF GREASE FIRE:**
- Smother flames with a lid or similar object; never pick up a flaming pan.
- Avoid using water to extinguish flames.
- If the fire escalates, evacuate the area and contact emergency services.

**INSTALLATION INSTRUCTIONS:**
- Follow local codes and standards, including those provided by the National Fire Protection Association (NFPA) and ASHRAE.
- Use only metal ductwork for safety.
- Appliances may pose dangers under specific conditions; supervise food while cooking to prevent fires.

**OPERATING INSTRUCTIONS:**
- Familiarize yourself with the operation settings, including speed control and lighting options.
- Utilize the timer and intensive function as described to optimize performance.
- This model includes an automatic stop feature after a set period.

**MAINTENANCE:**
- Regularly clean the unit after cooking to prevent grease accumulation.
- It is recommended to wash removable parts in warm, soapy water or in the dishwasher.
- For electrical components, ensure the unit is plugged into a 125V 5A outlet.

**INSTALLATION AND USAGE RECOMMENDATIONS:**
- Carefully follow installation and operational instructions to ensure the safe and efficient use of the range hood. 

Maintain proper care and follow all safety guidelines to prolong the lifespan of the appliance.

### Safety Warnings for Reducing the Risk of Personal Injury and Fire

**In the Event of Surface Grease Ignition:**

1. **Extinguish the Flames:** Take utmost care during this process to prevent burns. Evacuate the area and call the fire department.
2. **Never Touch a Hot Pan.**
3. **Do Not Use Water.**

**Installation Guidelines:**

To minimize the risk of fire, electric shock, and personal injury, adhere to the following:

1. **Consult the National Fire Protection Association (NFPA) and the American Society of Heating, Refrigerating and Air-Conditioning Engineers (ASHRAE).**
2. **Use Only Metal Ducts.**
3. **Note that some appliances may pose hazards.**

**Installation Instructions:**

- **Operating Instructions:** Read and retain these guidelines.
- **General Information:** Follow installation procedures carefully, ensuring proper wall mounting using the provided screws.
- **Controls:** Utilize the speed settings (Low, Medium, High) and the automatic shut-off timer.

**Maintenance Note:** Ensure a 125V, 5A fuse is in place.
