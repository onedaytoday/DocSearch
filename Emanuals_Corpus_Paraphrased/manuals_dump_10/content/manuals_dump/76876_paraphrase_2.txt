**SPECIFICATIONS**

The general design of the product is a self-rimming type with a recessed structure. It features an exposed polished finish that highlights its aesthetic appeal while providing heavy-duty sound protection. Note: unless stated otherwise, the product is provided as illustrated. The suffix "R" denotes the right side.

**OPTIONAL ACCESSORIES:**
- Cutting Boards: LKRB1520, LKRB715, or LKERBSS.
- Additional cutting boards: CB816, CB1516, CB1713, or CBS715.

All components conform to ANSI A112.19.3M standards, as recognized by the International Association of Plumbing and Mechanical Officials (IAPMO) according to the Uniform Plumbing Code (UPC).

**SINK DIMENSIONS (INCHES):**
- Model options include: PSMR3322, PSMR3322R (right side), and various configurations for faucet holes ranging from one to six, as designated by their specific model numbers (e.g., PSMR33221 for two faucet holes, PSMR33226 for six faucet holes).

**COUNTERTOP SHIP DIMENSIONS:** Refer to the reverse side for precise product dimensions. All provided dimensions are in inches; to convert to millimeters, multiply by the appropriate factor.

**HOLE DRILLING CONFIGURATIONS:**
- Sequential model specifications for faucet hole configurations are available, such as PSMR3322R1 (one faucet hole) up to PSMR3322R6 (six faucet holes).
