**ELMO ESD-CC2X Specifications**  
The ELMO ESD-CC2X features an RS-485 interface alongside a PTZ-compatible LCD and built-in protection functions. It includes an RJ11 joy connector and supports both RS232 and RS485 communication protocols, complete with parity settings. The unit is equipped with a graphic LCD and a real-time clock integrated circuit, powered by a DC source of 12V at 1.5A.
