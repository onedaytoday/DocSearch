### Preparation for Use

**Unpacking and Setup:**  
Remove the unit from its packaging carefully and position it on a flat, stable surface. Ensure it is connected to a 110V AC power source, avoiding placement near heat sources or high humidity areas.

#### Battery Backup Installation

Locate the battery compartment as shown in the manual. Insert the included 9V battery, ensuring it connects properly. This battery will maintain the clock's functionality during brief power interruptions. 

#### Power Failure Indication

If the clock loses power or if the battery is exhausted, the clock display will flash, indicating that the time is incorrect.

### Operating Instructions

#### Setting the Clock

1. Press and hold the CLOCK and HOUR buttons simultaneously until the display starts to flash.
2. Set the correct hour (AM or PM) and release the buttons once set.
3. Press CLOCK and MINUTE buttons to set the minutes.

#### Setting the Wake-Up Time

1. Press ALARM and HOUR buttons simultaneously to set the hour for the alarm, then release.
2. Repeat with the MINUTE button to finalize the alarm time.

#### Listening to the Radio

Switch between AM and FM using the BAND switch. Adjust VOLUME to your liking and use the TUNING control to select your desired station. 

#### Wake to Radio or Buzzer

- For radio: Set the alarm to the desired station, ensuring the switch is in the 'ON' position.
- For buzzer: Set the alarm switch to 'BUZ'.

#### SNOOZE Feature

When the alarm sounds, press the SNOOZE button to temporarily silence it and activate it again after a few minutes.

#### Sleep Timer

Set the sleep timer to enjoy music before falling asleep. Adjust the SLEEP and MINUTE buttons to choose the auto-off duration.
