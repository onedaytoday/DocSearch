## Important Safety Instructions
To ensure safety, please adhere to the following guidelines regarding the appliance's placement and use. It is essential that the unit is installed as per the instructions provided. The back of the refrigerator must not be exposed to heat sources. Always allow the appliance to stabilize after transportation before use. If there is any sign of malfunction, such as unusual noises or ignition, disconnect the unit immediately. Keep the appliance out of reach of children to prevent the risk of suffocation. Use care when handling cleaning products and ensure the power supply is turned off prior to cleaning or servicing. Only qualified service personnel should conduct repairs.

---

## Appliance Description
The refrigerator includes adjustable shelves, a thermostat, and designated areas for fresh food storage.

### Operation
To adjust the temperature, turn the thermostat to the desired setting (O for off). Depending on food storage requirements or ambient conditions, you may need to modify settings more frequently. Always ensure food is properly wrapped and stored. Follow best practices for food storage to maintain quality.

---

## Health and Safety Guidelines
To enhance food safety, shop weekly and monitor food temperatures. Cooked food should be cooled before being placed in the refrigerator, and raw meat must be stored separately and securely.

---

## Defrosting
The defrosting mechanism is located at the back of the appliance. Regular defrosting ensures optimal performance.

---

## Maintenance and Cleaning
Always disconnect the appliance from the power supply prior to cleaning. Avoid using abrasive cleaners, strong detergents, or waxes inside the unit as they can damage surfaces. Ensure that drainage pathways are clear, and replace the light bulb per the provided instructions.

---

## Troubleshooting
If you experience issues such as excessive frost buildup or malfunctioning lights, consult the troubleshooting section. Ensure that the thermostat is set correctly between 10°C to 32°C.
