### Instruction Manual ERC 07002W8 (GB FR DE)

**Overview:**
This manual offers guidance on product selection, usage, and maintenance to ensure user convenience and safety. It contains essential information and tips for proper operation.

**Installation and Safety:**
Ensure that the installation follows the provided instructions for optimal performance. Special attention is required for cleaning and servicing, particularly for specialized components such as cords. Cleaning and maintenance should be performed exclusively by qualified professionals to avoid risk.

**Safety Precautions:**
- Keep the device out of reach of children to prevent accidents.
- Avoid exposure to heat or aggressive cleaning agents.
- Ensure cords are properly managed to avoid overheating.

**Cooling and Temperature Control:**
It's essential to maintain optimal cooling temperatures for food preservation, with specific temperature guidelines outlined for various food types. The thermostatic settings should be adjusted as needed to ensure food is kept at safe temperatures (at or below 0°C).

**Defrosting:**
Regular defrosting is necessary to maintain efficiency. Build-up of frost can compromise cooling capabilities, leading to increased energy consumption. Follow the defined procedures for defrosting to prevent damage.

**Cleaning:**
Routine cleaning of all compartments is required to maintain hygiene. Use appropriate cleaning supplies to avoid damage to surfaces.

**Troubleshooting:**
If issues arise, consult the troubleshooting section for common solutions and ensure that the device operates correctly. If problems persist, professional servicing may be needed.

**Warranty and Service Information:**
For service inquiries or warranty claims, please contact the designated service center, providing the product information and proof of purchase.
