### Features

The DITOMIX CHROME Professional planetary mixer, equipped with the DITOMIX5, excels in rigorous whisking tasks. The DITOMIX5 beater attachments are designed for efficiently whipping egg whites, making chantilly cream, and preparing shortcrust pastry. This accessory enhances the mixer’s performance by optimizing activation and adjustments in speed. It caters to a variety of mixing needs, including choux pastry, shortcrust, and more.
