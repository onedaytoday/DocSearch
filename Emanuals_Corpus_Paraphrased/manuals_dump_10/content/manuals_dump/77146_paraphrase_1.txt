### OSD Functionality

The OSD (On-Screen Display) allows adjustment of parameters such as brightness and phase. You can set the horizontal position (H-Position) of the OSD for different configurations: factory settings, C1/C2 settings, or USER mode, with color temperatures either 9300K or 6500K. Confirm your selection by pressing the SELECT button. You can also individually adjust settings like red (R), green (G), and blue (B) colors within the USER mode through the OSD.

To modify the contrast using the OSD, press the SELECT button and navigate accordingly. The OSD will automatically close after a few seconds of inactivity. This system supports multiple languages, including English and Spanish, for user accessibility.
