**IMPORTANT SAFETY INFORMATION**  
Please retain this manual for reference. If the appliance is transferred to another owner or location, it’s important for the new user to be familiar with its operation and safety information. Safety precautions must be strictly adhered to while using this heavy appliance. Ensure it is connected according to specifications and never expose it to heat sources. The rear of the appliance should remain unobstructed and comply with the installation instructions. Allow adequate cooling time after use, and only authorized technicians should service the unit. This appliance is designed for indoor use and should not be exposed to prolonged sunlight or similar environments, as this may lead to overheating or damage. 

Refrain from using sharp instruments to remove frost; instead, allow the appliance to defrost naturally according to the appropriate defrosting instructions. Ensure that cleaning and servicing are done by authorized personnel only. In case of malfunction, please contact Service Force for repairs by qualified technicians.

**CONTENT SUMMARY**  
This manual includes information on activation, switching modes, operating sounds, cleaning, defrosting, troubleshooting, and technical positioning.

**OPERATION**  
To operate the appliance, turn it on using the ON/OFF switch. The thermostat display will indicate when the unit is operational. Adjust the thermostat to maintain the desired temperature, particularly for high-density foods. When activating functions, pressing the designated buttons will initiate settings accordingly. 

For frozen foods, allow ample time for thawing and avoid consuming items that have been improperly stored. Follow food safety guidelines to ensure all items are appropriately cooked or stored.

**MAINTENANCE AND CLEANING**  
Before cleaning, disconnect the power supply. Use mild cleaning agents suitable for plastics; avoid abrasive materials or strong detergents that could damage surfaces. Ensure thorough cleaning without using harmful chemicals. The unit does not require manual defrosting as it is frost-free.

**TROUBLESHOOTING**  
If the appliance is not functioning correctly, consult the troubleshooting section. Possible solutions include checking power connections, ensuring the thermostat settings are correct, and verifying that ventilations are not obstructed.

### Installation Guidelines

**Warning**: If you are disposing of an old appliance that has a locking door, ensure it is rendered unusable to prevent children from being trapped inside.

For proper installation, select a location that adheres to the specified ratings. Ideal operating temperatures are as follows:

- SN: +10°C to +32°C
- N: +16°C to +32°C
- ST: +18°C to +38°C
- T: +18°C to +43°C

Ensure the appliance is in a location that avoids direct sunlight, heat sources, or areas susceptible to extreme conditions. 

Ensure proper electrical connections are made; the appliance must be earthed. If the electrical wiring is to be connected, follow these colors: 

- Earth/E: Green/Yellow
- Neutral/N: Blue
- Live/L: Brown/Red

Use only wires and terminals that comply with the BSI standards.
