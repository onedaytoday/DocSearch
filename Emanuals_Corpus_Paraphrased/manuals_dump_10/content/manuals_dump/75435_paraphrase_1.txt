### Installation Instructions

**Important**: Installation and servicing of the freestanding electric range must be carried out by a qualified installer. Keep this document for reference by the local electrical inspector. 

#### Clearances and Location
Ensure proper clearances are maintained between the range and adjacent structures as specified in the instructions. There should be a minimum distance between the cooking surface and the bottom of unprotected wooden or metal cabinetry. If the cabinet is protected by flame-retardant material, a reduced clearance is permissible. Adhering to these specifications is essential to avoid property damage, fire hazards, or incorrect countertop and cabinet measurements. To minimize the risk of burns or fire from reaching over hot surfaces, avoid placing cabinet storage above the cooking area. If storage is necessary, consider installing a range hood that extends at least a certain distance beyond the cabinet bottom.

#### Important Safety Instructions
Failure to follow these guidelines may result in fire or electrical shock, causing property damage or injury. Ensure all electrical connections comply with local codes and ordinances, and retain these instructions for future reference.

#### Installation Steps
**Before You Begin**: Gather necessary tools including a wrench and screwdriver. You may also need a power supply cord based on your installation type.

1. **Anti-Tip Bracket Installation**: It is crucial to reduce the risk of the range tipping over. Install the provided anti-tip bracket according to the enclosed instructions.

2. **Electrical Connection Requirements**: Ensure that the supply cord is properly connected and grounded according to National Electric Code (ANSI/NFPA). Ensure wires are not pinched during installation.

3. **Power Cord Connections**: 
   - If using a supply cord, follow the instructions included for proper connections. 
   - The range has been shipped from the factory with pre-installed wiring for ease of connection.

4. **Permanent Wiring Connections**: If applicable, follow the provided steps to connect the range using a permanent wiring method, ensuring compliance with the latest National and State codes.

5. **Final Positioning of the Range**: Carefully slide the range into its designated spot, adhering to the specified clearances while fully engaging the anti-tip bracket.

By following these guidelines, you can ensure a safe and proper installation of your freestanding electric range.

### INSTRUCCIONES DE INSTALACIÓN PARA LA ESTUFA ELÉCTRICA

**Advertencia:** Utilizar un cable de alimentación de calibre incorrecto puede resultar en riesgos de incendio o choque eléctrico. Es crucial seguir todas las instrucciones de instalación y utilizar el soporte del sujetacables.

#### Conexión eléctrica de la estufa

1. **Notas sobre conexiones:**
   - Verifique los bornes traseros.
   - Importante: No afloje los bornes.
   - Conecte los siguientes cables:
     - **Alambre negro** (fase)
     - **Alambre blanco** (neutro)
     - **Alambre rojo** (segunda fase)

2. **Conexiones del cable de alimentación:**
   - Solo use bornes adecuados.
   - Asegúrese de no aflojar los bornes.
   - Mantenga asegurados los tornillos de los bornes.

3. **Instalación de la estufa:**
   - Deslice cuidadosamente la estufa a su ubicación final.
   - Asegúrese de que el soporte antivuelco quede bien enganchado.

4. **Conexión a tierra:**
   - Utilice el tornillo de conexión a tierra.
   - Asegúrese de que la cinta de conexión a tierra esté firmemente fijada al sistema de tierra.

5. **Revisión final:**
   - Apriete todos los terminales de los bornes.
   - Verifique que todas las conexiones estén seguras.

Estos pasos son esenciales para garantizar una instalación segura y eficiente de su estufa eléctrica.
