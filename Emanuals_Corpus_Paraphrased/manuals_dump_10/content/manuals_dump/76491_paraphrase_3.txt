Double-sided activity LER involves engaging participants with tasks related to money management. The activity, offered by Learning Inc. in the U.S. and the U.K., promotes an interactive approach to learning key concepts such as addition, subtraction, and currency recognition. Participants use cards to practice these skills in a dynamic setting.

The activity includes various challenges that focus on securely managing money and understanding different denominations. Players are expected to match items correctly based on numbers provided by the game, ensuring they navigate tasks safely and effectively.

Throughout the game, players will encounter opportunities to make choices, interact with the materials, and develop essential math skills. Successful completion of these tasks encourages math fluency and enhances understanding of financial concepts by incorporating hands-on learning experiences. Players will also need to read and interpret instructions carefully, enhancing their comprehension skills along the way.
