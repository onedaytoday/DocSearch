**Instruction Manual for ER C**

This instruction manual should be kept for future reference. It is important for users, whether the original owner or a subsequent one, to familiarize themselves with the operation and maintenance of the appliance. The information provided includes critical safety warnings. Improper handling could result in damage or injury.

**IMPORTANT SAFETY INFORMATION:**

- Do NOT use sharp tools for thawing or defrosting, as this could damage the appliance.
- Regularly check to ensure that food storage areas are not obstructed and that defrosting occurs safely.
- Switch off and unplug the appliance before cleaning or servicing. Avoid allowing children to play with it.
- Repairs should be conducted by authorized personnel only; do not attempt DIY repairs as this could pose safety risks.
- Ensure proper grounding of the appliance as per ASTA or BSI standards. 

**Electrical Connections:**

Be aware of the different wire colors and their purposes. When connecting to the mains supply, ensure wires are correctly fitted. The appliance has been designed in compliance with E.E.C. directives for your safety.

**Installation Guidelines:**

Position the appliance to allow adequate air circulation around it. Avoid placing it in direct sunlight or near heat sources. Ensure the space behind and above the unit is free from obstructions to facilitate proper cooling.

**Operating Conditions:**

Keep the appliance in an ambient temperature between +10°C to +38°C for optimal performance. Listen for normal operational sounds and any unusual noises which may indicate issues.

**Dimensions and Specifications:**

Refer to the technical specifications for height, gross capacity, net capacity, and energy consumption details.

**Health and Safety Recommendations:**

- Store food correctly, keeping raw meat separate from cooked items to avoid contamination.
- Allow sufficient time for food to cool before placing it in the appliance.
- Clean the appliance regularly, following safety protocols to prevent damage or malfunctions.

**Maintenance and Cleaning:**

Before performing maintenance, ensure the appliance is disconnected from the electricity supply. Avoid using abrasive cleaners or polishes that may damage the surface. Always refer to the instructions for cleaning specific components safely.

Please keep this manual accessible for reference to ensure the safe and effective operation of your appliance.
