**Features of the XBM30 Specialized Bread Mixer**

The XBM30 system is designed to flawlessly manage a range of products, particularly specialty breads. This model integrates both universal and planetary mixing capabilities, with a mechanical overload protection system ensuring optimal dough processing. It is compatible with various accessories, including a meat mincer and a puree attachment, making it versatile for different culinary applications.
