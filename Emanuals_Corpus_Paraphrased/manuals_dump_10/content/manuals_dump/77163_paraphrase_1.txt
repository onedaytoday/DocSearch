### Contents

1. **Unpacking the Touchcomputer**  
   - Includes items like the Touchcomputer, wall mounting bracket, and screws (M4, M3).

2. **Setup**  
   - Follow the illustrated setup instructions.  
   **Caution:** Handle the Touchcomputer carefully during unpacking and installation.

3. **Touchscreen Calibration**  
   - If recalibration is necessary, right-click the touchscreen, go to Properties, and follow the on-screen instructions.

4. **Additional Mounting Options**  
   - The Touchcomputer can be mounted using VESA mounts. Refer to the included documentation for options.

5. **Operation**  
   - LED functionality and I/O access, including details on brightness and CPU information. The LEDs indicate the operating state (inputs, hibernation, etc.).

6. **Safety and Maintenance**  
   - To prevent electrical shock, maintain proper cleaning protocols. Use a 3-wire grounding cord and avoid unauthorized openings to preserve warranty.
