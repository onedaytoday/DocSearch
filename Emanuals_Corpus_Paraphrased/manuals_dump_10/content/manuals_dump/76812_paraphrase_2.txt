**LK27 Specifications**

**General Design Features:** The LK27 is equipped with a chrome-plated top and includes a remote as an accessory. 

**Components:** It contains a rubber spring O-ring, lever arm, and packing unit. The design allows for compatibility and functionality without relying on offerings from other manufacturers. 

**Materials:** The components are manufactured using stainless steel, C.P. brass tailpiece, rubber stopper, and nylon gasket.
