The XEM-40 Planetary Mixer (model XEM40S) features a self-lubricating planetary mechanism designed for efficient operation. It integrates a mechanical system that ensures proper functionality when the unit is lowered. This device, provided by Dough and Professional, Inc. (www.electroluxusa.com/professional), operates with a NEMA 6-20P power connection. It includes controls for 'OFF' and 'ON' operations, as well as safety mechanisms that prevent incorrect usage when the mixer is in a lowered position. 

The XEM-40 is both ETL and CETL certified, and it comes with a warranty of several years, supporting various mixing applications including dough and professional mixing tasks. Additional options available include different mixing attachments and accessories tailored for specific culinary needs.
