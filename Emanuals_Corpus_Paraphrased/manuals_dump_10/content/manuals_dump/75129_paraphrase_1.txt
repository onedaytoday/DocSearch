### FEATURES

The XBE20 planetary mixer is designed for a variety of doughs and mixtures, including fresh bases and creams. It can be supplemented with optional accessories, including a touch control panel.

The specifications for the XBE20 model are as follows:

- **Capacity and Height:** 
  - Compatible with dough for Choux, Shortcrust, and Cold Meat Purees.
  - Features easy cleaning and is equipped with an installed electric motor.

**Models:**
