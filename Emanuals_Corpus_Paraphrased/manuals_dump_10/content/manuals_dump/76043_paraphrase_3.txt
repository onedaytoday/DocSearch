### EL2050 to EL2059 Instructions

**IMPORTANT SAFETY INSTRUCTIONS**  
Before using this vacuum cleaner, please read all instructions carefully.

**Warnings:**  
To minimize the risk of fire, electric shock, or personal injury:
- Avoid using the vacuum near flammable materials or heat sources.
- Only use the designated charger (SSA-135045US) for recharging.
- Do not mutilate or incinerate batteries, as they may explode at high temperatures.
- Keep the power cord free from damage, and avoid using it near heated surfaces.
- Maintain cleanliness around the suction openings and ensure they are clear of obstructions, debris, and hazardous items like matches or ash.

**California Proposition 65:**  
This product contains chemicals known to the State of California to cause cancer or reproductive harm. Wash your hands thoroughly after handling.

**Recycling Information:**

### COMPONENTS AND ACCESSORIES
- On/Off Switch
- High/Low Setting
- Release Button for Charging
- Brush/Crevice Tool Release Buttons
- Charging Indicator Light

### UNPACKING AND SETUP
**Caution:**  
Ensure the vacuum is turned off and cool before handling. Assemble with the provided locking screw using a screwdriver. 

Charge the unit for 4-5 hours before first use. Avoid placing the charger in direct sunlight or near a heat source. The charging light indicates when the battery is charged. 

Using the On/Off switch will allow you to choose between high and normal settings for optimal cleaning.

### MAINTENANCE
**Caution:**  
For service, contact Authorized Service Centers only. 

**Emptying and Cleaning:**  
- Regularly empty the dust container and clean components (recommended after every use).
- To release parts, press the appropriate buttons and reassemble after cleaning.

### TROUBLESHOOTING
If the unit overheats, turn it off and let it cool down. Clear any blockages in the hose or filter. Should the problem persist, seek assistance through www.electrolux.ca.
