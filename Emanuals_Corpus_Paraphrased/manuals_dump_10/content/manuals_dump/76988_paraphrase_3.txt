**ELMO ANNOTATION MODULE EVR-100 SETUP**

To set up the ELMO EVR-100, ensure that the device is oriented correctly. The dimensions of the EVR-100 are 12.4" (W) x 14.8" (D) x 2.4" (H). 

**OPTIONAL ACCESSORIES: Writing Light Unit SV-450**

The EVR-100 is compatible with the SV-450, as well as the ELMO EV-2000AF or HV-3000XG, and can also work with the EV-200 or HV-100XG models for a streamlined, user-friendly experience.
