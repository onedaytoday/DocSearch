# USER MANUAL ERN

## IMPORTANT SAFETY INFORMATION

This manual should be kept for reference. If the appliance is sold or transferred to another owner, it's essential that this information is provided, ensuring the new owner is informed about its operation. This information is critical for safe usage. The appliance is heavy and should be handled with care. Follow the specifications outlined in this manual, and contact Service Force for any servicing needs. The appliance should be protected from exposure to heat. 

When cleaning or servicing, always disconnect from power as directed. Children should be kept away from the appliance, especially during cooling and recharging, which should only be performed by authorized technicians. Avoid prolonged sunlight exposure as it may affect performance.

Ensure food is stored properly to prevent issues such as frost or freezer burn. Avoid using sharp instruments to remove ice or defrost. Follow defrosting and cleaning instructions carefully. Unauthorized repairs may lead to malfunction.

---

## OPERATION

Ensure all operational guidelines are reviewed. Adjust the thermostat as required for optimal cooling, especially during periods of high ambient temperature or heavy usage. The thermostat should be set correctly to maintain the appropriate cooling for fresh and frozen foods. Cooked and defrosted food should never be refrozen, and defrosting procedures must be followed to avoid spoilage.

---

## MAINTENANCE AND CLEANING

Before cleaning, disconnect from the electricity supply. Use mild detergents and avoid abrasive cleaners that may damage surfaces. Cleaning should be performed at least once a year to maintain efficiency. Prevent ice buildup by following the recommended defrosting procedures.

---

## TROUBLESHOOTING

If the appliance is not functioning correctly, consult the troubleshooting guide. Common issues include blown lights or incorrect thermostat settings. For persistent problems, contact Service Force for assistance and advice.

**Installation Warnings**

If discarding an old appliance with a door lock or catch, ensure it is rendered unusable to prevent accidental entrapment of children. 

**Positioning Instructions**

Install the appliance in a location that matches the specified temperature ranges:
- SN: +10°C to +32°C
- N: +16°C to +32°C
- ST: +18°C to +38°C
- T: +18°C to +43°C

Ensure the installation site is free from direct heat and is not located in an outhouse. 

**Important Safety Notes**

**WARNING:** This appliance must be earthed. Ensure proper connection and installation to prevent electrical hazards. The wires are color-coded for correct terminal connections:
- Earth (Green/Yellow)
- Neutral (Blue)
- Live (Brown or Red)

Use wires compliant with BSI362 or ASTA standards. Consult Service Force if necessary, in line with E.E.C. directives concerning electrical interference and safety.

**Built-In Installation Instructions**

For built-in applications, follow the provided guidelines for clearances and fittings. Unscrew relevant components as indicated in the manual and ensure proper alignment during installation. When fitting covers, ensure screws are securely fastened to maintain operational efficiency.
