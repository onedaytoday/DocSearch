### Instruction Manual Summary for AEG Customers

Dear Customer,

Congratulations on acquiring your new appliance. This manual contains crucial information about its features and functionalities. It is essential to familiarize yourself with these operating instructions, which include important safety warnings.

**Contents Overview:**
- **Cleaning & Maintenance**: Guidelines for thorough cleaning and care.
- **Technical Specifications**: Detailed information that may be of interest to both users and technicians.
- **Safety Warnings**: Important instructions regarding safe operation and handling.

**Installation Requirements**: 
- Ensure the appliance is connected to an earthed general-purpose outlet.
- For LPG models, adhere to Australian standards (AS4551, AS5601), and ensure compatibility with your gas supply.

**Operation Guidelines**: 
- Always ensure the appliance is turned off when not in use, and keep the area around it clear to avoid accidents.
- Children should be supervised to prevent burns or injuries.

**Cleaning Instructions**: 
- Use a damp cloth for routine cleaning and avoid abrasive materials that may damage surfaces.
- To maintain the finish, consider using a gentle polishing agent.

**Troubleshooting**:
- If you experience issues like uneven burning or ignition failure, check the cover placement and ensure the device is properly maintained.

**Recycling Information**: 
- Dispose of the appliance in accordance with local regulations to promote environmental sustainability.

For any service or repair needs, please consult the AEG authorized service team. Always refer to the manual for specific maintenance procedures and safety guidelines. 

Thank you for choosing AEG; we are committed to providing you with excellent service and high-quality products.
