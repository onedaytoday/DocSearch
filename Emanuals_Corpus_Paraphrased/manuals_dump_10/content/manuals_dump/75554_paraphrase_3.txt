**Instruction Manual for AEG Appliance**

Thank you for choosing AEG. For effective use of your new appliance, please refer to this instructional manual. If you need additional assistance, contact AEG Customer Service via telephone or write to: AEG Appliances, High Street, Slough, SL1 1DZ.

### Safety Information
To protect the product, follow the cleaning and service guidelines outlined here. Ensure the appliance is unpacked and all technical specifications are checked prior to installation. Please note that this manual includes critical information, and changes may occur; always verify before use.

### Usage Safety
- Exercise caution with children around the appliance, especially when it is in operation. 
- Always turn off the appliance when not in use. 
- Be mindful of flammable materials near hot surfaces to prevent fires.
- Use appropriate cleaning methods to avoid damage and ensure safety.

### Operating Instructions
- Before starting, ensure the appliance is properly connected to the power supply. 
- Use matches or a lighter to ignite, if necessary. Consult the cooking guide for optimal temperature settings and cooking recommendations. 
- Ensure the cooking items are placed correctly to avoid mishaps.

### Cleaning and Maintenance
- Regularly clean grease splashes and food residues to prevent fire hazards.
- Use a damp cloth or designated cleaner for maintenance.
- Avoid using abrasive materials that could damage surfaces.

### Installation Guidelines
- Ensure proper measurements for installation to avoid electrical shock. 
- Follow detailed mounting instructions, ensuring secure placement and connections.
- Be mindful of any structural modifications required for installation.

### Compliance and Technical Standards
This appliance meets the standards set forth by the Appliance Directive (90/396/EEC), the Low Voltage Directive (73/23/EEC), and the EMC Directive (89/336/EEC). For further specifications regarding model details and safety certifications, refer to the rating plate located on the appliance.

### Warranty Information
The product is covered under a warranty against defects in workmanship and materials for a specified duration post-purchase. The warranty is valid only if the appliance operates according to the manual's instructions and is serviced by authorized personnel.
