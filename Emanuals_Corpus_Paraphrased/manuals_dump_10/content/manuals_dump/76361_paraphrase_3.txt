   - Ensure both hoses are securely attached and clean.
   - If necessary, turn off the washer and reconnect the hoses, as well as clean any clogs.

2. **Operational Checks:**
   - Refer to the instructional manual on how to operate the washer properly.
   - An unbalanced load may cause it to stop tumbling. Use the touch controls to select options for load balancing.

3. **Understanding Washer Sounds:**
