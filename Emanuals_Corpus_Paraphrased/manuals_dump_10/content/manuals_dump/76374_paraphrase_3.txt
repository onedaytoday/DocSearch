The W5105S Professional Washer offers straightforward selection options and control buttons, enhancing user experience. It features high-performance elements, with varying capacities and operational factors, including additional supply options through an optional I/O board. The washer operates efficiently with both heated and non-heated water, accommodating temperatures from 15°C to 65°C, and is compliant with ISO and IP 24D certifications. The unit can run on multiple voltage configurations, including 220-240V and 380-415V. For detailed specifications and further information, visit www.electrolux.com/laundrysystems.
