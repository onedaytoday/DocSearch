### USER MANUAL - ERN

**IMPORTANT SAFETY INFORMATION**

It is essential to keep this manual for reference. If the appliance is transferred to another owner, the manual should be provided, ensuring they are aware of its operation and safety details. This information outlines critical safety musts to prevent damage or hazards resulting from improper use. 

When connecting the appliance, ensure it is done according to instructions, particularly regarding the electrical supply. Prevent exposure to heat sources. For optimal functionality, the appliance may require cooling and recharging by authorized technicians. Avoid placing the unit in direct sunlight or near boilers to prevent overheating.

**Defrosting and Cleaning**

Before cleaning or servicing, ensure the appliance is turned off and disconnected from the power supply. Avoid using sharp tools to remove frost, and allow the appliance to defrost naturally. Only authorized personnel should undertake repairs to prevent further damage or serious malfunction.

**OPERATION GUIDELINES**

Adjust the thermostat when necessary, especially in fluctuating weather conditions. When food is stored, ensure that excess frost does not build up in the freezer section, as this can lead to inefficient cooling. 

Cooked food that has thawed should not be refrozen. Follow packaging instructions for microwave thawing, and ensure meat and other foods are thoroughly cooked before consumption. 

**HINTS AND TIPS**

Ensure that food is properly stored, avoiding overcrowding to maintain adequate air circulation. Keep the fridge organized, and use airtight containers to preserve food freshness.

**MAINTENANCE AND CLEANING**

Disconnect the appliance from the electricity supply before cleaning. Employ a soft cloth with mild cleaners, avoiding abrasive products that can damage surfaces. If the light bulb requires replacement, ensure to turn off the appliance and follow the provided instructions for safe access to the light fixture.

### Installation Instructions

**WARNING:** If you are disposing of an old appliance with a door lock or catch, ensure that it is rendered unusable to prevent accidental entrapment of children.

**Positioning:** The appliance should be installed in a location that meets the following temperature requirements:
- **SN:** +10°C to +32°C
- **N:** +16°C to +32°C
- **ST:** +18°C to +38°C
- **T:** +18°C to +43°C

Install away from direct sunlight, heat sources, and damp areas. Ensure ventilation as outlined in the instructions.

**Electrical Warning:** This appliance must be properly earthed. The wiring must be connected per the following color codes:
- Earth (E): Green/Yellow
- Neutral (N): Blue
- Live (L): Brown or Red

Ensure connections meet BSI362 or ASTA standards, and any relevant EEC directives are adhered to for safety against electrical interference.

**Built-in Installation:** Follow specific instructions to secure the appliance into cabinetry:
- Unscrew relevant components for space allocation.
- Use provided screws to fit covers as illustrated in the manual.
- Ensure adequate clearance during operation according to specifications.

Check all connections and the mounting fit to ensure optimum performance of the appliance.
