The ELMO system includes the lightweight EWB-1610 wall mount, constructed from black ABS plastic. It is compatible with the ELB-110C and ELB-110S clear housings. Additionally, the accessories comprise the ELMO models PTC-200 and PTC-201C, featuring galvanized nipples for IP-rated PTZ applications. The overall setup is designed for use with the EWB-1610 wall mount.
