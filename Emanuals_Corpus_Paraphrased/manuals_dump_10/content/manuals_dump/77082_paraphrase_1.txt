**Bricks Wand - 1529L LCD Multifunctional Touch Monitor**

The 1529L Touch Monitor (OEM version) is available with optional features including a Magnetic Stripe Reader (MSR) and VESA wall mounting. This device utilizes AccuTouch and CarrollTouch technology and serves as an All-in-One touch computer. 

### Applications:
The 1529L incorporates a high-performance Active TFT LCD display (model MS500540) with dithering touchscreen capabilities. Connectivity options include RGB, DVI-D, VGA, and AC/DC power inputs (12V). It meets several safety and compliance standards, including FCC-A, TV-GS, CE, VCCI, and C-Tick.

### Features:
- On-Screen Display (OSD) adjustments: Horizontal and vertical positioning, RGB, YUV, and phase settings
- ENERGY STAR certified
- VESA DDC compliant
- Optional customer displays: a 12.1" VGA touchscreen and a 20" VGA display with VFD capabilities
- Optional fingerprint scanner
