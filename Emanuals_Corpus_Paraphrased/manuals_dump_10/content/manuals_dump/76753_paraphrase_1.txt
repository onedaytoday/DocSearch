### Specifications Overview

The countertop-mounted fountain is made from polished, lead-free material, designed to prevent splashing and featuring a flow height compliant with P.S.I. standards. This drinking fountain adheres to the lead-free criteria established in the Safe Drinking Water Act and the Lead Contamination Control Act. The LK142 model is fully lead-free and certified by ANSI/NSF61, Section 9. 

Constructed as a DRKR14C type fountain, it includes a cutout that accommodates countertop thickness and is compatible with IPS supply and waste fittings. 

**Installer Notes**: The drinking fountain is equipped with a bubbler and valve, along with all necessary fittings made from completely lead-free materials. It is advisable to use non-metallic shut-off valves and supply tubing provided by others.

**Installation Instructions**: 
To install the bubbler assembly, align the recess on the underside of the bubbler with the corresponding dimple on the fountain. Secure the assembly using the waste fitting and tiedown bracket.

**Installation Procedure**:
Create a cutout of the desired diameter in the countertop. Apply caulk (not provided) around the cutout and waste opening. Position the fountain over the opening and insert the strainer. Use screws and nuts to securely attach the unit to the countertop, then wipe away excess caulk.
