**Using the Appliance**  
**Indicator Lights**: These indicate whether the appliance is operating correctly. The lights will switch off once set temperatures are achieved. The Fast Freeze feature should be activated when placing fresh food inside. 

The thermostat controls the internal temperature, which should be set according to the ambient conditions and desired storage temperatures. Ensure food is kept properly stored to avoid freezer burn and maximize freshness.

**Normal Operating Sounds**  
Expect to hear typical appliance sounds, such as whirring and clicking, related to the cooling process. Ensure that air circulation is not obstructed for optimal performance.

**Maintenance and Cleaning**  
Always disconnect the appliance from the electrical supply before cleaning. Avoid using aggressive cleaning agents or bleach. Clean the interior surfaces carefully, and perform regular maintenance to prolong the appliance's lifespan.

**Troubleshooting**  
If you experience issues, refer to the troubleshooting section of this manual. Common symptoms may include excessive frost buildup or unusual operational sounds. For further assistance, contact Service Force with your purchase details.
