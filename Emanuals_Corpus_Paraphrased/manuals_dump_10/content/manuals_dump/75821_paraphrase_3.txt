### Important Safety and Cleaning Instructions

Before cleaning or performing maintenance tasks, switch off the appliance to prevent any accidents, and follow the defrosting guidelines provided. Only certified technicians should service this appliance, as unauthorized repairs can lead to further issues. Be vigilant about safety instructions provided in this manual.

### Appliance Components Overview

- **Thermostat**
- **Light**
- **Compartments and Shelving**
- **Defrosting System**

Ensure the area around the appliance is well-ventilated, avoiding exposure to high heat sources. Installation should be in accordance with the manufacturer’s guidelines to maintain optimal performance.

### Temperature Guidelines

- Fresh food should be stored between **0°C to 5°C (32°F to 41°F)**.
- For freezing, set the thermostat to **-18°C**. 

Use a thermometer to monitor temperatures accurately. Ensure food is stored in airtight containers to minimize frost/freeze burns. Items should be labeled with chronological storage dates to maintain freshness.

### Normal Sounds

Expect to hear operational noises, including whirring and clicking sounds, indicating normal functioning. Ensure that air can circulate around the appliance and that shelves are not blocked.

### Cleaning Instructions

Before cleaning, disconnect the appliance from the electricity supply. Use non-abrasive cleaning agents and avoid bleach-based products. Regularly check and clean the trough and vents to avoid clogs.

### Defrosting Guidelines

The appliance will automatically defrost when frost becomes too thick on the cooling plate. It is advisable to manually defrost occasionally to maintain performance.

### Troubleshooting

If any issues arise, reference the problem symptoms indicated in this manual. If the appliance exhibits unusual behavior (such as persistent light or high temperatures), contact Service Force for support. Their contact information can be found in your local directory.

For further assistance, keep this manual and any relevant product information readily available.
