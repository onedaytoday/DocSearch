**INSTALLATION Warning**: If you are disposing of an old appliance with a door lock or catch, it is crucial to render it unusable to prevent children from becoming trapped inside.

**Positioning**: The appliance should be placed in a location that adheres to its temperature rating, avoiding exposure to direct sunlight. The specified temperature ranges for placement are as follows:  
- SN: +10°C to +32°C  
- N: +16°C to +32°C  
- ST: +18°C to +38°C  
- T: +18°C to +43°C  

Ensure that the appliance is situated in a well-ventilated area, away from sources of heat or cold, and not in an environment below 10°C (50°F). If the ambient temperature is within these limits, you can proceed with installation. There should be at least a few centimeters of space around the back and top of the appliance to ensure proper ventilation, ideally with a gap of at least 5 cm above the unit. 

**Warning**: THIS APPLIANCE MUST BE EARTHED. If disconnecting the appliance, ensure power is turned off and the safety earth is installed correctly. The wiring should follow the color codes below:  
- Earth wire: green/yellow  
- Neutral wire: blue  
- Live wire: brown or red  

Use only the specified molded plug that complies with BSI362 ASTA or BSI standards. This appliance also complies with relevant E.E.C. directives concerning electromagnetic interference.

Before proceeding with installation, ensure the appliance remains functional and free from breakdown during shelf adjustments. For shelf operations, follow the arrows indicated, releasing the shelves carefully.
