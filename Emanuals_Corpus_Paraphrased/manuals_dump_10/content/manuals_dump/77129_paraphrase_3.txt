### Touchmonitor 1537L LCD Touch Monitor

**Model:** ET1537L LCD Touch Monitor  
**Part Number:** E250918

**Overview:**
The Touchmonitor ET1537L features an LCD interface designed for kiosk applications. It utilizes AccuTouch technology, compatible with various operating systems, including Windows XP, NT, and MS-DOS, and supports USB connections.

**Specifications:**
- **Display Type:** XGA TFT LCD
- **Resolution:** Supports VGA, SVGA, XGA formats
- **User Interface:** On-Screen Display (OSD) for brightness, contrast, and RGB adjustments
- **Connectivity Options:** USB and RS232 for external connections
- **Power Requirements:** AC 100-240V; compliant with UL, C-UL, FCC-B, CE, and other international standards

**Installation:**
For setup, connect the monitor via USB or RS232. Installation software is provided on a CD for different operating systems, including instructions for configurations.
