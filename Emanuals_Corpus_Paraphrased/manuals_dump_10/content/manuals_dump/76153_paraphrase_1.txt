**Instructions for the Electrolux Cooker Hood EFC**

Thank you for selecting the Electrolux Cooker Hood EFC. For optimal use, please follow these instructions closely. Should you require additional information or assistance regarding this product, please refer to Customer Service. 

**Ownership and Installation Information**
For details regarding the installation of your hood, including extraction and cleaning procedures for grease and charcoal filters, refer to the Appliance Information section. Ensure that your cooker hood meets the recommended clearance height from the wall. If the hood becomes overheated, turn off the power and allow it to cool down.

**Safety Precautions**
It is crucial to follow safety protocols, especially when connecting the hood to an existing power supply. Children should not operate the appliance unsupervised. The cooker hood contains components that may overheat and pose a fire risk.

**Operating Instructions**
The cooker hood features three switches located to the right front for grease extraction and lighting. Ensure the grease filters are cleaned approximately every month or more frequently if needed. These can be washed by hand or in a dishwasher. 

**Maintenance and Cleaning**
The charcoal filters should be replaced every three months or more frequently based on usage. To replace them, remove the grease filter and unscrew the charcoal filter from its housing. When cleaning the filters, avoid using abrasive materials.

**Troubleshooting**
If you encounter operational issues, first check that the cooker hood is properly connected to the electricity supply. If the unit is not functioning, consult Service Force for additional support.

**Warranty Information**
The Electrolux European Guarantee covers defects in workmanship for a specified period from the date of purchase. Please refer to your warranty details, which are applicable only when the appliance is used according to the operating instructions.
