### INSTALLATION Warning

**IMPORTANT:** When disposing of an old appliance with a door lock or catch, ensure that it is rendered unusable to prevent young children from becoming trapped inside.

**Positioning Instructions:**
The appliance must be installed in a location that meets the temperature ratings indicated. The following temperature ranges apply:
- SN: +10°C to +32°C
- N: +16°C to +32°C
- ST: +18°C to +38°C
- T: +18°C to +43°C

Place the appliance in an environment that is free from direct sunlight and away from cold drafts and outbuildings. It should not operate in temperatures below +10°C (50°F). Ensure proper ventilation by allowing space around the back and top of the appliance, with a minimum clearance at the top. Ideally, the appliance should be positioned on a level surface.

**Warning:** DO NOT block any vents or airflow by placing objects against the appliance.

**Electrical Supply:**
This appliance must be properly earthed. If you are installing a plug, follow these guidelines:
- The earth wire (green/yellow) should be connected to the terminal marked "E" or with the earth symbol.
- The neutral wire (blue) goes to the terminal marked "N."
- The live wire (brown) connects to the terminal marked "L."

Ensure that the plug complies with BSI362 or ASTA standards. Instructions for electrical connections are provided in the user manual.

**Compliance:** This appliance is built to comply with E.E.C. Directives concerning standard electrical safety.
