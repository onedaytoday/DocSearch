### Installation and Setup Instructions
**Touchmonitor Features:**  
- Front-Mount (1767L) and Rear-Mount (1747L) configurations. 
- Power connections and various video connectors.

**Connection Notes:**  
Ensure the touchmonitor and PC are powered off during setup. Follow these steps for connection:

1. **Connecting DVI-D and Power:**  
   Attach the DVI-D cable to the PC and touchmonitor. Secure connections by turning screws clockwise.

2. **Connecting the Touchscreen:**  
   Use either the RS-232 or USB cable. Connect the appropriate cable to both the monitor and the PC.

3. **Connecting the OSD Remote:**  
   Plug the remote connection into the designated OSD port on the bottom of the monitor.

4. **Power Connections:**  
   Ensure the power supply is correctly connected and routed to the monitor.

### Touchmonitor Operation
Understanding the On-Screen Display (OSD) is crucial for optimizing the performance of your touchmonitor. Adjust settings as necessary to meet your usage needs. If further adjustments are needed, please consult the section dedicated to OSD operations in this manual.

### Remote OSD Functions

- **AUTO/SEL Buttons:**
  - Display or exit the OSD menu.
  - Select the desired item.
  - Adjust brightness or settings clockwise for increase and counter-clockwise for decrease.

- **Functionality:**
  - Switches the touch monitor on/off.
  - To access the OSD, press and hold specific buttons for a few seconds.

- **Automatic Settings:**
  - The monitor automatically selects the VGA or LCD input.
  - Use the "USER" option to tailor colors (R/G/B).

- **Input Formats:**
  - Compatible with DSUB and DVI connections.
  - Adjust settings for clock phase and image quality (Brightness, Saturation, Hue, Flesh Tone).

- **OSD Timeout:**
  - The OSD will close automatically after a set time.

- **Troubleshooting:**
  - Check I/O connections if display issues arise. 
  - Ensure connections are secure for both the LCD and touch monitor.

- **Resolution Support:**
  - Native resolution is SXGA at 17 inches. Other resolutions supported include VGA, SVGA, XGA, and UXGA.

- **Maintenance and Safety:**
  - Turn off the monitor for cleaning; only use recommended cleaning methods.
  - Avoid unauthorized servicing to maintain warranty.
