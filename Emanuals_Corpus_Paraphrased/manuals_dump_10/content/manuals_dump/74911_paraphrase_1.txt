The T8E professional catering chamber features a user-friendly design that allows for easy unloading and cleaning. Accessible components ensure efficient maintenance. This chamber integrates seamlessly with the base, enhancing its cleanability. Key specifications include the model height, RPM, and electrical capacity, specifically rated at V, 1N. 

Notable optional accessories include an abrasive plate compatible with 5kg vegetable peelers, a drying basket for 8kg vegetable peelers, and filter baskets for both 8kg peelers and shelled produce. The T8E model is equipped to handle both cold and warm water requirements.
