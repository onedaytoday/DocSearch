**Service ESS20070 Specifications: General Overview**  
The ESS20070 service type features a coved corner design with a thick backsplash for a finished look. The unit is constructed to meet MIL-S-2143B specifications for shipboard applications. It includes rolled rim front ends and two-hole bracket plates, along with angle iron supporting brackets that are painted. Two loose brackets are provided, and the unit is welded together.  

**Rough-In Dimensions for Service ESS20070**  
Refer to the reverse side for the rough-in dimensions. The dimensions include slots for wall hangers and a floor line indication, along with hole diameters for installation. Printed in the U.S.A.
