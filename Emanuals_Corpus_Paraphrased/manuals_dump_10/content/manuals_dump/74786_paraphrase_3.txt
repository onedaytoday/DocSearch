- **Starting Up**: Ensure the appliance is plugged into a suitable power supply.

**WARNING: THIS APPLIANCE MUST BE EARTHED**

This unit must be connected to the electrical supply according to the specified rating indicated on the appliance. Ensure that the wiring is compatible with the following color coding: 

- The earth wire should be connected to terminals marked with an ‘E’ or green/yellow.
- The neutral wire should connect to the terminal marked ‘N’ or blue.
- The live wire should connect to the terminal marked ‘L’ or brown.

Take caution to avoid any electricity supply interruptions that could lead to severe consequences such as electric shocks.

**Installation Location**

The appliance must be installed in a well-ventilated area, shielded from direct sunlight or other heat sources. Clear any obstructions that may interfere with the appliance's operation. Follow the factory guidelines for positioning.

**Construction Instructions**

For proper assembly, ensure that the housing fits securely against the hinges and the included screws are used to fasten it. Covers marked (A), (B), (C), and (D) should be installed as illustrated, using the provided screws. Adjustments to maintain the recommended clearance are crucial for optimal performance.

If adjustments to the fixing screws (E) are required, refer back to the installation guide and retighten as necessary.
