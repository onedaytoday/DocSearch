### Important Safety Information

These instructions prioritize user safety during setup and operation. If you have questions about the appliance, contact Customer Service. Ensure the appliance is properly supplied with electricity before use. If there are concerns regarding electrical supply or potential heat exposure, please consult Service Force. 

### Appliance Overview

This section includes details on components such as the thermostat, light, compartment shelves, cleaning methods, and common operational sounds:

- **Temperature Control**: The appliance should be set between -18°C (0°F) for proper food preservation. 
- **Cleaning**: Before cleaning, switch off the unit and disconnect from power. Use mild cleaning agents; avoid bleach.
- **Dealing with Frost**: Regularly check for frost build-up. Defrosting may be required if frost exceeds a certain thickness.

### Using Your Appliance

- **Temperature Settings**: Keep fresh food between 0°C to 5°C (32°F to 41°F) for optimal preservation.
- **Food Storage**: Organize food items systematically. Ensure all food is airtight to prevent freezer burn.
- **Normal Sounds**: Expect typical operational noises such as whirring from the motor or clicking from the thermostat.

### Maintenance and Cleaning

- **Defrosting**: The appliance usually defrosts automatically. If needed, manually defrost when frost is excessive.
- **General Cleaning**: Disconnect the appliance before cleaning. Avoid aggressive cleaning products to maintain the surfaces and avoid any damage.
  
### Troubleshooting

If issues arise:

1. Check if the light is functioning properly.
2. Assess thermostat settings; ensure they are within the correct range.
3. If problems persist, consult Service Force for assistance.
