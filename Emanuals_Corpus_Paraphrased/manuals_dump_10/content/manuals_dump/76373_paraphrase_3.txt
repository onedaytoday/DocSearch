The W5105N washer offers a user-friendly selection of performance options, including high force with robust protection features. It supports various electricity heating configurations (non-heated) and is compatible with normal water temperatures from 15°C to 65°C, certified under ISO and IP 24D standards. Available power supplies include 220-240V, 380-415V 3N, 440V, and 480V, accommodating both steam and non-heated water. The unit also meets IEC sound standards, features a cold-water reuse option, and provides additional resources at www.electrolux.com/laundrysystems. Specifications are subject to change.
