### Installation Warning

**Important:** If you're disposing of an old appliance equipped with a locking door or latch, it's vital to render it inoperable to prevent young children from getting trapped inside.

### Positioning Instructions

Locate the appliance in a proper environment, avoiding direct sunlight, extreme cold, or warm areas. The operating temperature range for different models is as follows:

- **SN**: +10°C to +32°C
- **ST**: +18°C to +38°C
- **T**: +18°C to +43°C

Ensure the ambient temperature remains above 10°C (50°F). If the conditions are outside these ranges, the appliance may not function effectively. 

Provide adequate space around the unit, especially at the top and back, to allow for airflow. Ideally, the appliance should be positioned under a countertop.

**Caution:** Ensure the back of the appliance does not have obstructed airflow. Avoid screwing the unit too tightly against the wall or floor to prevent restrictions.

### Electrical Connection

**Warning:** This appliance must be earthed. Make sure the unit is disconnected from the power supply before installation. 

The wiring must be connected as follows:
- **Earth (E)**: Green/Yellow wire
- **Neutral (N)**: Blue wire
- **Live (L)**: Brown/Red wire

Use the correctly molded plug that complies with BSI362 ASTA standards. The colored wires should be matched with the respective terminals as indicated.
