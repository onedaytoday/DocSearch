**Touchmonitor Information**  
This document pertains to the installation and operation of the touchmonitor, specifically addressing safety precautions to minimize the risk of electric shock. Always ensure the touchmonitor is turned off during cleaning, and use a 3-wire grounding cord connected to a properly grounded outlet. Avoid voiding your warranty by refraining from unauthorized service. The warranty information can be found on the touchmonitor itself, either on the label or the back.

In the event the touchmonitor becomes damaged, exercise caution. 

**Touchmonitor 1915L Safety Guidelines**  
To prevent electric shock, always use the appropriate power supply and avoid using solvents such as cleaners or thinners. Clean the touchmonitor with a soft cloth, and for the touchscreen, use a designated cleaner on the cloth—never apply it directly to the screen. 

**Important Warnings**  
The touchmonitor contains mercury, which is a component of the backlight. It must be disposed of responsibly, in accordance with local waste regulations, such as the WEEE Directive in the European Union.
