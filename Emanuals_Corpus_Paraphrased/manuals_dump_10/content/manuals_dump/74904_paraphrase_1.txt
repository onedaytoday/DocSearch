**Food Gear Overview**

Food Gear provides a range of products suitable for use in supermarkets and food service environments, offering options for raw and cooked meats. Key features include high durability and effective cleaning, with sharpened edges for efficiency and heavy-duty operations. The gear is designed for easy handling and maintenance, featuring a non-stick coating for seamless operation. The cleaning plate facilitates simple maintenance when the unit is powered off and offers permanent protection during both operation and cleaning. Additionally, it boasts IP42 protection standards and is certified by CE and NSF.
