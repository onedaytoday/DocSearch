**Stereo Turntable with Speaker System Instruction Manual**

**Warning:** To minimize the risk of electric shock, do not expose this device to rain or moisture.

**Safety Precautions:** 
1. Do not remove the front or back cover as it may expose you to hazardous electrical components. 
2. This unit contains no user-serviceable parts. For maintenance or repairs, consult qualified technicians only.
3. Avoid the risk of electric shock by not opening the unit.

**Guidelines:**
- Always follow the provided instructions and retain this manual for future reference. 
- Be cautious of water and moisture exposure. Ensure proper ventilation, especially in enclosed spaces like bookcases. 
- Keep the unit away from direct heat sources or flames.
- Ensure a stable power supply and proper grounding to avoid potential hazards.
- Exercise caution regarding power cords; do not allow them to become pinched or damaged.
