**General Specifications for Classroom/Institutional Sink Packages**

The fountain-type sink packages DRKRC2220C, DRKRC2517C, and DRKRC3119C are designed with scratch and crack-resistant features. They include the LKC2432 model, which features a two-handle design, as well as the LK1141A model that complies with ANSI and ADA standards for lead-containing materials. These products meet ANSI A112.19.3M specifications and are certified by the International Association of Plumbing and Mechanical Officials (IAPMO) in accordance with the Uniform Plumbing Code (UPC), while also adhering to ANSI/NSF61 and UL standards.

**Sink Dimensions (Inches):**
- The sink models DRKRC2220C, DRKRC2517C, and DRKRC3119C are detailed with exact dimensions, measuring from right to front and back. These designs are functional and comparable to offerings from other manufacturers.

All dimensions are in inches. To convert to millimeters, multiply by 25.4. Please refer to the specific model numbers DRKRC2220C, DRKRC2517C, and DRKRC3119C for more information on specifications.
