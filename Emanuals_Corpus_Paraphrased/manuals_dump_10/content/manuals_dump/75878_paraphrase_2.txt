#### Important Safety Information

**Location and Installation**  
Ensure the installation area is well-ventilated and not exposed to direct sunlight or heat sources. The applianceâ€™s location must comply with specified ratings, as indicated on the rating plate. 

**Electrical Safety**  
This appliance must be properly earthed. Ensure the power supply meets the specifications outlined in the instructions. The color-coded wires must be connected appropriately:  
- **Earth (E)**: Green/Yellow  
- **Neutral (N)**: Blue  
- **Live (L)**: Brown/Red  

Take precautions against electrical shock during installation or maintenance.

#### Operating Instructions

1. **Initial Setup**   
   - Turn on the appliance using the ON/OFF switch.
   - Settings can be adjusted for temperature; "1" indicates the highest setting.

2. **Food Storage and Defrosting**  
   - Store food appropriately to ensure effective cooling. 
   - Clean the appliance regularly, following the guidelines in the "Cleaning" section. 

3. **Shelves and Storage Management**  
   - Shelves are adjustable for optimal space management. 
   - Ensure proper airflow around items to maintain cooling efficiency.

4. **Normal Sounds**  
   Sounds may be heard during operation due to the cooling process and thermostat adjustments. These are generally normal but should be monitored.

5. **Cleaning Instructions**  
   - Disconnect the appliance from the electricity supply before cleaning.
   - Use appropriate cleaning products, avoiding those with bleach or harsh chemicals. Ensure water drainage outlets are clear.

6. **Troubleshooting**  
   For persistent issues, check the thermostat settings, door closures, and ensure proper temperature settings. If problems continue, contact Service Force.
