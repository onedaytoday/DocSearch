**Specifications for ELU1113 and ELUH1113**

**General Description:**  
The ELU1113 and ELUH1113 are designed as heavy-duty sinks with a protective finish. The ELUH1113 features a highlighted top that is both durable and visually appealing.

**Installation:**  
These sinks are easy to install under the countertop, with recommended dimensions provided to ensure proper fit. A template is included with each ELU/ELUH unit to facilitate accurate installation.

**Sink Dimensions (Inches):**  
Refer to the template provided for the specific measurements necessary for installation.

**Mounting Bracket:**  
Note that fasteners for the mounting bracket are not included. The ELU and ELUH can be installed using typical mounting fasteners appropriate for the countertop type.

**State Compliance:**  
The specifications comply with ANSI A112.19.3M and the standards set forth by the International Association of Plumbing and Mechanical Officials according to the Uniform Plumbing Code.

**Additional Notes:**  
Silicone caulk will be necessary for installation but is not supplied. The design of the ELU1113 and ELUH1113 incorporates a contour edge that can be customized per customer preference.

Model number: 1-117C
