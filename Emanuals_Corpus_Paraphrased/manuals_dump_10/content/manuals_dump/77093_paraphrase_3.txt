The 1739L LCD touchmonitor is designed for optimal performance with an active TFT display capable of SXGA resolution, ensuring compatibility with VGA, SVGA, XGA, SXGA (non-interlaced), and graphics cards per VESA DPMS standards. It also supports DDC1/2B functionality.

**Unboxing the Touchmonitor**  
This section provides information for unpacking the LCD touchmonitor and includes details about the components, such as the LCD unit and USB Touch supply.

**Touch Interface Setup**  
Ensure the touchmonitor and PC are powered off before connecting. The touchscreen can be connected via RS232 or USB ports located at the rear. Securely tighten the screws to establish grounding. Follow the software installation instructions provided on the CD.

For operating systems such as Windows XP and NT, additional driver installation steps are available at www.elotouch.com. Follow Plug-and-Play instructions for seamless integration.

**About the Touchmonitor**  
The LCD touchmonitor is engineered with a high-performance microprocessor to achieve precise touch functionality. Refer to section A for more detailed performance criteria.

**Operating Instructions**  
Utilize the OSD for controlling display settings, including brightness and sharpness. Specific functionality is available for adjusting the clock, phase, and position parameters.

**Common Features and Power Management**  
Reduce energy consumption through power-saving features, including automatic switch-off and standby modes, which comply with energy efficiency standards (<40W operational).

**Troubleshooting Guide**  
For issues related to the touchmonitor, check the connection type (VGA, SVGA, XGA, SXGA, UXGA) and ensure compatibility, especially for video resolution settings. For any maintenance needs, use grounded cords and follow standard cleaning procedures.

**Maintenance and Safety**  
Avoid potential electric shocks by ensuring proper handling and maintenance. Use a soft cloth for cleaning the touchscreen and avoid using cleaners that may scratch the surface.

**Final Notes**  
Ensure proper electrical safety by utilizing the grounded plug. Keep the touchmonitor free from shocks and follow operational best practices to extend its lifespan. For further assistance, consult the technical support section (Chapter C).

### Touchmonitor Specifications

**Model:** 1739L LCD TFT Active  
**Resolution:** 338(H) x 270(V)  
**Pixel Pitch:** 0.264(H) x 0.264(V)  
**Compatible Resolutions:** VGA, SVGA, XGA, SXGA  
**Aspect Ratio:** Typical  
**Brightness:** With typical Tf settings: 16.7M colors  
**Interface:** R.G.B. TTL or Negative  
**Connectivity:** Rear Select; includes On-Screen Display (OSD) for adjusting brightness, phase, clock, and language options (English, Spanish)  
**Touch Technology:** Optional capacitive touch  
**Power Supply:** AC 100-240V  
**Operating Conditions:** 0 °C to 50 °C (32 °F to 122 °F)  
**Certifications:** UL, C-UL, FCC-B, CE, VCCI, C-Tick, MPRII
