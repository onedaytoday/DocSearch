The Ice KG system is designed for efficient catering, operating reliably throughout various hours. It features an easy installation process with straightforward connections for water and hoses. The interior is made of hygienic AISI Food-grade ABS, ensuring simplicity in cleaning. The device is user-friendly and easy to maintain, with accessible components for cooling. It is environmentally rated for 21°C with a supply temperature of 15°C. 

Technical specifications include the LEGEND CWI Cold Water model with electric supply compatible with V, single and three-phase configurations (V, 1N). Models RIMC024SA and RIMC024SW are included in this series, providing robust cooling capacity without compromising on performance.
