**XEM-20 Planetary Mixer Specifications**

The XEM-20 Counter Planetary Mixer (Model XEM20ST) is designed with various features suitable for professional kitchens. It includes water-resistant mechanical components, ensuring stable operation even when the mixer is in a lowered position. 

Key features include:

- ON/OFF buttons with a compact design
- Safety locks that prevent incorrect lowering of the bowl 
- High-performance self-lubricating mechanics
- ETL and CETL certification 

Accessories available for the mixer include various beaters, a dough whisk, and a reinforced tray/shelf.

Please refer to Electrolux Professional for more detailed information: www.electroluxusa.com/professional
