**Installation and Setup**

Proper setup of the touchmonitor is critical. Position it at least 18 inches away from other devices and heat sources, and avoid direct light reflection. 

For connecting the touchmonitor to a PC, ensure both devices are turned off before making any connections. Attach the appropriate cables—either RS-232 or USB—and also connect the AC power cable.

---

**Software Installation**

Proceed to install the touchscreen software using the TouchTools CD-ROM. Follow the on-screen instructions to complete installation across supported operating systems, including Windows NT.

**Operation of the Touchmonitor**

To adjust settings, utilize the On-Screen Display (OSD) buttons below the screen. Access the OSD menu and select the desired settings. The menu will disappear after a preset timeout.

Adjust brightness using the OSD options for optimal visibility. For troubleshooting, refer to the relevant sections if issues such as incorrect refresh rates or connectivity problems arise.
