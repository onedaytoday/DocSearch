**GENERAL SPECIFICATIONS**  
This specification pertains to fountain-type sink packages designed for classroom and institutional use, specifically models DRKRC2220C, DRKRC2517C, and DRKRC3119C. These sinks are resistant to scratches and cracks and include lead-free components compliant with ANSI and ADA standards. The sink includes the LKC2432 two-handle faucet and the LK1141A lead-free aerator. All products meet ANSI A112.19.3M standards and are certified by the International Association of Plumbing and Mechanical Officials, conforming to the Uniform Plumbing Code and ANSI/NSF61, Section UL.
