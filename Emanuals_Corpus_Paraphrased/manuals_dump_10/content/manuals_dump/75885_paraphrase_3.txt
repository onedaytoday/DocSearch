**Safety Information**  
It is important to retain this instruction manual for future reference. If the appliance is transferred to another owner or location, ensure this manual accompanies the unit. Safety features include a childproof latch to prevent access by children, as any misuse could pose a risk. Ensure the appliance is secure and well maintained without any mechanical issues. Only qualified technicians should perform repairs to ensure efficiency and safety.

**Temperature Ratings**  
Optimal food preservation is achieved between +10°C and +43°C for various settings. Strict adherence to these temperature guidelines is essential to prevent food spoilage.

**Cleaning and Maintenance**  
Regular cleaning of the interior and exterior is essential. Use mild cleaning agents to maintain efficiency and hygiene. Remove frost buildup using appropriate methods without damaging the unit. Ensure proper airflow by keeping vents clear. 

**Defrosting**  
Defrosting should follow the instructions provided to avoid damage. Remove food items before initiating the process for optimal results.

**Customer Service**  
For any operational issues, contact authorized service centers. Ensure you have the appliance's model number and warranty details ready for quick assistance.

**Installation Instructions**  
Unpack the appliance and position it away from direct sunlight and heat sources. Ensure there is adequate space for airflow. Follow the guidelines for safe and secure electrical connections, adhering to local regulations.

**Warranty Information**  
This appliance includes a warranty valid in specific countries. Refer to the warranty card for coverage details and conditions specific to your region.
