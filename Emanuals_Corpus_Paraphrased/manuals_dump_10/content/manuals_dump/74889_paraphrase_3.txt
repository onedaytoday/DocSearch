**Range Overview**

Zanussi Professional offers advanced ice-making solutions that prioritize performance and efficiency. These models are available in both air-cooled and water-cooled configurations, featuring durable construction with sophisticated design elements. 

**Technical Specifications and Features**

Key characteristics include capacity, height, and type of electrical supply (V, 1 phase). Each model is complemented with essential accessories such as a 200 kg stainless steel collection bin. The ice-making process is optimized for flow and formation, meeting international standards with productivity ratings at 15°C and 21°C.
