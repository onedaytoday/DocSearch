### Product Overview  
The 1739L LCD Touchmonitor features a rear-mount design with SXGA resolution, utilizing active thin-film transistor (TFT) technology for optimal performance. It supports VGA, SVGA, XGA, and SXGA (non-interlaced) formats and conforms to VESA DPMS standards. 

**Key Features:**
- Microprocessor-based functionality for enhanced precision.
- On-Screen Display (OSD) for settings management.
  
### Installation and Setup  
To connect the touchmonitor to a PC, ensure the device is powered off prior to installation. Connect the power supply (12V, 3A) and plug the touchscreen using either RS232 or USB interfaces. Secure all connections by tightening screws.

For software installation, insert the provided QuickStart CD and follow on-screen prompts. The touchmonitor is compatible with various Microsoft operating systems, including XP and NT.

### Operating the Touchmonitor  
The OSD menu can be accessed using the remote control buttons or on-screen options. Key functions include adjusting brightness, selecting inputs, and configuring display settings.

### Troubleshooting Tips  
If any display issues arise, verify that the input source matches the LCD’s native resolution (SXGA). Ensure connections are secure and refer to the troubleshooting section in the product manual for further assistance.

### B-26 Touchmonitor Information

This section details the touchmonitor's operation and installation procedures. To minimize the risk of electric shock, ensure the touchmonitor is powered off during cleaning. Avoid placing any objects on the front or top of the device. If the touchmonitor comes into contact with liquids, it may pose a hazard.

#### Safety Features of the Touchmonitor

The touchmonitor is designed to function safely. To reduce the risk of electrical shock, do not attempt to service the unit yourself; only qualified personnel should perform maintenance. Ensure the unit is unplugged before cleaning. Use a soft cloth and avoid using abrasive cleaners or solvents that could scratch the touchscreen. Never apply cleaning solutions directly onto the screen. 

**Warning:** This product contains mercury, which is part of the backlight. According to EU regulations, proper disposal and recycling must be conducted in compliance with local waste management guidelines.
