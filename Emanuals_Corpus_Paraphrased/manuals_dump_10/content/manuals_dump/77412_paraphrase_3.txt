**Powering On/Off the Device**

- **Power Button**: Press and hold the [POWER] button until the red and blue LED lights flash alternately.
- **Charging the Device**: Connect the Bluetooth headset to a USB power source using the provided USB cable. The red LED will indicate the charging state. It may take approximately a few hours for a full charge, during which the LED will remain red. Once charging is complete, the red light will turn off.

**Pairing with a Phone**

1. Ensure Bluetooth is enabled on your phone.
2. With Bluetooth turned on, press and hold the [POWER] button until the lights flash alternately.
3. On your phone, go to Bluetooth settings and select "EM12" from the list of available devices.
4. If prompted, confirm the pairing by selecting "YES".

**Usage Instructions**

- To increase or decrease volume, use the [VOLUME UP] or [VOLUME DOWN] buttons as necessary.
