### Important Safety Instructions
To ensure safe operation and user protection, adhere strictly to all provided instructions. Heavy items should be handled carefully. Avoid exposing the appliance to extreme temperatures or moisture. Always allow the appliance to cool adequately before servicing. Keep the back of the appliance clear for ventilation as specified. Use authorized personnel for servicing needs, particularly concerning the cooling system. Be mindful of potential hazards, particularly to children, to prevent trapping and suffocation. Regularly monitor food items to avoid frost/freezer burn.

### Operation Instructions
The warning lights indicate important conditions. The red warning light signals that the temperature in the appliance exceeds -12°C. Adjust the thermostat as needed for optimal performance, especially when loading new food. Follow guidance on how often to operate the fresh food and freezing functions to ensure quality retention. Distribute food properly to maintain adequate airflow. 

Once food is completely frozen, you may proceed to the next steps involving food management.

---

### Health and Safety Guidelines
To maintain food safety, cool cooked items before storing and avoid covering them tightly, allowing proper air circulation. Thaw food gradually to preserve quality. Ensure that food storage containers are airtight to prevent spoilage. 

---

### Normal Operating Sounds
Appliances make typical operating noises. You may hear a whirring sound from the thermostat. Clicks may occur when the thermostat regulates temperature. These are normal and indicate proper functioning.

---

### Defrosting
Regularly defrost the appliance to prevent excessive frost buildup. When the layer of frost is significant, turn off the appliance and allow it to thaw. 

Refer to specific guidelines for various food types to ensure quality preservation during the process.

---

### Maintenance and Cleaning
Always disconnect the appliance from the electrical supply before cleaning. Use gentle cleaning agents, avoiding harsh chemicals that could damage surfaces. Regular maintenance checks will prolong appliance lifespan.

---

### Troubleshooting
If issues arise, such as frost accumulation or operational failures, consult the troubleshooting guide or contact Service Force for assistance. Ensure that household electrical conditions are suitable and the thermostat is correctly adjusted.

---

### Service and Spare Parts
For service inquiries or purchasing spare parts, contact Service Force via phone. More information is available on the Service Force website. For customer support matters, please reach out to our Customer Care Department directly.

**INSTALLATION WARNING**: If disposing of an old appliance, ensure it can no longer be used to prevent potential entrapment of children. Select a suitable location for installation as per the provided guidelines. The specified temperature ranges for operation are:
- SN: +10°C to +32°C
- ST: +18°C to +38°C

Ensure the appliance is sheltered from direct sunlight, heat sources, and is installed correctly according to the provided guidelines.

**ELECTRICAL CONNECTION WARNING**: This appliance must be properly earthed. If any issues arise, the power should be turned off immediately. Use color-coded wires as follows:
- Earth: Green/Yellow
- Neutral: Blue
- Live: Brown (or Red)

Ensure that the included fuse meets the relevant safety standards (BS1362 ASTA/BSI).

This product complies with the E.E.C. Directives regarding electromagnetic interference.
