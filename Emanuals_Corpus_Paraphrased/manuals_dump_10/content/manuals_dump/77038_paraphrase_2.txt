**Overview of the 1515L LCD Touchmonitor**

The 1515L LCD Touchmonitor is designed with advanced thin-film-transistor (TFT) technology to deliver high-quality XGA graphics and support various operating systems including Windows XP, ME, and NT, as well as MS-DOS. Key features include compatibility with VGA, SVGA, and XGA (non-interlaced) graphics cards, and it conforms to VESA DPMS standards while supporting DDC 2B functionality. Optional wall-mounting capabilities are also available.

**Installation and Setup**

Ensure the touchmonitor and PC are powered off before connecting the USB touch interface. The setup includes connecting the USB cord and optionally connecting an MSR (Magnetic Stripe Reader) directly to the PC. When using the MSR, utilize the MSR CHANGE MODE.EXE utility to switch its functionality between standard and HID (Human Interface Device) modes. It is advised to secure the touchmonitor to prevent theft, and information concerning operating systems and additional software can be found online.
