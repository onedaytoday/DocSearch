**DTT TV Datasheet**

The DTT TV (DVB-T) allows users to enjoy digital television effortlessly via USB, featuring a range of software options. It supports fast TV viewing and recording, enabling users to record hours of programming and create playlists accessible on iPhone or Apple TV. 

The package includes TV software on a CD-ROM (DTT TV DVB-T model MCX), along with a suction mount. The device is compatible with systems that have built-in USB capabilities, such as G4, G5, or Core processors with at least 1 MB of physical RAM. It accommodates DVB-T COFDM signals and is equipped for VHF and UHF frequencies, supporting QAM and QPSK modulation. The connections include a USB input (IEC) and IR functionality.
