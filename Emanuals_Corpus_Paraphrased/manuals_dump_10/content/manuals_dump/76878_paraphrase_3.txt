### GENERAL SPECIFICATIONS

Type: Self-rimming. 

### DESIGN FEATURES
Models: PSR3319, PSR3321, and PSR4322. 

- **Coved Corners and Recess:** Features exposed polished areas with a bright finish for enhanced aesthetics and sound insulation. 
- **Sound Protection:** Models equipped with MR2 or as otherwise specified. 

**Compliance:** These designs meet ANSI A112.19.3M standards and are recognized by the International Association of Plumbing and Mechanical Officials (IAPMO), conforming to the Uniform Plumbing Code (UPC) guidelines.

Dimensions for each countertop ship are provided. 

**Models:** 
- PSR3319 MR2 
- PSR3321 MR2 
- PSR4322 MR2
