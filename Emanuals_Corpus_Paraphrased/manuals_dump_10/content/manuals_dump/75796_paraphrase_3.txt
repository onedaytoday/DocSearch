### IMPORTANT SAFETY INSTRUCTIONS
These guidelines are crucial for the safe use of the appliance. If you have any concerns regarding installation, please consult the customer service team. Ensure that the appliance is installed as per the specified requirements. It should not be exposed to heat sources or placed in a location that violates these guidelines. The back of the appliance must have adequate space for airflow.

Before starting, let the appliance sit powered on for a few hours for optimal cooling. Be cautious of potential hazards, such as ignition risks if the appliance becomes overly hot. This device should be kept away from children to prevent suffocation hazards. Only authorized technicians should handle cooling and servicing to maintain safety.

Avoid prolonged exposure to direct sunlight and keep the appliance away from boilers or heat sources. This appliance is designed for food storage only, so do not use it for other items. Always operate in accordance with the manufacturer’s instructions to prevent damage.

### DESCRIPTION OF THE APPLIANCE
The appliance includes fresh shelves and a thermostat. 

#### OPERATION
To operate, turn the thermostat to an appropriate position. Factors such as food placement and surrounding temperature will affect performance. Food should not be stacked too high to ensure proper airflow. Always keep meat covered or wrapped securely to prevent contamination.

---

### NORMAL SOUNDS
You might hear various sounds during operation, such as whirring or clicking, indicating normal cooling activity and thermostat adjustments.

---

### HEALTH AND SAFETY GUIDELINES
To ensure food safety, regularly check the storage temperature and avoid overloading the unit. Cooked food should be cooled rapidly, and meat should not be left uncovered. Always keep surfaces clean to prevent cross-contamination.

---

### DEFROSTING
Defrost the appliance when frost buildup becomes excessive, as this can impede performance. To defrost, turn the thermostat to OFF and remove food items. Allow frost to melt, and clean any residual moisture before restoring power.

---

### MAINTENANCE AND CLEANING
Before cleaning, disconnect the appliance from the electricity supply. Clean the interior and exterior using mild cleaning solutions; avoid abrasive cleaners. Ensure vents and drainage are clear for optimal performance. If the light bulb needs replacing, disconnect power first.

---

### TROUBLESHOOTING
If you experience issues with the appliance, check the following: ensure the light functions, verify the thermostat settings, and confirm that there is no frost buildup. If problems persist, contact customer service.
