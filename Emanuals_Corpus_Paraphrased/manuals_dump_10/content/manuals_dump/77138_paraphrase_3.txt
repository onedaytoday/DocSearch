**Introduction to the Product**

**Product Precautions:**  
The performance of the Touchmonitor can be affected by improper use. Ensure compatibility with the LCD specifications, which utilize active thin-film transistor (TFT) technology, supporting SXGA graphics. Follow guidelines provided in this document for optimal touchscreen operation.

**Features of the 1915L LCD Touchmonitor:**  
The 19-inch TFT LCD features a microprocessor that enables high-precision functionality. It supports various graphics modes, including VGA, SVGA, XGA, and non-interlaced SXGA. The product complies with VESA DPMS and supports DDC1/2B compatibility, with an integrated advanced OSD wall-mounting capability.

### Touchmonitor Information

This document provides important information regarding the touchmonitor, specifically in the sections on Installation and Operation. To minimize the risk of electric shock, always ensure that the touchmonitor is turned off before cleaning. Use a 3-wire grounding cord and connect it to a properly grounded outlet to ensure safety. Unauthorized modifications may void the warranty. The information regarding the model, including the serial number, can be found on the back or bottom of the unit. If the touchmonitor starts to malfunction, avoid any contact with it.

### Touchmonitor Specifications (Model 1915L)

- **Display Type**: LCD TFT Active
- **Resolution**: 376.32 mm (H) x 301.06 mm (V)
- **Pixel Pitch**: 0.294 mm (H) x 0.294 mm (V)
- **Supported Resolutions**: VGA, SVGA, XGA, SXGA
- **Brightness**: Typical brightness values, 16.7M colors, CR > 10
- **Connections**: R.G.B. TTL or Negative Connector, OSD settings for Contrast, Brightness, and Sharpness.
- **Power Supply**: AC 100-240V
- **Operating Temperature**: 0 °C to 50 °C (32 °F to 122 °F)
- **Humidity**: 10% to 90% non-condensing
