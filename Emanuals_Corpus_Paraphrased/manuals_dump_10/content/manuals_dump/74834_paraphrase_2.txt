The S90 C-Board (model WFGUOFOOOO) is designed as a high BTU unit for versatile installation, whether back-to-back or with the unit’s top positioned for easy cleaning. This robust unit features a heavy-duty construction and incorporates a high-efficiency thermostatic heating system, with overheat protection. The design includes rounded corners that facilitate cleaning, ensuring a hygienic environment.

It comes with a two-year service warranty and a one-year performance guarantee from Professional, Inc. More details can be found at www.electroluxusa.com/professional. The product features a thermocouple for temperature control, with base units connected to enhance stability, and offers a four-round plinth option. 

Included in the package are an insulated closing cover and a portioning shelf. The specifications include a capacity defined in liters and a minimum thermostat setting of 105°C (221°F), while the maximum temperature can reach 185°C (365°F).
