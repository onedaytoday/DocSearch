### Instruction Manual for EUN UK

**Instructions**  
This manual outlines important operating guidelines for the appliance. To ensure safety and optimal performance, it is crucial to follow these instructions closely. The appliance should only be operated under specified conditions and not exposed to excessive heat or moisture. Always refer to the guidelines provided in this manual.

**Cooling and Safety Precautions**  
Children should not be allowed to operate the appliance, as this could pose a risk of suffocation. Please ensure the appliance is properly cooled and charged by authorized technicians. Avoid prolonged exposure to direct sunlight and ensure proper ventilation around the appliance. When handling food, keep it at safe temperatures to avoid frostbite or freezer burn.

**Servicing**  
Repairs should only be conducted by authorized service personnel. Do not attempt to fix the appliance yourself, as this could result in serious malfunctions. For servicing, please contact Service Force, the authorized support service.

**Operating the Appliance**  
- **Warning Lights**: Indicators such as a red light may indicate issues like high temperatures or other operational problems. Ensure the thermostat settings are adjusted as needed.
- **Loading Capacity**: When loading fresh food, distribute it evenly to maximize cooling efficiency. Avoid overloading, as this can affect performance and food quality.

**Normal Operating Sounds**  
It’s normal to hear certain sounds during operation, like whirring or clicking, especially from the thermostat. Ensure that food is stored without overcrowding to maintain air circulation.

**Defrosting**  
Regularly check for frost buildup and defrost the appliance when necessary. Follow the specified procedures for defrosting, including switching off the appliance and using appropriate materials to manage the frost. 

**Cleaning**  
Before cleaning, disconnect the electricity supply. Use only recommended cleaning agents; avoid abrasive substances that could damage the surface and create lingering odors. 

**Service Contacts**  
For service inquiries or if issues arise, please contact Service Force directly. Further details can be found on the official website: www.serviceforce.co.uk.

**Customer Support**  
For additional questions regarding your appliance or service, please reach out through the provided customer support channels. 

Make sure to maintain the appliance properly to ensure its longevity and effectiveness. Follow all guidelines for optimal usage and performance.

### Installation Guidelines

When positioning the appliance, it is essential to follow the rating and instructions provided carefully. Ensure that the appliance is kept in an environment that avoids direct sunlight, excessive heat, or humidity. Refer to the specific temperature ranges (e.g., SN +10°C to +32°C, ST +18°C to +38°C) for optimal performance.

### Electrical Safety

This appliance must be earthed. The electrical connections should be performed according to color codes: 
- Earth: Green/Yellow
- Live: Brown
- Neutral: Blue 

Always use an appropriate fuse as specified (BS1362 or equivalent) for safety.

To assemble the housing, follow the provided indications carefully. Ensure all components (covers and screws) are used as specified, and verify that the housing is properly secured to avoid operational issues.
