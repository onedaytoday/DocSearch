**IMPORTANT SAFETY INSTRUCTIONS**  
Please ensure you follow these guidelines: READ ALL INSTRUCTIONS BEFORE OPERATING THIS VACUUM CLEANER.

**WARNING:** To minimize the risk of fire, electric shock, or injury:  
- Only use the charger and components provided.  
- Do not allow the charger or vacuum to overheat.  
- Ensure the vacuum is stored out of reach of children and is not used as a toy.  
- Avoid charging near heated surfaces or using heated extension cords.  
- Do not mutilate or incinerate batteries, as they can explode at high temperatures.  

This product is intended for household use only. For recycling information, visit www.electrolux.com. If you need to make a purchase, please refer to www.electroluxappliances.com (USA) or www.electrolux.ca.

### COMPONENTS AND ACCESSORIES  
- On/Off Switch  
- Charging Station  
- Brush/Crevice Release Buttons  

**UNPACKING**:  
Carefully remove the vacuum and its components from the carton.  

**WARNING:** Charge the unit for several hours before the first use.

### CHARGING STATION  
Keep the charging station away from heat and direct sunlight. Make sure it is properly connected to a power outlet. The charging light indicates the charging status.  

### MAINTENANCE  
**Emptying and Cleaning**: Ensure that the dust container is emptied and cleaned regularly (recommended after each use). To clean, release the appropriate buttons and detach the parts.  

**Troubleshooting**:  
If the unit does not operate, ensure the brush roll is off, the unit is charged, and the hose is clear of obstructions.
