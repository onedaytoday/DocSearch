# User Manual EUF

## Important Safety Information

Please keep this instruction manual for reference. If the appliance is sold or transferred to another owner, ensure that they are informed about its operation and the necessary safety precautions. This information is crucial for safe usage. 

### Safety Precautions 

- Always adhere to the specified criteria when using this heavy appliance. 
- Avoid exposing it to heat sources or direct sunlight, as this may affect its performance.
- Allow the unit adequate time to cool down before servicing. Use only authorized technicians for repairs.
- Do not use sharp instruments to remove ice, and follow the provided defrosting instructions carefully.

## Operation

The appliance features various operational modes indicated by an LED display. To turn on or off, utilize the corresponding switch/button. For temperatures around -18°C, adjust the thermostat accordingly. For different food types, settings will vary, so refer to the specific guidelines. 

### Defrosting and Cleaning 

- Prior to cleaning, disconnect the appliance from the electricity supply.
- Use mild cleaners exclusively for the interior; avoid any harsh chemicals that could damage the surfaces. 
- Ensure that air vents are not blocked and that there's proper circulation for effective cooling.

## Maintenance

Regular maintenance is essential for optimal performance. Always check that the appliance is clean and functioning correctly. If you encounter any issues, consult the troubleshooting section or contact Service Force for assistance.

## Customer Support

For service inquiries or spare parts, please contact Service Force. Further details are available on their website. If any issues arise, ensure you follow the troubleshooting steps outlined in the manual.

### Installation Instructions

**Warning:** If discarding an old appliance that has a lock or catch on the door, ensure it is rendered unusable to prevent trapping children inside.

**Positioning:** Place the appliance in a location that adheres to the specified temperature range. Optimal conditions are between +10°C to +32°C, with varied maximums for different models. Avoid areas with direct sunlight, heat sources, or cold drafts.

If the appliance requires built-in installation, refer to the provided instructions for proper supply access.

**Safety Warning:** This appliance must be earthed. If disconnecting the power, ensure the supply is turned off and correctly installed. Wire connections should follow these color codes:

- **Earth (E)**: Green/Yellow
- **Neutral (N)**: Blue
- **Live (L)**: Brown or Red

The molded plug should conform to BSI362 standards and be used in accordance with E.E.C. directives to minimize interference.

### Reversibility Instructions

To reverse the door, unscrew the hinges and spacers, reposition them, and secure back into place following the supplied instructions. Ensure that all clearances are observed for effective operation. If modifications are needed, the provided clearances must be maintained for optimal functionality.
