The S90 Island is designed for high-efficiency BTU installations and features a compact short item unit configuration. It includes a heavy-duty internal structure with a top-mounted cleaning unit located above and a thermocouple positioned in the front for easy operation. The unit is equipped with rounded corners for simplified cleaning and provides a two-year service warranty along with a one-year warranty from installation. The product also allows for connection between multiple units, enhancing its functionality.

For installation, the unit includes a closing cover and optional right-side portioning shelf, as well as a round plinth option. Specifications include a thermostat range from a minimum of 105°C (F) to a maximum of 185°C (F). The design allows for adaptability with LPG connections and ensures efficient heat insulation.
