**Installation Instructions for the WhiteBoardScreen Universal**

To install the WhiteBoardScreen, please follow the steps outlined below:

1. **Preparation**: Ensure you have all necessary materials, including wall screws, hollow anchors, and three M3 screws (model 02811AS) from www.elitescreens.com.

2. **Positioning**: Determine the appropriate location on the wall for the WhiteBoardScreen. Mark the position of the top section for accurate mounting.

3. **Wall Anchors**: If the chosen location requires it, use the provided hollow wall anchors for support. This is crucial for stability.

4. **Mounting the Screen**: Secure the WhiteBoardScreen to the wall by attaching it at the top using the three M3 screws as indicated.

5. **Final Check**: After installation, ensure the screen is securely fastened and adjustments can be made if needed.
