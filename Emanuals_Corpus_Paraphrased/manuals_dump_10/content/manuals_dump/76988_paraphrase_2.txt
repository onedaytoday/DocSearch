**ELMO Annotation Module EVR-100 Setup**

To set up the EVR-100, refer to the specifications: it measures 12.4"(W) x 14.8"(D) x 2.4"(H). 

**Optional Accessories:**  
The Writing Light Unit SV-450 can be used with the EVR-100, as well as with the ELMO EV-2000AF or HV-3000XG. This allows for a streamlined, user-friendly experience with both the EV-200 and HV-100XG models.
