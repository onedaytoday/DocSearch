Thank you for purchasing the GIBSON PERFORMANCE EXHAUST SYSTEM for your vehicle. If you have any further inquiries, please do not hesitate to contact Technical Support.

**EXHAUST INSTALLATION INSTRUCTIONS**:  
When working on the exhaust, ensure all pipes are cool to avoid burns!  
**Recommended Tools**: Wrench

**Installation Steps**:  
1. Ensure there is adequate clearance from all brake lines, shock boots, tires, fuel lines, etc.  
2. Begin by loosening the factory exhaust components.  
3. Apply WD-40 to the factory assemblies as needed.  
4. Install Assembly #A at the front and connect it to the factory components.  
5. Attach Assembly #B to the factory exhaust.  
6. Connect Assembly #C and secure it.  
7. Finally, install Assembly #D, ensuring all connections are tight and secure.
