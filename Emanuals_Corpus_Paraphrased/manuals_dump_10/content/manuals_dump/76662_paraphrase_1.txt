### POWER: 60W Performance Features

This Class A/B amplifier utilizes LAPT transistors for enhanced performance. Its design incorporates a mechanically isolated, honeycomb chassis with a curved profile, providing improved durability and sound quality. The SX-A6-J model supports various formats such as XM Ready, MM, and AM/FM, and is compatible with XM Connect-and-Play services.

### Connections and Specifications

The rear connections are designed to work seamlessly with Pioneer and Elite products, as well as services offered by XM Inc. This model is developed in collaboration with AIR STUDIOS, enhancing its audio fidelity and overall performance.
