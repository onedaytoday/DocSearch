### S90 Island Specification Overview

The S90 Island features a high BTU operation specifically designed for starch cooking processes. This robust unit has a rounded internal structure that connects various components seamlessly. 

It comes with a two-year service warranty and a one-year performance guarantee from Professional, Inc. For more details, visit www.electroluxusa.com/professional. 

Key specifications include:

- **Item Model:** SIS AIA
- **Operational Features:** Functions efficiently for all cooking processes incorporating water systems.
- **Water Drainage:** Includes a low-water shut-off feature to prevent overheating.
- **Thermocouple Placement:** Located at the front for ease of access when the unit is connected.
- **Design Options:** 
  - Four round burners.
  - Optional right-side portioning shelf.
  - Round closing cover.
