#### Important Information

1. **Product Safety**
   - Always keep the appliance clean and free from any obstructions that could affect its operation.
   - Ensure children are supervised around the appliance when in use.
   - Avoid using materials that could catch fire, such as cloths, near heating elements.

2. **Packing and Installation**
   - Follow the technical and operating instructions carefully. Ensure all packaging is removed and disposed of responsibly.
   - The appliance operates on electrical energy and must be installed by qualified personnel to avoid risks, such as electric shock.

3. **Operating Instructions**
   - Before turning on the appliance, make sure all parts are properly assembled.
   - If using matches to ignite the appliance, exercise caution and always keep a fire extinguisher nearby.
   - For optimal cooking, adjust the heat according to the food being prepared.

4. **Cleaning and Maintenance**
   - Regularly clean any grease or food splatters to prevent fire hazards.
   - Use a damp cloth with a suitable cleaner for routine cleaning.
   - If food is burned, switch off the appliance immediately and clean it after it cools.

5. **Technical Specifications**
   - Refer to the appliance's rating plate located on the back for model specifics.
   - Ensure compliance with relevant directives and regulations, such as the Appliance Directive and EMC Directive. 

6. **Customer Support**
   - For any service-related inquiries, please contact AEG Service Force. You can also visit [www.serviceforce.co.uk](http://www.serviceforce.co.uk) for more information.

7. **Guarantee Conditions**
   - AEG guarantees that the appliance will be free from defects for a specified period after purchase. 
   - Proper installation and use according to these instructions are essential for warranty coverage.

For more detailed troubleshooting or service needs, please keep this manual handy and follow the instructions outlined for optimal appliance performance.
