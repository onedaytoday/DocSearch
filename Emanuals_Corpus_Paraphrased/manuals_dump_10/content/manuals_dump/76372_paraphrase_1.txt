The W4280X, W4350X, and W4600X models offer versatile options for heating and non-heating applications, designed for professional laundry setups. They boast an efficient rated capacity and extraction G-factor, operating on a 230/400V electrical system. These machines can handle both cold and hot water, with steam capabilities, and are certified with ISO standards and IP 24D for protection. The models support various water connections and heating configurations, including 3-phase and single-phase electricity options. Additional technical specifications, such as sound levels and dimensions, can be found on the Electrolux website. Specifications are subject to change.
