## Safety and Operating Instructions:

For your safety, please read this manual thoroughly. If you notice any damage, such as cracks, disconnect the appliance from the power supply and contact AEG Service Force immediately. Ensure that children are supervised around the appliance and are not allowed to operate it without adult supervision. Always allow the appliance to cool before cleaning.

Repairs should only be carried out by an authorized AEG Service Force technician as improper handling may lead to serious malfunctions or safety hazards.

## Cleaning and Maintenance:

To maintain the appliance, please clean it properly after each use, especially areas that may collect grease or food particles. Use a damp cloth for general cleaning; avoid harsh scouring pads which may damage the finish. If the splashguard requires cleaning, ensure it's removed correctly and do not put it in the dishwasher. Instead, clean it with a soft cloth.

## Installation Instructions:

Ensure you follow the installation guidelines for proper mounting and electrical connections. The cooktop requires a clearance suitable for the thickness of the work surface. Use the screws provided to secure it in place, ensuring that the installation meets local electrical codes.

The appliance is designed to connect to a 230V power supply with a wattage of 1500W, compliant with relevant safety and EMC regulations (73/23/EEC, 89/336/EEC).

## Warranty and Customer Service:

This appliance comes with a warranty for the first purchaser, covering defects in materials and workmanship for a specified period. Repairs performed by unauthorized individuals or incorrect installations will void this warranty. Should you require service, contact AEG Service Force, detailing any issues experienced.

If this appliance is moved to another country, please be aware of the regulations governing its use in that jurisdiction. Service is only provided for domestic, household use.

For further information on AEG products or assistance, please contact AEG Customer Service as noted above.
