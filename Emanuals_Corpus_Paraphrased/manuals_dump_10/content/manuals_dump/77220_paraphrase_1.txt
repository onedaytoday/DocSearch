**TEATIME DESIGN WATER KETTLE/TEA POT COMBINATION STRUCTURE**

This manual pertains to the TEATIME Design Water Kettle and Tea Pot combination. It provides important safety information and instructions for use, cleaning, and maintenance. 

### BEFORE FIRST USE
- Ensure the unit is connected to the correct power supply. 
- Check that the power supply type matches the specifications provided. 
- Use the appliance only as intended.

### OPERATION
- The kettle features an On/Off switch and a heating plate. 
- Follow the MIN/MAX indicators for optimal usage. 
- Avoid operating the kettle without water. 

### SAFETY INFORMATION
- To prevent electric shock, do not immerse the kettle or cord in water.
- Keep the kettle out of reach of children.
- Disconnect from the power supply before cleaning.
- Allow the kettle to cool completely before cleaning.

### CLEANING AND MAINTENANCE
- Use a damp cloth for cleaning the exterior. 
- Regularly empty and rinse the kettle with fresh water. 
- Avoid using abrasive cleaners.

### TECHNICAL DATA
- Power: 2200W (V~)
- Typical usage: 35W - 40W

### WARRANTY AND CUSTOMER SERVICE
- Please retain your purchase receipt.
- The warranty covers manufacturing defects within the specified period.
- For any issues, please contact our customer service.

By following these guidelines, you will ensure the safe and efficient operation of your TEATIME kettle and tea pot combination.
