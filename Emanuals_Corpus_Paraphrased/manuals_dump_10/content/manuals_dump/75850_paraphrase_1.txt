### Instruction Booklet for the ERN Series (UK)

#### Instructions
These instructions are important for ensuring proper use of the appliance. For any concerns regarding setup, operation, or compatibility, please refer to the specifications outlined within this booklet.

Considerations:
- Avoid exposing the appliance to direct heat or moisture.
- Follow the installation guidelines carefully to ensure safety and efficiency.
- Allow the appliance to settle before use, especially after transportation, to prevent ignition risks.

**Safety Warnings:**
- Keep children away to prevent accidental injury or suffocation.
- Service and repairs should only be performed by authorized technicians to maintain safety and functionality.

#### Appliance Features:
- **Food Storage**: Place items on the designated shelves; avoid overcrowding.
- **Defrosting**: Ensure to defrost correctly to maintain food quality; follow guidance on thermostat settings.
- **Thermostat Control**: Adjust the thermostat as necessary based on external temperature conditions to ensure efficient cooling.
  
**Food Storage Guidelines**:
- Allow warm food to cool before placing it in the appliance.
- Use airtight containers or wrap food properly to prevent contamination.
- Regularly clean shelves to maintain hygiene.

#### Cleaning Procedures:
- Disconnect the appliance from the electricity supply before cleaning.
- Use soft cleaning materials; avoid harsh detergents or abrasive products as these can damage surfaces.
- Ensure all drainage areas are free from blockages to maintain proper operation.

#### Troubleshooting:
If issues arise (e.g., a malfunctioning light or failure to cool), refer to the guidelines provided or contact the Service Force for assistance. They can help diagnose problems related to mechanical breakdowns or incorrect thermostat settings.

### Service Information
For service inquiries or to report an issue, please contact the Service Force at the numbers listed or visit their website for more information. Ensure that the appliance is correctly maintained following the warranty conditions, which cover workmanship defects within the specified period.

**Positioning Guidelines**:
The appliance should be placed in a controlled environment, away from direct sunlight and heating sources, for optimal performance. Ideal ambient temperatures range from +10°C to +38°C, depending on the model.

#### Disposal Instructions:
When discarding an old appliance, ensure that it is rendered unusable to prevent accidents, especially those involving children. Follow local regulations regarding appliance disposal.

For further inquiries, please reach out to our customer service indicated below or visit our website for comprehensive support.

**WARNING: THIS APPLIANCE MUST BE EARTHED**. Ensure that the appropriate connections are made to prevent any hazards. Disconnect the appliance from the power source before installation. The appliance must be connected as follows: 

- **Earth (E)**: Green/yellow wire
- **Neutral (N)**: Blue wire
- **Live (L)**: Brown wire

Use the appropriate fuse (D207 amp) that complies with BS1362 ASTA or BSI standards. This equipment adheres to the directives set by the E.E.C., including the EEC Directive on Electromagnetic Compatibility. During installation, make sure that the wiring is securely connected to avoid any breakdown.

**Building Instructions**: Ensure that the housing structure aligns with the specified height (PR0). Assemble according to D526 and D022 guidelines, ensuring that no parts come into contact with the mounting surfaces. Use screws (I) provided with covers C-D as indicated. For model PR266 parts A, B, and C, perform the assembly as shown in PR166 (A) using the supplied screws. Ensure compliance with assembly instructions for PR33/2 (C) and confirm that the components fit together correctly. If necessary, verify that the clearance is maintained for proper operation, and double-check the assembly of covers (D) and (B) with respect to models PR167 and PR168.
