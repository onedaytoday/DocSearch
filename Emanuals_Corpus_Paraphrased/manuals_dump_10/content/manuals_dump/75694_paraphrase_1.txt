### SMART FT (RH06RE2HU) & (RH06RE2HRU) Overview

The SMART FT models are designed with internal high-density AISI polyurethane insulation to optimize performance efficiency. They feature a microswitch that enables automatic shut-off for energy conservation. With HACCP-compliant capabilities, they track service history and provide high operational performance. The units are eco-friendly, being free from CFC and HCFC. 

These models offer a two-year service warranty and a one-year performance warranty post-installation. They are designed for easy installation, with options for wall-mounting and accommodating both Fahrenheit and Celsius scale operations, suitable for meat and other perishable goods. 

### Key Features:

- **Ease of Cleaning**: The forced cooling system ensures uniform temperature across the interior, constructed from high-quality AISI material with seamless corners for enhanced cleanliness.
  
- **User-Friendly**: The SMART FT models come with a clear interface allowing easy operation. They utilize HACCP tracking with acoustic alerts to notify users of any necessary adjustments based on the four-probe temperature monitoring system.

- **Maintenance**: Cooling mechanisms allow access for maintenance through the top or rear. The design facilitates straightforward service handling.

- **Environmental Considerations**: The insulation is designed for superior thermal efficiency, and the units are CFC and HCFC-free.
