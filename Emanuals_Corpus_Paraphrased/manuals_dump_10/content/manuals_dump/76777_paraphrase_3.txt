**Specifications for ELUH3621**

**General Information:**
The ELUH3621 is a premium type of undermount sink designed for durability and sound dampening. Its finish is highlighted for aesthetic appeal and protection against wear.

**Design Features:**
This model includes the ELUH362110 variant, which is an exposed version. It delivers optimum sound insulation and is suitable for various countertop types. 

**Optional Accessories:**
Recommended accessories include:
- LKRB1614
- LKERBSS 
- LKOBG1821 
- Cutting Board: CBT2213 
- LC1412

These products comply with ANSI A112.19.3M standards set by the International Association of Plumbing and Mechanical Officials (IAPMO), as part of the Uniform Plumbing Code (UPC).

**Sink Dimensions (Inches):**
For the right countertop, models ELUH3621L, ELUH3621R, ELUH362110L, and ELUH362110R are available. A template is provided for accurate installation, ensuring correct front-to-back alignment.

**Mounting Guidelines:**
Each ELUH sink includes a template for installation, but note that countertop mounting brackets and fasteners are not included. Silicone caulk is also not furnished and should be selected based on customer preferences for edge contouring.

**Conversion Note:**
To convert all dimensions from inches to millimeters, multiply by 25.4.
