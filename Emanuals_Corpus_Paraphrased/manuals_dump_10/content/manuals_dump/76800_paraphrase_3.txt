**Design Features:** Includes a heavy-duty coating for sound dampening. 

**Note:** Unless otherwise stated, the product is provided as depicted.

**Sink Dimensions (Inches):** Models ILGR6022L, ILGR6022R, ILGR6022LC, and ILGR6022RC feature right-corner configurations. The dimensions from front to back are consistent with specifications, ensuring compatibility across various manufacturers. 

**Country of Manufacturing:** Printed in the U.S.A. 

**Countertop Shipping:** Refer to the dimensions provided on the reverse side. All measurements are in inches; to convert to millimeters, multiply by 25.4.
