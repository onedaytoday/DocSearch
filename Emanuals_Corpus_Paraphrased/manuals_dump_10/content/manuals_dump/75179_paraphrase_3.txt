### RANGE COMPOSITION

Zanussi Professional offers functional ovens equipped with cooking chambers designed for optimal performance. The ovens are insulated with thick materials to reduce heat escape and maintain consistent temperatures. They also include protected internal lighting and armored heating elements that are completely sealed from the cooking chamber, ensuring safety and ease of operation.
