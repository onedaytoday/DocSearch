The Food DSLHD14 is a single-phase appliance designed for food processing. It features durable self-lubricating bushings and bearings, with a polished chromium-plated finish that enhances its longevity and efficiency. The unit allows for thickness adjustments and includes a powerful motor, making it ideal for handling cooked meats and other tough products.

Constructed with high-quality materials such as ABS, LEXAN, PLEXIGLAS, and AISI, it meets safety standards set by UL and NSF. The model is equipped with a NEMA 5-15P plug and offers a one-year warranty. Its mechanical safety features include a meat cover for added protection, while electrical safety is ensured by a micro-switch that prevents reactivation during maintenance. For further details, visit the Electrolux Professional website.
