**S90 Char-Broiler Specifications**

The S90 Char-Broiler (Model: WDGRAFOOOO) features a sturdy base designed for high efficiency, offering impressive BTU output and easy cleaning capabilities. It allows for the convenient rotation of various meats and supports both back-to-back and side-by-side installations. The unit is equipped with a grease management system and is constructed from heavy-duty materials, ensuring durability.

It comes with a two-year service warranty and a one-year performance warranty from Professional, Inc. Further details can be found at www.electroluxusa.com/professional. 

The design includes a thermocouple for accurate temperature management and high splash guards to facilitate easy cleaning. The unit can be configured with optional components such as a round plinth, portioning shelf, and scraper for enhanced functionality.

The S90 Char-Broiler also features a depth-optimized cavity for effective heat insulation. Note that all specifications are subject to change without prior notice.
