### Important Safety Instructions

For your safety, please adhere to the following guidelines. This appliance is heavy; ensure safe handling during installation and operation. Electrical supply must be properly grounded, and the appliance should not be exposed to moisture or extreme heat. When moving the freezer, keep the back clear as instructed to prevent damage. After installation, let the appliance settle for several hours before use. Avoid overloading the compartments which can restrict airflow and lead to poor performance. If the appliance shows signs of malfunction, seek assistance from authorized service personnel.

**Child Safety**: Keep children away from the appliance to prevent any risk of suffocation. Use the appliance only as intended. 

**Food Safety**: Frozen items should be stored correctly to avoid frostbite or freezer burn. For specific guidelines on storage and defrosting, refer to the manufacturer’s instructions.

### Defrosting

Frost build-up is normal. To defrost, unplug the appliance and let it thaw using recommended methods. Use layers of newspaper to absorb moisture and maintain temperature. Once defrosting is complete, ensure thorough cleaning before reconnecting.

---

### Maintenance and Cleaning

Always disconnect the appliance from the electrical supply before cleaning. Use only gentle cleaners to avoid damaging surfaces or leaving behind odors. Regular maintenance improves efficiency and prolongs appliance life.

#### Installation
For optimal performance, position the appliance away from heat sources, boilers, and direct sunlight. Operating temperature range is between +10°C and +32°C. Refer to the installation instructions for built-in and reversible configurations as necessary.

### Electrical Connection
**Warning:** This appliance must be properly earthed. Ensure that the electrical supply is disconnected before installation. Use the following color code for wiring connections: 

- **Earth:** Green/Yellow
- **Live (L):** Brown or Red
- **Neutral (N):** Blue

Ensure the appliance is connected to the appropriate terminals labeled accordingly. Use a BS1362 ASTA or BSI approved fuse. For further assistance, contact Service Force.

This appliance complies with E.E.C. Directives regarding interference and safety.
