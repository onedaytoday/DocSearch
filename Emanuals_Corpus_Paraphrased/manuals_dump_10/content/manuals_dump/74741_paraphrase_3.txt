### Installation Instructions

**Warning:** When disposing of an old appliance with a door lock or catch, ensure it is rendered unusable to prevent young children from becoming trapped inside.

#### Positioning

The appliance should be installed in a location with the following temperature ratings:

- SN: +10°C to +32°C 
- ST: +18°C to +38°C 
- T: +18°C to +43°C 

Ensure it is situated away from direct sunlight and other heat sources, and should not be exposed to temperatures below 10°C (50°F). If temperatures exceed these limits, reposition the appliance appropriately. Allow for free airflow around the back and top of the unit, with at least a few centimeters of clearance.

#### Installation

**Warning:** This appliance must be earthed. Proper grounding is essential. Disconnect the supply before installation. Ensure easy access for servicing by positioning the appliance properly. 

The installation includes screws and spacers, as shown in the accompanying diagrams. 

Wire connections should be made as follows:

- Earth (green/yellow) to terminal marked E
- Neutral (blue) to terminal marked N
- Live (brown or red) to terminal marked L 

Use a properly molded plug complying with BSI362 or ASTA standards. 

This appliance conforms to the relevant E.E.C. Directives regarding electromagnetic compatibility.

#### Reversibility Instructions

Follow these steps to reverse the appliance door:

1. Remove screws and the cover from the back panel. 
2. Unscrew the front cover and reposition as needed.
3. Allow the appliance to settle for a few hours.

Ensure all screws are tightened properly after adjustments.
