### Important Safety Instructions
These guidelines ensure the safety of both users and the appliance. Clients should consult the manual thoroughly. Always exercise caution when handling the appliance as it is heavy. Ensure proper electrical supply and avoid placing it close to heat sources. Keep the rear of the unit clear as per the provided instructions. Allow the unit to stabilize for a specified number of hours upon startup, as it operates under high compatibility conditions. If the appliance exhibits any signs of malfunction or ignition, it must be serviced by authorized personnel.

#### Child Safety
Keep children away from the appliance to avoid incidents of suffocation or injury! Handling and maintenance should be done by authorized technicians, especially when dealing with cooling systems, as exposure to heat sources may cause issues.

### Appliance Description
- **Fresh Food Compartment**: Designed for optimal storage of fresh items.

### Operation
Utilize the thermostat to control temperatures efficiently. The red warning light indicates when temperatures rise above -12°C, while the yellow light shows when the fresh food compartment is running. Adjust the thermostat settings as needed.

#### Fast Freezing
For effective fast-freezing, ensure that food is placed strategically and avoid overcrowding. Once food is properly frozen, the function may be turned off manually after a specified duration.

### Defrosting
Frost buildup should be managed regularly. For defrosting, adjust the thermostat as detailed in the manual. Utilize appropriate methods to handle thawed food safely and effectively.

### Maintenance and Cleaning
Always disconnect the appliance from the electricity supply before cleaning. Never use harsh detergents or abrasive materials, as they can damage surfaces and create unpleasant odors. Clean both interior and exterior surfaces thoroughly using suitable methods.

### Troubleshooting
If issues arise, consult the troubleshooting section for common problems. If malfunctions persist, contact Service Force for assistance. Ensure all electrical connections are intact and that the thermostat settings are correct based on the surrounding environment.

### Installation Guidelines

For optimal performance, position the appliance in a location with ambient temperatures ranging from 10°C (50°F) to 32°C (90°F), while avoiding heat sources and direct sunlight. Ensure there is adequate airflow around the back and top of the unit.

### Electrical Connection

**Warning**: This appliance must be earthed. 

Ensure proper disconnection from the power supply and fit a suitable fuse before installation. The wiring must conform to the appropriate color codes, with earth wires connected accordingly:

- **Earth (E)**: Green/Yellow  
- **Neutral (N)**: Blue  
- **Live (L)**: Brown

Use a fuse that complies with BS1362 standards to prevent electrical interference.
