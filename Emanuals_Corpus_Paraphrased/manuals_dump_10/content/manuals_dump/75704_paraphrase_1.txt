**SMART FT (RH14RE2GEU) Short Specification Overview**

The SMART FT is engineered with internal AISI high-density expanded polyurethane insulation. It features a microswitch designed to turn off the unit when not in use, in accordance with SMART Control systems, which include HACCP monitoring for service history as needed. Outlet lighting is also included.

Specifications include:

- Temperature range: +2°C to +8°C (or +35°F to +46°F), adaptable for both meat and other food items.
- Environmentally friendly, being CFC and HCFC-free.
- Capacity designed for 2/1GN coated shelves.
- Two-year service warranty and one-year performance guarantee from the date of installation.

Additional Features:
- **Ease of Installation:** Options available for various mounting configurations.
- **Ease of Cleaning:** Designed for easy maintenance with forced air circulation for uniform temperature distribution. Constructed from durable AISI materials, it features rounded corners for efficient cleaning.
- **User-Friendly:** Accommodates GN shelves with sufficient lighting, equipped with SMART technology including HACCP monitoring via acoustic alerts and logging capabilities.
- **Environmentally Considerate:** Utilizes cyclopentane insulation for effective performance, adhering to eco-friendly standards. 

Technical Details:

- Operating temperature range: +2°C to +8°C (or +35°F to +46°F).
- Unit type: Top-hinged with right-side configuration.
- Power consumption: 450W.
