The VSX-90TXV A/V receiver enhances audio experiences by providing high-quality sound processing and connectivity options. It features advanced technologies, including Sound Direct Amplification, Phase DSP MCACC with EQ, and support for THX Select2 and DTS-ES surround sound formats. The receiver supports HDMI connectivity, including HDMI pass-through and switching capabilities, and is XM-ready for enhanced surround audio.

Key specifications include compatibility with multiple surround formats such as Dolby II, EX, and various DTS versions. All rights related to HDMI and THX technologies are held by their respective companies, including HDMI Licensing LLC and THX. The VSX-90TXV is designed for seamless integration with other Pioneer Elite products, offering a user-friendly experience through an LCD learning remote. Note that some features require separate subscriptions or services.
