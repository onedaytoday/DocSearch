**UNPACKING AND INITIAL CHECK**  
Caution: Ensure the unit is turned off and cool before charging for the first time (approximately 3 hours). Assembly requires a locking screw, which can be tightened using a screwdriver or coin. 

**CHARGING STATION**  
Place the charger away from direct heat or sunlight. Connect it correctly to ensure optimal performance. The unit features indicators for battery capacity that will light up based on the charge status. If any lights indicate overload or problems, clear any obstructions and charge accordingly.

**CLEANING WITH BRUSHROLLCLEAN TECHNOLOGY**  
Utilize this feature to maintain effective suction. Ensure hoses and brushes are free from obstructions that may impact performance. It is recommended to use this feature regularly for optimum cleaning efficiency. Caution: Do not leave the unit unattended while in cleaning mode. 

**MAINTENANCE**  
Regularly empty and clean the dust container (recommended after every use). Detach and clean the brushroll and hose, ensuring they are free of debris.
