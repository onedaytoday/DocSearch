**S90 Char-Broiler**

The S90 Char-Broiler (model: WDGUAFOOOO) is a high-efficiency cooking unit designed for optimal BTU output and ease of maintenance. It is engineered for the grilling of meats with the capability for both front and back-to-back installations. The unit features a robust grease management system and is constructed with heavy-duty internal components. 

The design enables easy cleaning, with splash guards and a thermocouple for internal temperature regulation. The S90 can be equipped with various options, including a round plinth, scraper for cleaning, and a right portioning shelf, depending on your specifications.
