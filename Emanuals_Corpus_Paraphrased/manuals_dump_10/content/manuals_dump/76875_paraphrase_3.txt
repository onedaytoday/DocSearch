### General Specifications and Design Features

**Depth and Recess:** Finished to a polished highlight with sound-dampening properties. 

**Drain Types:** Includes PSLVR1917 with LKF294 overflow assembly, or PSLVR1917LO assembly, which consists of a housing and washer assembly. Recommended models include PSLVR1917 (with LK174, LKAD174, LK175) and PSLVR1917LO (with LK174LO, LKAD174LO). 

**Compliance:** All models are furnished with CS3 unless specified otherwise and conform to ANSI A112.19.3M.

**Sustainability Standards:** These products meet standards set by the International Association of Plumbing and Mechanical Officials (IAPMO) as part of the Uniform Plumbing Code (UPC).

### Sink Dimensions (Inches)*

- **Model with Overflow:** PSLVR1917 or PSLVR1917LO 
- **Center Configuration:** Flat corners with overflow center to the right and front to back dimensions specified.

These models are designed for ease of installation and functionality while remaining competitive with offerings from other manufacturers. 

**Note:** All dimensions are in inches; to convert to millimeters, multiply by 25.4.

For additional product dimensions, refer to the opposite side.

**Drilling Configurations:** Configurations for hole drilling are provided for model CS3 and PSLVR1917, PSLVR1917CS3, PSLVR1917LOCS3, etc. 

**Manufacturing Location:** Printed in the U.S.A., model 1-70F.
