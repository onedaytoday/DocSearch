**IMPORTANT SAFETY INFORMATION**

Please ensure that this manual is kept for future reference. If the appliance is transferred to another owner or location, it is crucial that the next user understands the functionality, operation, and safety guidelines outlined here. Failure to adhere to these guidelines could result in safety hazards.

Exercise caution when operating the appliance, particularly when adhering to the specifications. This is a heavy device and requires proper handling. Ensure that all installations are consistent with the provided instructions. Allow adequate cooling time for the appliance, and only have it serviced by authorized technicians to avoid potential issues.

Avoid exposure to direct sunlight and refrain from placing any items that could obstruct airflow or create frost or freezer burn. Always adhere strictly to the manufacturer’s guidelines for defrosting.

**CLEANING AND MAINTENANCE**

Before cleaning or servicing, disconnect the appliance from the electrical supply. Use soft cleaning agents and avoid abrasive materials or highly scented cleaners as these can damage the interior surfaces and leave unwanted odors. 

**TROUBLESHOOTING**

If you encounter issues, such as lights not functioning or water leakage, refer to the troubleshooting section or contact Service Force for assistance. Ensure all parts are properly installed and the appliance is receiving adequate power.

**SERVICE AND SPARE PARTS**

For service inquiries or to order parts, contact Service Force directly. Additional information can be found online or in the manual. Ensure to keep your purchase receipt for warranty purposes.

**GUARANTEE CONDITIONS**

You are entitled to a warranty covering defects in workmanship for a specified period following the date of purchase. This warranty covers repairs, provided the appliance has been used according to the guidelines. Unauthorized repairs could void the warranty.

**INSTALLATION Warning:** If you are disposing of an old appliance that has a lock or catch on the door, ensure that it is rendered unusable to prevent young children from becoming trapped inside.

**Positioning Instructions:** The appliance should be placed in a location that meets its temperature rating. The recommended temperature ranges are as follows:
- SN: +10°C to +32°C
- ST: +18°C to +38°C
- T: +18°C to +43°C

Choose a position that avoids direct sunlight and heat sources. If conditions are warmer or colder, follow the provided installation instructions carefully.

**Shelf Installation:** To adjust the shelves, follow these steps:
- Tilt the shelf in the indicated direction.
- Remove the shelf and any obstructions.
- Ensure correct alignment with the installation instructions.

**WARNING:** THIS APPLIANCE MUST BE EARTHED. If fitting is required, ensure that it is safely connected. Disconnect the power supply before installation.

**Wiring Instructions:** Follow the color coding for the wiring:
- Earth (E) is typically green/yellow.
- Neutral (N) is usually blue.
- Live (L) is often brown or red.

Use a molded plug that complies with BSI362 or ASTA standards. Obtain any necessary replacement components through Service Force.

This appliance adheres to E.E.C. Directives on electromagnetic interference.

**Reversibility Instructions:** If adjustments are needed, unscrew the relevant components and reposition them as instructed.

**Built-in Recess Height:** Verify the necessary clearances between the top and the appliance to ensure proper installation.

**Cover Fitting Instructions:** Apply covers (C-D) and (B) as per the illustrations, fastening with the supplied screws. If necessary, adjust clearances during operations for proper functionality.
