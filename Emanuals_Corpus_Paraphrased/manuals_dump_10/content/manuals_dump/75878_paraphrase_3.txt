**Safety and Maintenance:**  
To prevent accidents, ensure the appliance is kept away from children to avoid the risk of suffocation. Use a fire extinguisher if necessary. Before cleaning, switch off the appliance and disconnect it from the power supply. Repairs should only be conducted by authorized personnel to prevent risks from inexperienced handling.

**Location and Installation:**  
The appliance should be installed in a well-ventilated area, away from direct sunlight and any heat sources. The rating plate indicates the optimal temperature settings for the environment in which the unit will be located. Ensure the appliance has proper spacing for ventilation.

**Electrical Requirements:**  
This appliance must be earthed. Ensure that the connection to the electricity supply corresponds with the specifications outlined on the rating plate. The colors of the wires should match as follows:
- Earth: Green/Yellow
- Neutral: Blue
- Live: Brown

**Operational Features:**
- The ON/OFF switch indicates the appliance's status, with "1" signifying the highest temperature setting.
- Optimal storage temperatures range from 2°C to 8°C, depending on the food type and required cooling levels.

**Defrosting and Cleaning:**  
Defrost the appliance only as indicated in the relevant sections. When switching off the unit, ensure to turn off the interior light and allow time for any residual frost to thaw and evaporate completely. 

**Shelves and Storage:**  
Shelves can be adjusted to various heights to accommodate different storage needs. Ensure that they are secure and positioned correctly. 

**Normal Operational Sounds:**  
Some operational sounds such as whirring or clicking may be heard during normal usage. These sounds are typically from the cooling system and thermostat adjustments.

**Cleaning Guidelines:**  
Always disconnect the appliance from the power supply before cleaning. Use appropriate cleaning products and avoid those with bleach. Ensure drainage outlets remain clear to prevent water buildup.
