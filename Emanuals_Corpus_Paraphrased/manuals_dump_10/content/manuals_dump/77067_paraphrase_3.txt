**Supported Resolutions:** VGA, SVGA  
**Aspect Ratio:** Typical  
**Brightness:** 262K colors  
**Viewing Angles:** Left/Right - typical, Up/Down - typical  
**RGB Input Voltage:** 0.7V to TTL or Negative  
**Connectors:** LED indicator, OSD control for Horizontal/Vertical Position, Phase, Track, Text/Graphics, Information, ROM/RAM  
**DDC Compliance:** DDC1/2B  

**Touch Technology:** AccuTouch (optional)

**Operating Temperature:** 0°C to 50°C (32°F to 122°F)  
**Storage Temperature:** -20°C to 60°C (-4°F to 140°F)  
**Humidity Range:** 20% to 80%
