### Touchmonitor 1537L LCD Touchmonitor Overview

**Model**: ET1537L  
**Part Number**: E250918  
**Manufacturer**: TouchSystems, Inc.  
**Technology**: AccuTouch, AT4, CarrollTouch, MonitorMouse, SecureTouch

#### Specifications:
- **Display Type**: 15" LCD (XGA, TFT)
- **Connectivity**: USB, RS232
- **Resolution**: Supports VGA, SVGA, XGA via VESA DPMS, DDC1/2B
