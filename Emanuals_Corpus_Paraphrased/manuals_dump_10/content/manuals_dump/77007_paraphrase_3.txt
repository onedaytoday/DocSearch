### LCD MP-700E/MP-50E Overview

The MP-700E and MP-50E models both feature high-quality imaging capabilities suitable for various applications, including presentations and document displays.  

#### MP-700E Features:
- **Display Technology**: 1.3-inch TFT LCD with a native resolution of XGA and compatibility with UXGA.
- **Optical Features**: 6.25X zoom capability and image rotation for optimized viewing.
- **Light Source**: 300W super-high-pressure mercury lamp.
- **Sound Output**: 2W speaker.
- **Power Requirements**: Operates on VAC 220-240.
- **Dimensions**: 400(W) x 315(D) mm.
  
#### MP-50E Features:
- **Display Technology**: 1.0-inch TFT LCD with a native resolution of XGA and compatibility with UXGA.
- **Optical Features**: 6.25X zoom capability and image rotation for enhanced presentation flexibility.
- **Light Source**: 260W super-high-pressure mercury lamp.
- **Sound Output**: 1W speaker.
- **Power Requirements**: Operates on VAC 220-240.
- **Dimensions**: 390(W) x 380(D) mm.
