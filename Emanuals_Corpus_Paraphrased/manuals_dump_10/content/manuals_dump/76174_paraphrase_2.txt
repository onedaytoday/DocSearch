# Electrolux Cooker Hood EFT UK Instruction Manual

Thank you for choosing this Electrolux cooker hood. We recommend that you refer to this manual for effective use. For further inquiries about products or customer service, please visit [Electrolux Customer Service](http://www.electrolux.se).

### Ownership and Information
For appliance ownership details, refer to the provided information and instructions regarding extraction and cleaning. Use synthetic grease and charcoal (optional) as prescribed. To replace the charcoal filter or troubleshoot issues, follow the service conditions outlined in the manual.

### Safety Information
Ensure compliance with BS.6172, BS.5440, BS.6891, and BS.5482 standards (LP Gas Regulations) and the Building Regulations in your country. If the appliance becomes overheated, deactivate it and allow it to cool before operating again. 

Children should not operate the cooker hood without supervision, as it can reach high temperatures and pose a fire hazard. 

### Operating Instructions
To operate the appliance:
1. **Extraction**: The cooker hood removes grease and odors, filtering them through the grease filter and, if used, charcoal filter.
2. **Cleaning**: Disconnect the appliance from the power supply when cleaning. Use protective gear and non-abrasive cleaners. 

**Synthetic Grease**: Replace when it changes color, indicating saturation. To clean, press the catches as illustrated, and remove the filter for cleaning. 

**Charcoal Filter**: Clean every three months or more frequently depending on usage. To replace the charcoal filter, unscrew it while holding the filter securely.

### Maintenance
Regularly check the performance and light function. If the light is not working, ensure the E14 screw bulb (220-240V 40W) is correctly installed. 

### Service and Support
If issues arise, refer to the troubleshooting section or contact Service Force. Record all details related to purchase and service history.

### Warranty Information
The warranty covers defects in workmanship for a certain period post-purchase. It excludes damages caused by improper use or unauthorized service.

### Technical Specifications
- **Dimensions**: Height, Width, Depth
- **Weight**: Gross and Net
- **Electrical Supply**: 115W power consumption with specific ratings for fan motor and lamp.
- **Installation Requirements**: Suitable for installations above specified electric and gas hobs, adhering to I.E.E. regulations.

### Installation Guidelines
Follow clearance height specifications to ensure safe operation. Use provided mounting templates and screws during installation. Ensure that ducting complies with safety regulations (e.g., DIN4102-B1) and is connected correctly for effective performance.

For detailed installation steps, refer to the instructions provided in this manual and ensure proper reinforcement where necessary.

**Note**: This appliance is double insulated and should not be earthed. 

For any modifications or inquiries regarding your specific installation, please consult an authorized service provider.
