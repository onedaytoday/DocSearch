### Installation Warning

**IMPORTANT:** When disposing of an old appliance equipped with a door lock or catch, it is crucial to render it unusable to prevent young children from becoming trapped inside. 

#### Positioning

Ensure the appliance is installed in a location that meets the specified temperature range for optimal operation. The ratings for various models are as follows:

- SN: +10°C to +32°C 
- ST: +18°C to +38°C 
- T: +18°C to +43°C 

Choose a location that is shielded from direct sunlight and away from cold areas, ensuring the temperature does not drop below +10°C (50°F). If the ambient temperature is lower, the appliance may not function correctly. Maintain sufficient space around the back and top of the unit to allow for proper airflow. Ideally, there should be at least the height of the appliance's top from the floor.

**Warning:** This appliance must be properly grounded. If requested, disconnect the appliance from the power supply before installing. 

#### Electrical Connection

Use the provided rear spacers as depicted to prevent any restrictions. Ensure all screws are tightened adequately. The wiring connections must follow these colors:
- Earth (E) - Green/Yellow
- Neutral (N) - Blue
- Live (L) - Brown

Ensure the molded plug complies with BSI362 ASTA specifications. This appliance meets the E.E.C. Directives on electromagnetic compatibility.
