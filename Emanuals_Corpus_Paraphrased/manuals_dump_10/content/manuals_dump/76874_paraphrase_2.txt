**PSLVR1916 and PSLVR1916CS Specifications**

**General Information:** Both models are self-rimming bearing types.

**Design Features:** The depth and recess are polished to a bright finish, enhancing their visual appeal and sound performance.

**Other Details:** The PSLVR1916 is compatible with the LKF293 assembly, while the PSLVR1916CS is designed to work with the LKF293 overflow assembly. Both units feature a three-inch outer diameter at the top. They adhere to the ANSI A112.19.3M standards and are recognized by the International Association of Plumbing and Mechanical Officials (IAPMO) under the Uniform Plumbing Code (UPC).

**Sink Dimensions (Inches):** 
- **PSLVR1916:** Dimensions with overflow centers.
- **PSLVR1916CS:** Dimensions with overflow centers.
