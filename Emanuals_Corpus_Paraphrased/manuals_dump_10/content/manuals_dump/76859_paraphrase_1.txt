### Specifications Overview

**Type**: Self-rimming bearing with the highest specifications.

**Design Features**:
- **Depth**: LR(Q) Depth and LRAD(Q) Depth.
- **Corners**: Coved corners and recessed designs with highlighted finishes.
- **Sound Protection**: Models LR(Q)3322 and LRAD(Q)3322 are protected against sound transmission. 

**Positioning**: Units are designed to be left-to-right and off-center front-to-back as illustrated.

**Note**: Unless specified otherwise, configurations will be furnished as depicted.

**Optional Accessories**:
- Items include LKWRB1316SS, LKWERBSS, LKWUCSS, LKWBG1316SS LR(Q)3322, and LKWOBG1316SS LRAD(Q)3322. 
- Cutting boards available: CB1613, CB1713, CBR1316, or CBS1316. These comply with ASME A112.19.3 standards.

**SINK DIMENSIONS**: 
- Dimensions are in inches; refer to countertop specifications including Quick-Clip corners: LR3322, LRQ3322, MR2, or LRAD3322, LRADQ3322, MR2. The design allows for an off-centered orientation.

**Modifications**: For other configurations or modifications, please consult the illustrations provided.

**ADA Compliance**: 
- This product is built to meet A.D.A. requirements, but the unobstructed knee space may not align with desired preferences when adhering to standards. 

**Mounting**: 
- Quick-Clip mounting system integration details are included.
  
**Documentation**: 
- All specifications, including hole drilling configurations, are documented and printed in the U.S.A. (IL 1-160C). All dimensions are in inches; to convert to millimeters, multiply by 25.4.
