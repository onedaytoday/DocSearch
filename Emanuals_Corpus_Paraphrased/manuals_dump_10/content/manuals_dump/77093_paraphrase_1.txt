**Introduction to the Product**
- The LCD rear-mount touchmonitor features SXGA TFT technology, ensuring high performance and compatibility with various graphics modes (VGA, SVGA, XGA, SXGA non-interlaced), in compliance with VESA DPMS standards. DDC1/2B compatibility is also supported. 

**Unpacking the Touchmonitor**
- Ensure the following items are included with the LCD touchmonitor and USB Touch supply.

**Installation and Setup Instructions**
1. Connect the touchmonitor to your PC, ensuring it is powered off.
2. Securely attach the power cord to the monitor and connect the touchscreen interface (RS232 or USB) to the rear of the unit.
3. Tighten all screws to ensure proper grounding.

**Software Installation**
- The necessary software for the touchmonitor is provided on a CD-ROM, compatible with Windows XP and NT. Follow the instructions for Plug-and-Play setup.
- For DOS systems, utilize the MOUSE.COM from the CD-ROM to configure the touchmonitor.

**Operation**
- The On-Screen Display (OSD) allows you to adjust settings such as brightness, sharpness, and positioning. Select options using dedicated buttons and navigate through the OSD menus.

**Common Troubleshooting Tips**
- If you encounter issues with the display resolution or input, ensure that the appropriate settings are selected and that connections are secure. Refer to the specifications for native resolutions and compatibility.

**Safety Precautions**
- To avoid electrical shock, always use a grounded three-wire cord when operating the touchmonitor. Clean the surface gently with a suitable cloth, avoiding any abrasive materials.

### Touchmonitor Specifications for 1739L LCD TFT

- **Model**: 1739L LCD TFT Active
- **Display Dimensions**: 338 mm (H), 270 mm (V)
- **Pixel Pitch**: 0.264 mm (H), 0.264 mm (V)
- **Supported Resolutions**: VGA, SVGA, XGA, SXGA
- **Brightness**: Typical brightness without Tf: 16.7M colors
- **Connection Type**: R.G.B. TTL or Negative

### Controls
- **On-Screen Display (OSD)**: Brightness, Phase, Clock adjustments available in English and Spanish. 
- **Touch Technology**: Optional capacitive touch support.

### Power Specifications
- **Input Voltage**: AC 100-240V
- **Operating Temperature**: 0°C to 50°C (32°F to 122°F)

### Regulatory Compliance
