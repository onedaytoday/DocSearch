**Professionally Designed Double Hot Plate Instruction Manual**

**Model DK200**

This professional-grade double hot plate is engineered for efficient cooking. Please refer to the instruction manual before first use to ensure safe operation.

**Safety Precautions:**
- Ensure the device is connected correctly to avoid electrical hazards.
- Avoid exposure to water or other liquids.
- Keep out of reach of children to prevent accidents or burns.

**Device Configuration:**
- The on/off switch controls power to both hot plates. Ensure both are turned off after use.
- When the heating process begins, an indicator light will signal that the plates are operating.

**Technical Specifications:**
- Voltage: 230V~
- Power: 1500W and 1000W settings available.
  
**Environmental Guidelines:**
- Dispose of the device responsibly.
- Follow recycling guidelines to minimize environmental impact.

**Cleaning and Maintenance:**
- Allow the plates to cool before cleaning.
- Wipe surfaces with a soft cloth. Avoid abrasive materials.
