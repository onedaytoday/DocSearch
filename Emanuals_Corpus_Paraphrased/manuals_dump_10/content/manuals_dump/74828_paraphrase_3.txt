**S90 One Specifications Overview**

Model: S90 One (WFWROFOOOC) and S90 One o (WFXROFOOOC)

**Characteristics:**
- Available in single or three-phase configurations.
- Features a heavy-duty design.
- The cleaning mechanism is located at the top, with components positioned either back-to-back or side-by-side.
- The unit includes a two-year service warranty and a one-year performance warranty from Professional, Inc. More details can be found at www.electroluxusa.com/professional.

**Design Features:**
- Rounded corners for easier cleaning.
- Internal heating elements are located at the top with a thermostatic control range. 
- Overheat protection is integrated for safety.
- The front cleaning access enhances usability.

**Options:**
- The unit can accommodate four round plates.
- Optional accessories include an insulated cover, a right portioning shelf, and closing fryers.

**Technical Information:**
- Maximum thermal capacity: MIN Top Thermostat (F C)
- Minimum height specifications must be adhered to for optimal function.
- Note that specifications are subject to change without prior notice.
