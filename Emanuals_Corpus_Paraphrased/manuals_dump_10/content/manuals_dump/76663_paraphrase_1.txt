**Power and Performance Specifications:**

The SX-A9-J amplifier delivers 70W of power and features a symmetrical, mechanically isolated design. It utilizes a Class A/B amplifier with LAPT transistors for superior performance. The construction includes a honeycomb chassis that enhances sound quality during MP3/AAC playback. 

Supported formats include USB and XM Ready* capabilities, with options for both MM/MC turntables and AM/FM radio. Note that XM Connect-and-Play and related services require separate subscriptions. 

This model is developed by Pioneer, a trusted name in audio technology, in collaboration with AIR Studios.
