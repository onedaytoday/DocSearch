**Introduction**

The 1739L Touchmonitor utilizes an LCD (Liquid Crystal Display) for optimal performance, incorporating active thin-film transistor (TFT) technology for effective SXGA graphics display. The device supports various video standards, including VGA, SVGA, XGA, and non-interlaced SXGA, complying with VESA DPMS standards.

**Installation and Setup**

This section covers how to install the LCD Touchmonitor and necessary software. Ensure that all components are included: the touchmonitor, USB cables, and the QuickStart CD.

To connect the touchmonitor to a PC, ensure the power is off. Install the USB or RS232 connection securely at the rear of the device. Tighten all screws to ensure a stable connection.

The setup software is located on the included CD-ROM, which is compatible with various operating systems including NT and MS-DOS. For troubleshooting, follow the provided installation instructions carefully.
