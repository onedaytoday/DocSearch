The theater features surround sound technology powered by the Advanced MCACC receiver, delivering a three-dimensional sound experience with DTS-HD High Resolution, DTS-96/24, and DTS-ES. It provides high-quality audio for compressed formats and includes an LCD remote for enhanced control. The system supports HDMI connectivity for A/V components and is compatible with FM/AM stations, as well as XM and SIRIUS services (each sold separately). 

Specifications include a clean construction that blends seamlessly around the TV. The use of Dolby Surround EX, along with various DTS technologies, ensures exceptional sound quality.
