The S90 Induction Island and S90 Induction Base facilitate induction cooking operations, featuring a three-phase unit designed for efficient heat management. The induction cooktop is engineered for high-capacity performance and features heavy-duty construction. When interconnected, the units form a cohesive cooking surface.
