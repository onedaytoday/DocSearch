**Dry-Erase Projection Installation Guide**  
For successful installation, please follow the instructions below. Note that to ensure durability, always use a high-quality microfiber cloth to clean the whiteboard surface. 

To maintain the integrity of the screen, avoid prolonged exposure to its factory settings. Measure the installation area carefully, marking the top-right and bottom-right corners to ensure proper alignment. Ensure the screen is fully secured in position.
