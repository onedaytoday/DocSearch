Congratulations on your purchase of the LCD mount touchmonitor. This high-resolution monitor is designed for optimal performance with an LCD interface that facilitates effective information flow through touch interactions. Follow the guidelines provided in this document to ensure proper setup and usage.

**Product Overview**
The LCD mount touchmonitor features:
- Direct RGB input supporting XGA, SVGA, VGA resolutions
- High refresh rates compliant with VESA standards
- Multiple language options for On-Screen Display (OSD) including English and Spanish
- VESA DDC 1/2B support and VESA DPMS compatibility
- Certified safety standards including UL, CUL, FCC, CE, and C-Tick compliance

**Installation and Setup**
Upon unpacking the touchmonitor, ensure you have the following components:
- LCD unit
- Installation CD
- Power supply brick

Ensure you have the necessary tools, including a hex wrench for mounting. Follow the provided instructions for securely mounting the touchmonitor to an appropriate surface while considering NEMA standards.

**Connecting the Touchmonitor**
Ensure that both the PC and touchmonitor are powered off while making connections. Connect the touchmonitor to the PC as follows:
- Attach the appropriate cables for video signals (using RS-232 as needed).
- Ensure proper alignment and secure connections as per the installation guide.

**Software Installation**
The included CD-ROM contains software that allows the touchmonitor to interface with different operating systems, including NT and MS-DOS. Follow the on-screen instructions for installation:
1. For Windows NT, execute the software from the CD and follow the prompts.
2. For MS-DOS, navigate to the CD directory and execute the INSTALL command.

**Operating the OSD**
The OSD can be accessed via the corded controls located at the back of the unit. Use these controls to navigate through settings such as brightness and positioning. Both the UP and DOWN buttons allow users to adjust display characteristics, while the AUTO button helps optimize settings automatically.

For troubleshooting or further details on connectivity and operating requirements, please refer to the relevant sections of this manual or visit www.elotouch.com for support.

### Touchmonitor Guidance and Specifications

Ensure that the touch monitor is properly connected. If issues arise, check connections and power supply based on troubleshooting steps in the relevant chapters. The LCD may display incorrectly if resolutions between the monitor and host do not match, affecting the tracking function and graphical refresh rates. 

For optimal performance, ensure compatibility between input types, such as VGA, SVGA, and XGA, as illustrated in Chapter Active Resolution A-25. Mismatched inputs can cause visual artifacts.

To maintain safety, power down the touch monitor before cleaning. Use a 3-wire grounding cord and avoid unauthorized repairs to prevent voiding your warranty. For cleaning, use a soft cloth lightly dampened, avoiding direct application of chemicals or cleaners on the touchscreen surface.

### Touchmonitor Specifications

The ET1566L model features a 15-inch active thin-film transistor (TFT) display and an AccuTouch touchscreen. It is designed to operate in a temperature range from 0°C to 40°C and is certified to various EMC standards.

**Touchscreen Performance:**
- Activation Force: Typically under 6 ounces
- Tested for over 30,000 touches without performance degradation
- Resilience against chemicals like hexane and ammonia

### Construction and Features

The CarrollTouch IR assembly consists of FR4 material, rated for safety, and utilizes a flat flexible cable (FFC) for connectivity. Designed to support multiple touchpoints and ensure durable performance, it complies with proper electrical standards.
