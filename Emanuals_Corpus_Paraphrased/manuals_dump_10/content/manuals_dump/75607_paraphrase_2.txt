# USER MANUAL ENN

## IMPORTANT SAFETY INFORMATION

This instruction manual should be kept for reference. If the appliance is transferred to another owner, or if it is moved to a new location, it is essential that the new owner understands its operation. This manual contains important safety information that must be followed. 

When using this heavy appliance, ensure it is properly connected to the power supply. Any maintenance or repairs should be carried out by authorized technicians, as improper handling can pose risks. The appliance should not be exposed to direct heat sources, and the back must be ventilated as per the instructions provided. 

This appliance is designed for household use. To prevent overheating, avoid prolonged exposure to sunlight and do not place items that can cause damage near the cooling mechanisms (such as hot food). For optimal performance and safety, avoid using sharp instruments to remove frost, and always allow ice to thaw naturally during defrosting.

When cleaning or servicing the appliance, ensure it is disconnected from the power source. Repairs should only be conducted by qualified personnel to prevent serious malfunctions. For recycling information, please contact your local waste management office or the retailer from whom the appliance was purchased.

## OPERATION

To operate the appliance effectively:
1. **Thermostat Control**: Turn the thermostat clockwise; this will switch on the power light. Ensure that the light indicates that the appliance is operational.
2. **Food Storage**: Organize food items to allow proper air circulation, and use airtight containers to maintain freshness.
3. **Defrosting**: Avoid using sharp tools to remove frost. When defrosting, allow frozen items to thaw naturally for quality maintenance.

### Cleaning and Maintenance

- Disconnect from the power supply before cleaning. 
- Use soft cloths and warm water for cleaning interiors; avoid harsh detergents or abrasive materials to prevent damage. 
- Check and replace parts as necessary, following the manufacturer's guidelines.

### TROUBLESHOOTING

### Installation Instructions

**Warning:** If discarding an old appliance with a locking door, ensure it is rendered unusable to prevent accidental trapping of children. 

Positioning recommendations specify the operating temperature range: 
- SN: +10°C to 32°C 
- N: +16°C to 32°C 
- ST: +18°C to 38°C 
- T: +18°C to 43°C 

Consider placing the appliance in a suitable environment away from heat sources or extreme cold.

**Warning:** This appliance must be earthed. Electrical connections must be made carefully. Directions include identifying wires by color: 

- Earth: Green/Yellow 
- Neutral: Blue 
- Live: Brown 

Ensure compliance with relevant E.E.C. directives.
