### Product Overview
Congratulations on your purchase of the Touchmonitor, a high-resolution display tailored for performance. The CRT touchmonitor features a microprocessor-based system with sizes of 13.8 inches, 15 inches, 15.7 inches, and 17 inches. It supports various refresh rates and offers VESA DPMS and VESA DDC1/2B compatibility. The device includes both RS-232 and USB interfaces for connectivity.

### Setup Instructions
**Unpacking and Initial Setup:**
1. Make sure the touchmonitor is positioned correctly, avoiding placement upside-down.
2. Maintain a distance of at least 6 inches from heat sources and ensure that light does not reflect directly on the screen. This helps in reducing glare and strain during use.

### Connecting the Touchmonitor
- Connect the touchmonitor to your PC using either RS-232 or USB interface, ensuring that both the monitor and PC are powered off during the connection process.
- Properly attach the AC power cord to a grounded outlet.
- For optimal functionality, ensure both the touchscreen connection and the PC connection are secure.

### Software Installation
- Follow the prompts on the TouchTools CD-ROM to install the required software for both Windows NT and other operating systems (like MS-DOS, OS/2).

### Operating the Touchmonitor
- Use the On-Screen Display (OSD) to navigate settings. Access the OSD by utilizing the MENU buttons. Here you can adjust settings such as brightness and screen alignment.

### Troubleshooting
- If you encounter issues with the touchmonitor, ensure connections are secure and check for compatibility with the graphics output.
- In the case of display distortions or improper function, consult the troubleshooting section for specific guidance on diagnosing common issues.
