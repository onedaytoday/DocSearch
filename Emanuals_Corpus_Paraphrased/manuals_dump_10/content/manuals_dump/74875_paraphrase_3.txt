Ice KG provides tailored catering solutions functioning continuously for varying hours. The setup is straightforward, featuring user-friendly supply lines and hoses, and is designed for easy installation. The AISI Food ABS material ensures it is simple to clean, while the operation remains intuitive. Maintenance access is conveniently located at the front, with cooling systems rated at 21°C for ambient conditions and a water supply temperature of 15°C. 

For technical specifications, the LEGEND CWI series includes options for electric cooling with different voltages and phases, such as the RIMC067SA, RIMC067SW, FGC65A42, RIMC085SA, RIMC085SW, FGC90A, and FGC90W, all designed for easy integration and performance.
