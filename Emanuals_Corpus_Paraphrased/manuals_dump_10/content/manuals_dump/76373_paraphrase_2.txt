The W5105N Steam Washer provides an easy selection of performance options, including high-force options with robust protection. The unit can be equipped with an optional I/O board for customization and is designed for both heated and non-heated applications. It efficiently handles hot and cold water at temperatures ranging from 15°C to 65°C, meeting ISO and IP 24D certification standards.

Operating voltage ranges include 220-240V, 380-415V 3N, 440V, and 480V. The washer accommodates different water capacities and sound levels compliant with IEC standards.
