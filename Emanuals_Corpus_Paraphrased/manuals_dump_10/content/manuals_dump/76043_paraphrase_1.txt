**READ ALL INSTRUCTIONS BEFORE USING THIS VACUUM CLEANER.** 

**WARNING:** To minimize the risk of fire, electric shock, or injury:

- Only use the vacuum cleaner as instructed and for its intended purpose.
- Do not service or charge when the unit is hot; allow it to cool.
- Keep this unit away from children and treat it as you would any electrical appliance.
- Only recharge using the SSA-135045US charger or appropriate cord.
- Avoid running the cord around heated surfaces, under carpets, or past doorways.
