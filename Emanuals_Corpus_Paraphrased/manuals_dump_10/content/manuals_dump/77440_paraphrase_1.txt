**User Manual for the HK39.IT.QXD PM Personal Stereo Cassette Player with Voice Recorder and Digital Alarm Clock**

**Model HK39**

This manual provides essential information concerning the HK39 PM Emerson Personal Stereo Cassette Player with Alarm Clock. Designed for years of reliable, hassle-free use, this device comes equipped with multiple features. For technical assistance, please contact Emerson Customer Service.

**Important Notes:**
- Avoid exposing the unit to high temperatures or prolonged sunlight, as this can cause warping or failure.
- Ensure the device is used appropriate locations.

**Control Placement:**
- Record Voice
- Record Mode
- Play
- Stop
- Fast Forward
- Voice Play
- Mic
- Volume
- Battery Compartment (located at the rear)

**Battery Operation:**
- Open the battery cover and insert three AA batteries (included).
- Follow polarity instructions indicated inside the compartment. Use new batteries to ensure effective operation. Rechargeable batteries may overheat if improperly used.

**FCC Information:**
- This device complies with FCC guidelines to minimize interference. Any interference affecting operation may be rectified.

**Clock Settings:**
To switch through clock modes, press MODE once to cycle through options. For setting the date and time:
- Press SET to display the month, then use MODE to adjust.
- Press SET again to display the hour. Use MODE to switch between AM/PM. 

**Alarm Settings:**
To set the alarm time:
- Press SET once; the display will show the hour and AM/PM.
- Activate or deactivate the alarm by pressing SET.

**Chime Settings:**
- To turn on the hourly chime, press SET followed by MODE to activate and adjust settings. Use MODE to turn it off.

**Cassette Player Usage:**
- Insert a cassette tape into the correct slot facing the appropriate direction. Use the RECORD button to begin recording; the RECORD light will illuminate.
- Adjust volume using the built-in mic and playback as needed. Press STOP to cease recording.
