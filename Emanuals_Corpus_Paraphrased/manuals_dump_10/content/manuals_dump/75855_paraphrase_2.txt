# User Manual ERN

## Important Safety Information
This instruction manual should be retained for reference. If the appliance is transferred to another owner, it's essential to convey this information regarding its operation and safety features. Users must adhere to safety guidelines when operating the appliance. This unit is heavy and should be handled according to the specifications provided. Only qualified technicians should service this appliance. Children should not be allowed to interact with it, and care should be taken to prevent overheating and frost damage. Follow all instructions regarding defrosting and cleaning, and do not use sharp tools to remove frost. Always ensure food is stored correctly to avoid frostbite and burns. 

## Operation
The appliance ON/OFF function is accessed through the control display. Adjust the thermostat as necessary to maintain ideal food storage temperatures. Various functions can be activated to optimize food preservation. Ensure food is stored on the correct shelf heights to promote air circulation. 

## Maintenance and Cleaning
Disconnect the appliance from the electrical supply before cleaning. Use mild cleaning solutions—avoid abrasive products that may damage surfaces. Regular maintenance ensures optimal performance, and any faults should be reported to authorized service personnel.

## Troubleshooting
If the appliance is not operating correctly, consult the troubleshooting section for potential issues like power supply or thermostat settings. For unresolved issues, contact Service Force directly.

## Service and Spare Parts
For service inquiries or parts purchases, please contact Service Force.

### Installation Warning

If you are disposing of an old appliance with a door lock or catch, you must render it inoperable to prevent young children from being trapped inside.

### Positioning

The appliance should be positioned in a place indicated in the provided instructions, taking into account the temperature ratings below:

- SN: +10°C to +32°C
- N: +16°C to +32°C
- ST: +18°C to +38°C
- T: +18°C to +43°C

Ensure the appliance is located away from direct sunlight and heat sources. If the ambient temperature is outside these ranges, proper adjustments must be followed. Refer to the installation instructions for built-in setups and shelf configuration.

### Electrical Warning

WARNING: THIS APPLIANCE MUST BE EARTHED. Ensure that the appliance is correctly wired for safety. Disconnect the power before any installation. The connection should include the following wire color codes:

- Earth (E): Green/Yellow
- Neutral (N): Blue
- Live (L): Brown or Red

Use a molded plug that meets BSI362, ASTA, or BSI standards. The appliance complies with relevant EEC Directives regarding electrical safety and interference.

### Reversibility

To reverse the door swing, perform the following steps:

1. Unscrew the hinges.
2. Detach the door and reposition the hinges on the opposite side.
3. Screw in the door back in place.

### Built-In Recess

Ensure that the appliance is installed according to specifications with the necessary clearances mentioned. Utilize the screws provided for securing covers (C-D) and (B) in the correct orientation as demonstrated in the instructions.

Check that all components are tightened properly, ensuring the appliance operates effectively. For more information, visit [Electrolux](http://www.electrolux.com).
