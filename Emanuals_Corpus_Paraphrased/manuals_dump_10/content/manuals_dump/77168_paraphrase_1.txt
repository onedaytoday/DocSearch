### Introduction
The Touch 1930L/1931L touchmonitor offers enhanced performance with its integrated touchscreen features. It provides high-quality display backed by a warranty. The monitor’s LED technology reduces mercury usage and features an edge-to-edge glass design for an aesthetically pleasing appearance. Please adhere to the precautions and guidelines as outlined in this user manual for optimal performance.

### Mounting Information
Included with the touchmonitor are protective covers, a regulatory information CD, VGA and DVI cables, and mounting screws. The monitor supports DVI or VGA inputs and requires a 12V DC power supply. Ensure the monitor is turned ON before use.

### Touch Software
The touchscreen software is compatible with projected capacitive designs and supports HID protocols. For additional details and software installation, visit www.elotouch.com.

### Operational Guidelines
To confirm your installation, click "Next" when prompted. Follow on-screen instructions to ensure the touchmonitor is functioning correctly. 

### On-Screen Display (OSD)
The OSD can be navigated using the touchmonitor’s buttons. Key features include:
- Brightness adjustment
- Input source selection
- Clock settings for VGA input
- Aspect ratio and sharpness adjustments

To optimize the display, adjustments can be made to positioning and scaling using the OSD. Inactive OSD will close automatically after a set period.

### Technical Support
For troubleshooting and technical assistance, refer to the specifications chapter or contact Touch Customer Service at www.elotouch.com/products or www.elotouch.com/go/contactsupport. 

### Cleaning and Maintenance
To maintain the integrity of the touchmonitor, use a soft cloth for cleaning. Do not apply any cleaners directly to the touchscreen or use abrasive materials.

### Waste Electrical and Electronic Equipment Directive (WEEE)
This product should be disposed of according to local regulations on recycling and recovery systems.
