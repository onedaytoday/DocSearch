**EL2010 User Manual**

**IMPORTANT SAFETY INSTRUCTIONS:**
Please read all instructions prior to using this vacuum cleaner. 

**WARNING:** To minimize the risk of fire, electric shock, or injury, do not mishandle the charger or any components. Ensure proper maintenance and servicing. Avoid overheating and store away from children. 

When charging, use only the specified model KSAS3R51800010VUD, and keep cords clear of edges and heated surfaces. Do not mutilate or incinerate batteries, as they may explode at high temperatures. 

SAVE THESE INSTRUCTIONS FOR HOUSEHOLD USE ONLY.

For recycling information, visit www.electrolux.com. For purchase inquiries, please see www.electroluxappliances.com (USA) or www.electrolux.ca.

---

**COMPONENTS AND ACCESSORIES:**
- Power On/Off switch
- Locking screw
- Charging station and indicator lights
- Brush and crevice tool release buttons

---

**UNPACKING AND CHECKING CONTENTS:**
Carefully remove all items from the carton. 

**CAUTION:** Ensure the device is turned off and cool before charging for the specified number of hours. Use a screwdriver or coin to tighten the locking screw. 

---

**CHARGING STATION:**
Connect the charger to the outlet, avoiding heat and sunlight. The charging indicator will illuminate when connected properly, and charging times will vary based on the model. 

---

**EMPTYING AND CLEANING:**
Regularly empty the dust container and clean filters. It's recommended to do this every time you clean. Handle release buttons with care when disassembling the unit for maintenance.

---

**TROUBLESHOOTING:**
If the vacuum cleaner cuts off, check for clogs in the hose or brush roll. Make sure to clear any blockages and allow the unit to cool before restarting.
