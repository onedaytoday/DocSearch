### Connection Details

**Caution:** Ensure your touchmonitor is connected correctly to avoid damage. The monitor can be connected via RS-232 or USB. Follow the connection procedures outlined below:

1. **Routing the Power Cable:** Guide the cable through the appropriate openings.
  
2. **Establishing Video Connections:** Locate the 15-pin video connector and link it to your PC, securing it with screws.

3. **Connecting Touchscreen Cables:** Use a 9-pin serial connection or USB cable to establish communication. Ensure all connections are tight.

4. **Additional Connections:** Hook up audio speakers via their designated port.

5. **Powering the Device:** Connect the AC power adapter, ensuring it is grounded.

### VESA Mounting Interface

The monitor features a VESA Mounting Interface compliant with FPMPMI standards allowing for flexible installations. To access this interface:

1. Remove the top cover screw.
2. Unscrew and detach the mounting cover to reveal the interface.
3. Secure the touchmonitor using appropriate VESA-compatible mounts.

### OSD Functionality

The On-Screen Display (OSD) controls brightness, contrast, and sharpness. Use the OSD buttons to navigate left/right or up/down and access factory settings. Select the "USER" option to customize settings individually. You can adjust the Red/Green/Blue (RGB) color channels from the OSD menu. The OSD can also handle various settings like phase and clock adjustments.
