**IMPORTANT SAFETY INSTRUCTIONS**
These guidelines ensure safe use and protection for users. If you have questions about these instructions, please consult a qualified technician. Ensure thorough adherence to the specified guidelines regarding installation and usage. Be cautious of heat exposure and ensure proper ventilation around the appliance. Prioritize safety by unplugging the unit before any maintenance work. Protect children from accessing the appliance. 

**Appliance Description**
The refrigerator features adjustable shelves and a thermostat for temperature control. Ensure proper usage to maintain optimal food safety and device efficiency.

**Operational Guidelines**
Adjust the thermostat based on the type of food stored and avoid frequent temperature changes. The cooling mechanism operates more efficiently when food items are organized correctly. Utilize airtight storage methods for meat and other perishable items to maintain freshness.

**Normal Sounds**
Listen for standard operational sounds such as whirring or clicking, which indicate that the thermostat and cooling system are functioning correctly.

**HEALTH AND SAFETY GUIDELINES**
Keep food properly stored and organized to prevent contamination. Regularly clean the appliance and avoid overcrowding to allow proper air circulation. Follow thawing guidelines to ensure food safety. Ensure that all food is stored in appropriate containers to maintain quality.

**DEFROSTING INSTRUCTIONS**
Defrost the appliance when frost buildup exceeds recommended levels. Follow the guidelines to safely disconnect power and carefully remove frost buildup. Maintain cleanliness during this process to ensure continued efficiency.
