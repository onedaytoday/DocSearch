### IMPORTANT SAFEGUARDS 
Before using this wine cooler, ensure you read all instructions thoroughly. 

- **Safety Precautions:** Ensure that young children are kept away from the cooler. It is crucial to clean the cooler only with the specified products. 
- **Installation:** Position the cooler with adequate clearance on all sides to facilitate airflow and heat dissipation. Maintain at least an inch of clearance on the top and back. Shield it from direct heat or sunlight to enhance cooling efficiency.
- **Usage and Handling:** This cooler is intended solely for household use. Always unplug before cleaning or moving the unit. Remove all bottles prior to relocating the cooler to avoid damage.

### Operating Instructions
- **Temperature Settings:** The cooler operates optimally at 77°F (25°C). Adjust the temperature settings between 54°F (12°C) and 66°F (19°C) for various types of wine as follows: 
  - White/Rosé: 46-54°F (8-12°C) 
  - Port/Sherry: 50-57°F (10-14°C)
- **Cooling Efficiency:** The unit performs best when not placed near sources of heat like dishwashers or microwaves. A built-in or recessed installation is preferred but ensures a minimum clearance of inches around it.

### Cleaning Instructions
When cleaning the cooler:
- Use a soft cloth to avoid damaging the finish. Never use abrasive cleaners or scouring pads, which can permanently mar the surface. 
- Regular maintenance is recommended to prevent cooling inefficiency.

### Troubleshooting Guide
If experiencing issues, verify the following:
- Ensure the unit is plugged in and the outlet is operational.
- Maintain temperature settings between 54°F (12°C) and 66°F (19°C).
- Ensure that the cooling vents are not obstructed by at least 1 inch of clearance around the unit.
If problems persist, consult a qualified technician.
