**Specifications for LKD2423 Series**

The LKD2423 is a top mount fixture designed to meet U.S. specifications. It operates within an I.P. range and features robust construction. The model LKD2423BH shares these specifications but includes a different countertop thickness. Compliance certifications include ASME A112.18.1M, CSA B125-01, NSF/ANSI, and ADA PC.
