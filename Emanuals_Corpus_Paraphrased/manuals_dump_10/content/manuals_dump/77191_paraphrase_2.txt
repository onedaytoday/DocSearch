### Power Supply
- **Voltage**: 230V~ 
- **Battery Backup**: 9V DC

### Radio Operation
- To operate the radio, turn the function selector to ON. Set the tuning control to select the desired station, and set the function selector to OFF when finished. 

### Clock Setting
- To set the clock, press the time setting buttons until the hour is displayed, then release. Repeat for minutes.

### Wake-Up Options
- Choose to wake with radio or buzzer by adjusting the alarm settings and tuning the alarm to your preference. 

### Snooze Function
- The snooze button allows for temporary silence of alarms, repeating at intervals until deactivated or turned off.

### Sleep Timer
- Activate the sleep timer by selecting the preferred station and pressing the sleep button to configure the timer settings.

### USB Playback
- You can play MP3 files from a USB device by connecting it to the USB port and using the playback controls.
