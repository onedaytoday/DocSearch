**Unpacking the Touchmonitor:** Ensure the following items are included in your package:
- LCD Touchmonitor

**Mounting Instructions:** Use the appropriate hex wrench and screws to securely mount the touchmonitor. Refer to the provided diagrams for precise installation steps.

### Connecting the Touchmonitor
Before making any connections, ensure both the touchmonitor and your PC are powered off. Follow the steps below:

1. **Touchscreen Connection:**
   - Connect the touchscreen cable (RS-232) to the PC.
   - Securely fasten all connections.

2. **Power Supply Connection:**
   - Connect the power supply to the touchmonitor. Use a grounded AC outlet.
   - Toggle the power switch on the back of the touchmonitor to turn it on.

3. **Software Installation:** 
   - Install the software necessary for your operating system (Windows NT, MS-DOS, etc.) by following the prompts on the CD-ROM. Additional details for other operating systems can be accessed via the manufacturer's website.

### Operating Instructions
**Using the On-Screen Display (OSD):**
- The OSD allows you to adjust settings such as brightness and contrast easily.
- Utilize the selection buttons to navigate through OSD options.
- For best results, follow the on-screen prompts to adjust options like sharpness and screen position.
