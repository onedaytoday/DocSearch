**Universal Mount A56-E25B2 Product Overview**

The A56-E25B2 mount offers versatile solutions for both low and high short-throw installations. It provides adjustable height and horizontal positioning to accommodate a range of setups. For installation specifications, visit www.elitescreens.com.

**Mounting Details**

This mount utilizes M5 screws, compatible with various mounting surfaces. Ensure that the screws are properly oriented for secure attachment. The adjustable design allows flexibility in positioning, either flush against the wall or extended outward.

**Assembly Instructions**

The mounting process involves using optional M6 threaded screws for LCD/DLP applications. Ensure that all screws and washers are securely fastened during assembly. The swivel head allows for easy adjustments to angle and orientation.
