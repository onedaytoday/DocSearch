**S90 Char-Broiler Overview**

The S90 Char-Broiler (Model WDGUAAOOOO) is designed for optimal efficiency with high BTU output. This unit features easy cleaning operations and includes grease management systems and high splash guards to maintain hygiene standards. The robust construction allows for effective heat distribution, with interconnecting elements that ensure stability.

It comes with a two-year warranty on parts and a one-year warranty on labor, provided by Professional, Inc. More details can be found at www.electroluxusa.com/professional. 

The unit allows for versatile cooking setups with an easily removable thermocouple and offers options like a round plinth and a right portioning shelf for customized configurations. 

For installation, consider the specified dimensions: height, depth, and top height, and ensure proper heat insulation as needed. Electrolux reserves the right to modify product specifications without prior notice.
