### SANTO TK Operating Instructions

Dear Customer,

Please refer to these operating instructions for important information regarding your appliance. These guidelines contain crucial details concerning operation, maintenance, and troubleshooting. 

**General Information:**
- Make sure to read the operating instructions thoroughly before use. 
- Follow all safety warnings highlighted throughout the document.

**Installation and Setup:**
- Ensure proper mounting of the appliance according to the provided instructions.
- Maintain adequate space around the appliance for optimal airflow and performance. Avoid exposure to direct sunlight or heat sources.

**Defrosting and Maintenance:**
- Follow the defrosting guidelines carefully.
- Clean the appliance regularly as described in the cleaning section to prevent malfunctions.

**Troubleshooting:**
- If you experience any issues, please check the “What to Do” section before attempting repairs.
- For severe malfunctions, avoid trying to fix them yourself and contact a qualified service technician.

**Safety Warnings:**
- Ensure the appliance is earthed properly to prevent electrical shock. 
- Use appropriate electrical ratings as specified in the manual. 

**Starting the Appliance:**
- Adjust the temperature settings appropriately based on the desired cooling level. Refer to the section on temperature settings for guidance.

**Storage Recommendations:**
- Keep food items covered or packed to maintain freshness and prevent contamination. 
- Pay attention to the arrangement of food within the appliance to promote efficient cooling.

**Cleaning Instructions:**
- Disconnect the appliance from the power source before cleaning. Use suitable cleaning agents and avoid harsh chemicals that may damage the surface.

**Contact Information:**
- For further assistance, reach out to AEG Service Force. They can assist with any persistent issues or repairs.

Thank you for choosing our appliance. We wish you a smooth experience!

**Warning: Electrical Hazard!**

Be cautious around the water and shelves. Immediately turn off the light switch to prevent electric shocks. If the light needs to be switched off, use the provided screw to secure the light cover as shown. Press the light cover properly and ensure it's secured at the back. 

**Appliance Symptoms:**
Touching the appliance may result in electric shocks, especially if water is present. Cleaning should be done carefully, following the guidelines. 

In case of mechanical failure or breakdown, we advise contacting AEG Service Force. Customers should be informed about any potential charges for service. For assistance, please communicate via writing or phone, providing the product purchase details and any required documentation.

**Important Features:**
A clicking sound may be audible when the switches are operated. If splashing sounds occur, this is normal during operation. These appliances comply with existing household safety regulations, including GSG, VBG, and VDE standards, as well as EU directives 73/23/EWG and 89/336/EWG (including amendment 92/31/EWG).

**Technical Information:**
Cooling is achieved through heat transfer from food or liquids, requiring the appliance’s insulation. Heat is dissipated to the surrounding environment. The unit is designed for built-in installation at the back, ensuring efficient heat management to safeguard surrounding areas.
