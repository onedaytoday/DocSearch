**Specifications for Wall-Mounted Three Station with Battery: EWMA7220SBC, EWMA7220SBMC, and EWMA7220SBTMC**

**General Description:**
The EWMA7220 series features a three-station design with coved corners and a high backsplash. The corners are exposed for finishing, and each unit is equipped with a specific model of mixing faucet and drain valve. 

- **Models:**
  - EWMA7220SBC: Comes with LK722B faucet and LK18B drain; no additional options.
  - EWMA7220SBMC: Includes LK722B faucet, LK18B drain, and LK724.
  - EWMA7220SBTMC: Features LK722B faucet, LK18B drain, and LK723.

**Operation:**
These faucets are sensor-operated and mounted on the backsplash for automatic activation by the user. Each model operates on battery power, using AA batteries. The faucet has a chrome finish from the top down to the base, ensuring durability.
