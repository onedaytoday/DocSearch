The Ice KG is designed for catering use, offering flexible operation with the capability to function continuously or intermittently. It is straightforward to install, featuring easy connections for water supply and hoses. Cleaning is facilitated by its AISI food-grade stainless steel and ABS materials, ensuring a hygienic operation. Maintenance is simplified with accessible components from the front. The unit is environmentally friendly, operating efficiently at a rated temperature of 21°C with a water supply of 15°C.

**Model Specifications:**
- CWI Cold Water EI
- Electric supply: 1N, V phases
- Models: RIMG094SA, RIMG094SW, RIMG150SA, RIMG150SW

These models are designed for effective water cooling without operational complications.
