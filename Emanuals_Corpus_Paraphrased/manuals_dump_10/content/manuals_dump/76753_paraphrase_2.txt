### GENERAL SPECIFICATIONS

This countertop beverage fountain is constructed from polished type stainless steel. It features a contoured splash guard and is designed to securely mount to the countertop. It adheres to a NO LEAD DESIGN, ensuring compliance with the lead-free standards outlined in the Safe Drinking Water Act and the Lead Contamination Control Act. The LK142 model is entirely lead-free and meets the ANSI/NSF61 Section guidelines.

The DRKR14C model includes a cutout dimension of IL CL DIA that accommodates countertop thickness, with IPS supply and waste fittings. 

**INSTALLER INSTRUCTIONS**  
This drinking fountain comes complete with a bubbler and valve, including all connecting fittings, all made from lead-free materials. It is recommended that the supplied shut-off valve and supply tubing be non-metallic.

#### INSTALLATION  
1. Make the necessary cutout in the desired location on the countertop.
2. Apply caulk (not supplied) around the cutout and waste opening.
3. Position the fountain over the opening and insert the strainer.
4. Use screws and nuts to secure the unit to the countertop, wiping away any excess caulk.
