**Specifications for EFS Models EFSBP25, EFSSP23, EFSBP33, and EFSSP21: General Overview**

This document pertains to high-back and/or EFS models. Each unit is supplied with screw heads. Ensure the models specified are checked: EFSBP25 (EFS2523C), EFSSP23 (with EFSBP25, EFS2523C*), EFSBP33 (EFS3321C), and EFSSP21 (with EFSBP33, EFS3321C*). The recessed dimensions as well as details for wall hangers (IL and turndown) are also included. A side view is provided for EFSSP21 and EFSBP33. Part number for reference: EFSSP21 C. Printed in the USA.
