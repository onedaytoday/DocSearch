### Specifications

**General Description**:  
Stainless steel type high backsplash countertop with an integrated top. The surface is sloped for drainage and finished with a polished appearance. The board thickness is LK251 O.D. All specified locations should be marked as non-shaded unless otherwise indicated. Drainboards are included, along with the specified fittings.

**Supply and Drain Details**:  
Fittings specified include drain and supply connections, all with LK251 O.D. Please indicate the number of faucet holes required when placing your order. Non-standard faucet and overflow drilling options are available; please specify on the accompanying drawing.

**Dimensions**:  
All measurements are provided in inches. For millimeter dimensions, refer to the other side. 

**Corner Compartment Models**:  
- **No Drainboard**: SS8230, SS8236, SS8242, SS8248, SS8254, SS8260, SSC8200  
- **Right Drainboard**: SS8230R, SS8236R, SS8248R, SS8254R, SS8260R, SSC8200R  
- **Left Drainboard**: SS8230L, SS8236L, SS8248L, SS8254L, SS8260L, SSC8200L  
- **Right and Left Drainboard**: SS8230LR, SS8236LR, SS8248LR, SS8254LR, SS8260LR, SSC8200LR  

**Additional Features**:  
DIA. CSK holes are specified for necessary fittings. All manufacturing is completed in the U.S.A.
