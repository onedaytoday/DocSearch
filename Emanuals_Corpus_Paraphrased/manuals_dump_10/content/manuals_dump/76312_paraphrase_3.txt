**ECO Reduction**  
To deactivate the option, select the appropriate button to switch it to 'O'. Important: Choose either Prewash or another specified option. Select from the available washing programs such as Normal, Synthetics, etc. 

When sorting laundry, adhere to temperature guidelines for different fabric types. For example, use Cold for Synthetics, and specific temperatures for items based on their color and fabric composition. Specific washing instructions are provided for items like heavily soiled household linens at 90°C, while lighter colored items should be washed at lower temperatures such as 60°C or 40°C. 

Follow the indications on detergent types for items such as grease and lipstick stains, and ensure you select the correct options for effective cleaning.  

**Technical Guidance for Unpacking and Positioning**  
Ensure the water hose is correctly connected to the appropriate branch. The hose should be securely positioned to facilitate efficient operation of the machine. Refer to the manufacturer's instructions for proper installation and setup.  

**Note:** Make sure to adjust the height of the hose for optimal performance.
