### Specifications Overview

**General Features**  
The product is available in three top mount thicknesses and heights, designed for supply with IPS fittings. The warranty covers both straight and LKS/LKX options, available in the following finishes: Brushed LKS6475NK, LKX6475NK; Chrome LKS6475CR, LKX6475CR. The flow rates are 9.5 GPM at 60 PSI and 8.3 GPM at 60 PSI, per manufacturer recommendations for compatibility with countertops.

**Codes/Standards**  
The product adheres to ASME/ANSI A112.18.1, ANSI/NSF, IAPMO, UPC, and ADA standards.

**Warranty**  
Elkay provides a limited lifetime warranty to the purchaser against defects in materials and workmanship. This warranty remains valid as long as the original purchaser holds ownership of the faucet. In the event of a defect, the warranty covers repair or replacement at Elkay's discretion, excluding claims due to misuse, damage from harsh cleaners, or modifications. 

**Other Warranty Conditions**  
The warranty covers only residential faucets, excluding commercial applications. It does not account for wear and tear, improper installation, or cleaning practices. Additionally, it excludes indirect charges related to the faucet or its installation.

**To Obtain Warranty Service**  
For warranty claims, customers should contact Consumer Services at Elkay and provide details such as proof of purchase and faucet model (LKS6475 and LKX6475). 

**Exclusions**  
This warranty is in lieu of all other warranties, either expressed or implied, including but not limited to implied warranties of merchantability or fitness for a particular purpose. Please be aware that certain rights may vary by jurisdiction.
