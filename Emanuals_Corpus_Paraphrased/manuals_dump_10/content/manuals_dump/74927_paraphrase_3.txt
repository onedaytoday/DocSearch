CF4 Features:

The CF4 offers a variety of functionalities designed specifically for chip preparation, showcasing its polished cast-iron construction that enhances both cleaning and shredding processes. Its smooth surface facilitates easy maintenance, while its suction capability (available as an option) minimizes the effort needed for operation.
