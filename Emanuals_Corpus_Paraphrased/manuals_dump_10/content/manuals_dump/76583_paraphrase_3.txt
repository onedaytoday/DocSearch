### Connecting LCD TV to A/V Devices

To achieve the best picture quality with your LCD TV, connect your A/V devices, such as DVD players, using the HDMI input. This will enable you to experience stunning High Definition visuals. Be sure to follow the instructions provided for connecting HD devices through HDMI. 

Regarding other connection options, please refer to the following inputs available on your TV: HDMI, VGA (PC only), S-Video, Composite, and Component connections. For more detailed information, visit www.elementelectronics.com.

Included in the package are the power cord, various cables, a remote control, AAA batteries, and a basic RF cable for antenna connection. Additional audio inputs include Coaxial and VHF/UHF.
