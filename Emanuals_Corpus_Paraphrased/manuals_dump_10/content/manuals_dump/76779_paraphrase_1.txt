### Specifications Overview

**General**: Designed for maximum protection, this sink features a robust construction and an aesthetically pleasing finish.

**Design Features**: 
- **Depth**: Constructed to accommodate exposed installations, ensuring durability and sound dampening.

**Notes**: The sink is designed for easy integration into countertops. 

**Optional Accessories**: 
- Sink models: LKRB1611, LKRB1614, LKRB715, LKERBSS, LKOBG1814, and LKOBG1613.

This product complies with standard ANSI A112.19.3M, as recognized by the International Association of Plumbing and Mechanical Officials according to the Uniform Plumbing Code.
