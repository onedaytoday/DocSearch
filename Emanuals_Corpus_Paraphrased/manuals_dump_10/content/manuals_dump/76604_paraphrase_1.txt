### DTT TV Datasheet

The DTT TV (DVB-T) offers seamless connectivity via USB, enabling users to effortlessly access a variety of options and software. It allows for fast TV viewing and recording, with the capability to create playlists and store hours of content. Users can easily transfer recordings to devices such as iPhones or Apple TVs. The package includes TV software on CD-ROM, along with instructions for owners of G4, G5, or Core systems with a minimum of 1GB of physical RAM. 

The device supports both VHF and UHF frequencies and utilizes DVB-T COFDM technology, including QAM and QPSK for optimal performance. Additional technical specifications cover USB input (IEC) and IR capabilities.
