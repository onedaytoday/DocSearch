### LAVAMAT User Information

Dear Customer,

Please carefully review the following user instructions related to the LAVAMAT appliance. This includes essential information, warnings, and guidelines that are crucial for safe and efficient operation.

### Important Instructions

1. **Operating the Appliance:**
   - Begin by reading the initial setup instructions.
   - For selecting the desired wash cycle, speed, and options like Rinse Time Saver, Pre-Wash, or Stain treatment, follow the on-screen prompts for guidance.
   - Ensure that the child lock feature is activated for safety.

2. **Maintenance and Troubleshooting:**
   - Should you encounter any issues, refer to the section on remedies for common problems.
   - Technical support and service information are provided for transport and connection to the water supply and drainage systems.

3. **Safety Precautions:**
   - Always supervise children around the appliance to prevent risks of suffocation or injury.
   - Ensure the appliance is turned off before cleaning or performing maintenance.
   - Address any overheating or electrical hazards promptly.

### Features and Settings

- The appliance includes various settings tailored for different fabric types such as Energy Saving, Cottons/Linen, Easy-Cares, Delicates, and Wool/Silk.
- The Pre-Wash and Stain options offer flexible solutions depending on your laundry needs.
  
### Additional Guidelines

- Follow proper sorting of laundry by color and fabric type to optimize cleaning results.
- Be cautious of the load size; reduce it if necessary based on the wash cycle requirements indicated in the user manual.
- The appliance will indicate when to start/pause, and various indicators will help you track the cycle status.

### Troubleshooting Messages

- If an error code appears (e.g., E10 for water inflow issues, E20 for outflow issues), pause the cycle and consult the troubleshooting section to resolve the matter.
- Ensure hoses are correctly connected and not kinks or blockages are present.

### Cleaning and Maintenance

- Regularly clean the appliance's components as described in the manual.
- Use appropriate cleaning products, ensuring not to damage any surfaces or mechanisms.

For any further assistance, please contact customer service as necessary. Thank you for choosing LAVAMAT.
