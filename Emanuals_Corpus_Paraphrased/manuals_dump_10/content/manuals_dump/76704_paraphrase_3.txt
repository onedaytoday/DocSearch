**Short-Throw Whiteboard Installation Instructions**

For the installation of the Universal Short-Throw Whiteboard Screen, please follow the included guidelines carefully. Use the M6 screws and washers as shown in the provided diagrams.

1. **Assembly Steps**:
   - Attach the Top Upright Bracket to the screen using M6 screws and washers.
   - Ensure that the orientation is correct, with the back of the bracket facing the wall.
   - Proceed to secure the Upright Bracket in place using the remaining screws and washers.
