**LCD Models: MP-700E and MP-50E**

The MP-700E and MP-50E are designed for versatile media usage, including documents and photographs, and feature advanced specifications.

**MP-700E Features:**
- **CCD ANSI native resolution:** XGA, compatible with UXGA
- **Optical Correction and Rotation:** 6.25X
- **Display Type:** 1.3-inch TFT LCD
- **Aspect Ratio:** XGA
- **Light Source:** 300W super high-pressure mercury lamp
- **Maximum Screen Size:** Various formats
- **Sound:** 1W
- **Power:** 220-240 VAC

**MP-50E Features:**
- **CCD ANSI native resolution:** XGA, compatible with UXGA
- **Optical Correction and Rotation:** 6.25X
- **Display Type:** 1.0-inch TFT LCD
- **Aspect Ratio:** Similar to XGA
- **Light Source:** 260W super high-pressure mercury lamp
- **Maximum Screen Size:** Various formats
- **Sound:** 2W
- **Power:** 220-240 VAC

**Dimensions:**
- **MP-700E:** 400mm (W) x 315mm (D)
- **MP-50E:** 390mm (W) x 380mm (D)
