**Service ESS20070 Specifications**

**General Description:** The ESS20070 type service unit features coved corners and a thick backsplash. It is designed to meet the requirements of MIL-S-2143B for shipboard applications. 

**Construction Details:** The unit has rolled rim front ends and includes a two-hole bracket plate with angle iron supporting brackets, both of which are finished with paint. Two loose brackets are provided with the unit. 

**Note:** Refer to the reverse side for rough-in dimensions.

**Rough-In Dimensions:** The floor line features slots for wall hangers with a specified diameter for installation. 

**Manufactured in the U.S.A.**
