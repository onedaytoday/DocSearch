**S90 Char-Broiler Overview**

The S90 Char-Broiler (Model WDGRAFOOOO) is designed for high efficiency, featuring a BTU rating that ensures ease of cleaning. It allows for versatile cooking of meats, with the option for back-to-back installation. The unit includes grease management features and is constructed with durable, high-grade materials. Its heavy-duty internal design and top connection optimize performance. 

The S90 comes with a two-year service warranty and a one-year performance guarantee from Professional, Inc. For more details, visit www.electroluxusa.com/professional or contact SW Telephone. 

The features include multiple branches and a protective system that facilitates easy cleaning. It also contains a thermocouple for more effective temperature control. Optional accessories include a right portioning shelf and a round plinth for stability. 

For LPG configurations, please refer to specified information regarding the cavity depth and heat insulation requirements. Electrolux reserves the right to alter product specifications without prior notice.
