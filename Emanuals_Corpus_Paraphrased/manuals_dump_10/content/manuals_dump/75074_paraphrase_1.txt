**Installation Instructions for Microwave Oven**

This document contains essential instructions for inspectors and coordinators regarding the installation of the microwave oven. It is imperative that these guidelines be followed in conjunction with consumer guidance for mounting the unit.

1. **Overview of Installation**:
   - Prior to installation, carefully read all instructions and ensure that you understand the mechanical and electrical requirements.
   - Mount the microwave as indicated, ensuring a minimum clearance from the backsplash.

2. **Electrical Grounding Requirements**:
   - The microwave must be properly grounded adhering to national standards to minimize the risk of electrical shock.
   - **Warning**: Under no circumstances should the grounding pin on the plug be removed. Avoid using an extension cord. Ensure that the power supply is directly connected to the microwave.

3. **Installation Precautions**:
   - If the microwave interferes with other appliances like televisions, reposition it in a way that minimizes this issue.
   - Securely install ductwork as required, using appropriate adapters for round to rectangular transitions.

4. **Hardware and Assembly Instructions**:
   - Gather all installation hardware necessary for mounting. Items include screws, washers, and a cushion assembly.
   - Follow the provided diagrams for mounting, ensuring proper alignment with corresponding wall templates.

5. **Assembly of Recirculating Charcoal Filter**:
   - If using a non-vented installation, ensure the charcoal filter is correctly installed. The filter requires replacement every few months.

6. **Finalizing the Installation**:
   - Ensure that all components are tightly fitted and that the microwave is securely attached to the wall and cabinets.
   - After completing the installation, double-check all connections and refer back to the mounting templates for accuracy.

7. **Note**: This microwave cannot be installed properly without reviewing the provided templates. Make sure to follow all instructions closely for safe and effective installation. 

By adhering to these guidelines, you will ensure a compliant and efficient setup of the microwave unit.
