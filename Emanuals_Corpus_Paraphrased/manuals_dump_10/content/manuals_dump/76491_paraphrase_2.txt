The double-sided activity outlined in LER aims to enhance learning through a hands-on approach, focusing on money recognition, addition, and subtraction. It encourages participants to secure cards related to monetary values, where players engage with various amounts, including units, tens, and hundreds.

Players begin by drawing cards and must track their outcomes to improve their math skills. Activity attributes are covered, emphasizing correct numerical identification and practical application of math concepts. Participants take turns, with each having opportunities to count and compare values represented on the drawn cards.

As players navigate through the activity, they consolidate their understanding of these topics, reinforcing their ability to read and interpret the values while fostering an engaging and interactive learning environment. It's essential for players to connect the attributes of the items drawn with their corresponding numerical values to succeed in the game.
