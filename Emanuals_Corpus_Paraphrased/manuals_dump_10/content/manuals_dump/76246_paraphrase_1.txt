**LAVAMAT User Guide Overview**

Dear Customer,

This document provides essential information about your appliance. Please adhere to the following warnings and instructions, as they pertain to safety and optimal appliance function.

### Important Instructions

- **Initial Setup**: Before using the appliance, switch it on and select the desired program and spin speed. Pay attention to options such as RINSE TIME SAVER, PRE WASH, and STAIN REMOVER for enhanced cleaning.

- **Child Safety**: Prevent children from accessing the machine, as there is a risk of suffocation or injury. Always supervise children around the appliance.

### Maintenance and Care

- **Transporting the Appliance**: When moving the unit, follow the guidelines for disconnecting it from water supply and drainage systems. Ensure all hoses are secure to prevent leaks.

- **Wiring and Power Supply**: For safety, ensure the appliance is switched off at the socket before performing any maintenance. If you experience any issues, disconnect the power to avoid the risk of electric shock or fire.

### Program Features

- **Wash Cycles**: Various cycles are available for different fabric types, including COTTONS/LINEN, EASY-CARE, and DELICATES. The appliance is capable of cleaning a range of garments from lightly to heavily soiled.

- **Rinse Hold Function**: This feature pauses the cycle before the rinse stage, allowing you to manage your laundry timing more flexibly.

### Troubleshooting

- **Error Codes**: If you encounter error messages such as E10 (water inflow issues), E20 (drainage problems), or E40 (door problems), refer to the troubleshooting section of the manual for solutions. Common steps include ensuring the hoses are not kinked or clogged.

- **Cleaning**: Regularly clean the appliance exterior and door seal with a damp cloth. Ensure that the washing compartment is free from residues.

### Use of Additives

- Use recommended washing and fabric care products according to the manufacturer's instructions to avoid buildup or residue on your garments. 

If issues persist or further assistance is needed, please contact authorized service personnel. Ensure you adhere to all safety protocols when operating or servicing the appliance.

Thank you for choosing LAVAMAT. We wish you a smooth laundry experience.

**Maintenance Instructions**

This appliance is designed to be maintenance-free. If you encounter a malfunction, specifically related to the cover or blockages, please ensure that all items are washed accordingly. The housing is equipped with a protective cover. **Warning:** Turn off the switch before proceeding. Start by unscrewing the cover by turning it clockwise to remove it from the housing. Check the wheel at the back; if it rotates during removal, please contact Service Force for assistance.
