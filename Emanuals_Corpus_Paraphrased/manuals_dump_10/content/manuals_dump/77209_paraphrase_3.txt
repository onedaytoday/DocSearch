MINI FAN INSTRUCTION MANUAL

This instruction manual outlines the operation and maintenance of the mini fan. It covers safety precautions, cleaning and care tips, and product specifications. Ensure you thoroughly read these instructions before first use.

**Safety Precautions:**
- Ensure the fan is switched off before any maintenance or cleaning.
- Do not attempt repairs if you lack the necessary skills.
- Keep the fan out of reach of children to prevent accidents.

**Operation:**
- The fan operates via a USB connection or with LR6/R6/AA batteries.
- To turn the fan on or off, use the designated ON/OFF switch.
- Allow the fan to run for a few seconds after powering on to ensure proper functionality.

**Cleaning and Care:**
- Use a soft cloth for cleaning the fan. Avoid abrasive cleaners and scrubbing.
- If cleaning is necessary, unplug the fan and wipe with a dry cloth, especially on the exterior surfaces.

**Technical Specifications:**
- Battery Type: LR6/R6/AA.
- USB Compatibility: Ensure proper connection before powering on.

**Warranty and Customer Service:**
- The fan comes with a warranty covering defects in materials or workmanship. Refer to the manual for details on claims.

Be sure to keep this manual for future reference.
