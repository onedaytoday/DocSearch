### SAFETY INFORMATION

This manual should be kept for reference, especially if passed to another user. It is vital to maintain the safe operation of your appliance to prevent hazards, including potential dangers to children. Children are not permitted to play with or operate the appliance without supervision.

Take caution when handling frost, as it can cause frostbite. Avoid exposure to direct sunlight. Ensure proper ventilation by keeping the back of the unit clear of obstructions for adequate airflow. The appliance must be serviced only by authorized technicians. Unauthorized repairs can result in serious malfunction.

### INSTALLATION
**Caution**: If you are discarding an old appliance with a door lock or catch, ensure it is rendered unusable to prevent young children from getting trapped inside. 

**Positioning Instructions**: Choose a location based on the following temperature ranges:
- SN: +10°C to 32°C
- ST: +18°C to 38°C
- T: +18°C to 43°C

The appliance should be placed in shaded areas away from direct sunlight. Avoid locations below 10°C (50°F). Ensure there is sufficient airflow around the back and top, aiming for at least the recommended space.

**Installation Notes**: 
- Do not obstruct the appliance with the screws or fixtures.
- Ensure the power supply is accessible and correctly connected.

**Wiring Requirements**: 
- The appliance must be grounded. Connect the wires according to the following color codes:
  - Earth (E): Green/Yellow
  - Neutral (N): Blue
  - Live (L): Brown/Red

Use appropriate molded plugs conforming to BSI362 ASTA or BSI standards.

This product complies with E.E.C. Directives related to electrical interference.

**Reversible Door Installation**:
To reverse the door, follow the specified steps carefully. Unscrew the necessary screws, remove the relevant covers, and reassemble accordingly.
