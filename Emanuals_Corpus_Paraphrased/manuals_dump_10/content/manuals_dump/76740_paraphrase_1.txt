**SPECIFICATIONS**

**General Information**  
Celebrity products feature an integrated design with right coved corners and a recessed area beneath to enhance the exposed polished finish and acoustic properties. Unless otherwise specified, items are provided with four legs and comply with ANSI A112.19.3M standards. This information aligns with guidelines from the International Association of Plumbing and Mechanical Officials (IAPMO) and the Uniform Plumbing Code (UPC).

**Sink Dimensions (Inches)*
