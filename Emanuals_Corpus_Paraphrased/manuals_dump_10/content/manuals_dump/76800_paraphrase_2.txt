### Specifications Overview

**Type:** Self-rimming, models ILGR6022LC and ILGR6022RC feature a highlighted finish with a protected heavy-duty sound coating.

**Note:** Products are provided as illustrated unless otherwise indicated.

**Optional Accessories:** Available boards include CB816, CB1613, CB1713, CBR1316, CBR1418, CBS1316, CBS1418, CBS715, as well as sinks LKRB1611, LKRB1614, LKRB715, and LKERBSS, and models LKOBG1814 or LKOBG1613. These comply with ANSI A112.19.3M standards set by the International Association of Plumbing and Mechanical Officials per the Uniform Plumbing Code.

**Sink Dimensions (in inches):** 
- Models ILGR6022L or ILGR6022R or ILGR6022LC and ILGR6022RC.
- Dimensions range from front to back with standardized specifications to ensure compatibility with various producers. 

**Product Dimensions:** Refer to the reverse side for detailed measurements. All dimensions are in inches; for conversion to millimeters, multiply by 25.4.

**Drilling Configurations:**
- Model variations include ILGR6022L1, ILGR6022L2, ILGR6022L3, ILGR6022L4, ILGR6022L5, ILGR6022R1, ILGR6022R2, ILGR6022R3, ILGR6022R4, and ILGR6022R5.
