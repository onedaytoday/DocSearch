### Hooded Operation Manual

**English Version**

This manual provides information on Hooded Softboxes and their operation without the front cover. Please refer to the guidelines and specifications from Elinchrom S.A. (LTD) available at www.elinchrom.com to understand the usage and features of the Hooded Softboxes, including Softbox Octa types and other related models. 

For further details, visit Elinchrom S.A. (LTD) at www.elinchrom.com.
