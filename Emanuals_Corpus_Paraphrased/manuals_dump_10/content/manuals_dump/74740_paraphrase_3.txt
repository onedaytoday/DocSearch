# EUF User Manual

## Important Safety Information
Please keep this manual for future reference. If the appliance changes ownership or is relocated, ensure the new owner is familiar with its operation and safety guidelines. This information is critical before using or servicing the appliance. Due to its weight, exercise caution during installation and ensure adherence to the specifications to maintain power supply integrity. The device should be kept away from heat sources and direct sunlight.

Allow adequate time (a few hours) for the unit to stabilize after installation to ensure proper cooling and recharging by trained technicians. Avoid using sharp utensils to remove frost or force components apart; follow defrost instructions strictly. 

## Cleaning and Maintenance 
Before cleaning, switch off the appliance and disconnect it from the power supply. Utilize appropriate cleaning methods and agents, ensuring no abrasive or heavily perfumed products come into contact with the interior surfaces, as they may lead to damage and residual odors. 

Respect defrosting protocols, ensuring food items are handled correctly to prevent spoilage or frostbite.

### Installation

**Warning:** If you are disposing of an old appliance with a locking mechanism, ensure it is rendered unusable to prevent the risk of children getting trapped.

**Positioning:** The appliance should be placed according to the specified location and temperature ratings:  
- SN: +10°C to 32°C  
- N: +16°C to 32°C  
- ST: +18°C to 38°C  
- T: +18°C to 43°C  

Ensure the appliance is located away from direct sunlight and heat sources. If temperatures exceed those specified, proper functioning may be affected. Follow the outlined installation instructions closely.

**WARNING:** This appliance must be earthed. Ensure all electrical connections are safely fitted and properly grounded. Wire color codes should be followed as per regulations:  
- Earth (E): Green/Yellow  
- Neutral (N): Blue  
- Live (L): Brown/Red  

Use a BSI362 ASTA approved molded plug and follow E.E.C. directives to minimize interference.

### Reversibility

To reverse the door, unscrew the existing hinge and spacer, and follow the reverse assembly instructions provided. Dimensions and clearances must be adhered to during installation.
