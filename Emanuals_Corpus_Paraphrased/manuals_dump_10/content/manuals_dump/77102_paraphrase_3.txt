### ELO 1900L Touchmonitor Overview

The ELO 1900L Touchmonitor features a wide aspect ratio and high-definition performance, incorporating Advanced Acoustic Wave (APR) technology for exceptional touch responsiveness. This touchscreen is designed for high-traffic environments, combining aesthetic appeal with cutting-edge acoustic technology, which produces sound when the screen is interacted with.

The device supports various input formats including VGA and DVI (DVI-D connector) and operates on both AC and DC power sources. It is equipped with a built-in 2W audio output and is designed with height adjustment capabilities, ensuring flexibility in various setups while maintaining a space-saving profile.

In terms of performance, the 1900L boasts high optical clarity, is easy to clean, and has a long functional lifespan with minimal maintenance requirements. Operating temperature ranges from 0°C to 40°C (32°F to 104°F) and extended ranges from -20°C to 60°C (-4°F to 140°F) ensures reliable performance in diverse environments.

Key specifications include:
- **Brightness:** Typical brightness achieved is 250 cd/m² with a contrast ratio greater than 10:1.
- **Power Consumption:** The typical power supply is approximately 38.2W, with a maximum of 70W. 
- **Weight:** Specific weights vary depending on mounting options.
- **Touch Interface:** Features USB connectivity and complies with several regulatory standards including C-Tick, CCC, VCCI, and RoHS.
