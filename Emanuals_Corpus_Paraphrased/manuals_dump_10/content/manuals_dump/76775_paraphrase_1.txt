### ELUH3120L and ELUH3120R Specifications

**General Overview**  
The ELUH3120 series features robust, heavy-duty sound protection and a premium finish. Key design elements enhance sound dampening for an optimal user experience.

**Included Components**  
These sinks come with easy installation features, compatible with various countertops. 

**Optional Accessories:**  
- LKRB1611, LKRB1614, VB1816, LKERBSS
- LKOBG1613, LKOBG1814  
**Cutting Boards:**  
- CB1613, CB1713, CBC1714, CBT1713, CBT2213, CBR1316, CBR1418, CBS1316, CBS1418  

All components meet ANSI A112.19.3M standards set by the International Association of Plumbing and Mechanical Officials (IAPMO) as well as Uniform Plumbing Code (UPC) requirements.

**Sink Dimensions (Inches):**  
The dimensions for the ELUH3120L and ELUH3120R models are designed to fit seamlessly on standard countertops, with specific attention to front-to-back alignment.

**Installation Details:**  
The template and mounting hardware are included with every unit. For optimal installation, the recommended mounting bracket should be used. Please note that no fasteners or silicone caulk are supplied with the product. 

**Conversion to Millimeters:**  
To convert dimensions from inches to millimeters, multiply by 25.4.

**Contour Edge Customization:**  
Customization options for contour edges allow for individual user preference, though additional fasteners are not provided.

All specifications are printed in the U.S.A. and designated under model number 1-108E.
