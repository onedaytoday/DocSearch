**Cordless Electric Kettle Instruction Manual**

This document serves as the instruction manual for the WK101N1 Cordless Electric Kettle. It includes important safety information, operational guidelines, maintenance procedures, and technical specifications.

**Safety Precautions:**
- Always adhere to the specified minimum and maximum water levels.
- Ensure the kettle is switched off before handling it.
- Avoid any contact between the kettle and water while it is connected to the power source to prevent electric shock.
- Regularly check the power cord and plug for damage. Discontinue use if any defects are found.

**Operation Guidelines:**
- The kettle features a straightforward on/off switch (I/O) for easy operation. It automatically shuts off once the water reaches boiling point.
- Prior to first use, thoroughly clean the kettle as outlined in the maintenance section.

**Maintenance and Cleaning:**
- Clean the exterior with a damp cloth and mild detergent. Never submerge the kettle in water.
- Allow the kettle to cool completely before cleaning.

**Warranty and Customer Service:**
- This product comes with a warranty that covers manufacturing defects. Damage resulting from improper use or unauthorized repairs is not covered.
- For service inquiries, refer to the contact details provided in the warranty section.

**Technical Specifications:**
- Voltage: V~
- Power: Watts
- Features: On/Off switch, minimal/maximal water level indicators.

**Final Notes:**
- Ensure the kettle is placed securely on its base when in use.
- Keep the kettle out of reach of children and do not allow them to operate it without supervision.

For further details, please refer to the specific sections in this manual. Enjoy your new cordless kettle!
