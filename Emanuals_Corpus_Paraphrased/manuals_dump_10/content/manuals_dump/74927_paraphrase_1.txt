**CF4 Technical Overview**

The CF4 model is designed for efficient chip preparation, featuring a polished cast-iron structure that simplifies both cleaning and operation. The polished surfaces enhance the aesthetic while reducing the effort needed for maintenance. The cleaning process is further facilitated by an optional suction feature.
