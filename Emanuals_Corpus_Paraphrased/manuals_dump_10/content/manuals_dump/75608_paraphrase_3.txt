**OPERATION**  
To operate the appliance, use the ON/OFF function and the display panel. When functioning normally, the appliance will indicate the temperature inside (approximately +5°C). When the thermostat is adjusted, the settings should reflect the desired cooling level.

When placing food inside, consider the following: adjust the thermostat based on the amount and type of food; different food items may require different cooling settings. For optimal storage conditions, wrap items properly to minimize odors and ensure the appliance operates efficiently.

**MAINTENANCE AND CLEANING**  
Before cleaning, disconnect the appliance from the electricity supply. Use a damp cloth for interior cleaning and avoid abrasive or highly perfumed cleaners, as these can damage surfaces. For defrosting, ensure to empty the contents thoroughly and follow the specific instructions related to the light cover.

### Installation Warning

**Important:** If you are disposing of an old appliance with a door lock or latch, ensure it is rendered unusable to prevent the risk of young children being trapped inside. 

**Location Requirements:**
Select a placement for the appliance that adheres to the specified temperature ratings: 
- SN: +10°C to +32°C 
- ST: +18°C to +38°C 
- T: +18°C to +43°C 

The appliance should be positioned away from direct sunlight, heat sources, or outbuildings. If the ambient temperature is outside these ranges, proper installation may not be achieved. 

**Shelving Instructions:**
To install the shelves, remove them by following the directional arrows, ensuring they are freed from the supports. This allows for a straightforward installation process.

**Electrical Warning:**
**Caution:** This appliance must be properly earthed. Ensure that the power supply is turned off before connection. Use wires as follows:
- Earth (E) - Yellow/Green
- Neutral (N) - Blue
- Live (L) - Brown/Red

The molded plug should comply with BSI 362 or ASTA standards. 

**Reversibility:**
For reconfiguration of the appliance's door, you will need to unscrew specific components and swap their positions accordingly. 

**Built-in Installation:**
Ensure that the appliance is fitted according to the specified height and clearance requirements. Follow the installation instructions carefully, using the supplied screws to secure the appliance in place.
