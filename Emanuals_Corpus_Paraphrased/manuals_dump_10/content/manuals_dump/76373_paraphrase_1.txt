This text provides an overview of the W5105N Professional Laundry Washer, focusing on its operational features and options. It allows for an easy selection of performance levels, offering high-force capabilities with robust protection options (optional I/O board) at the front. The unit supports both heated and non-heated water, functioning within a temperature range of 15°C to 65°C, and is certified according to ISO standards and IP class 24D. It is recommended to operate at voltages ranging from 220-480V, with steam or non-heated water capacity and sound specifications compliant with IEC standards. The washer is designed for efficiency, allowing for the reuse of cold water, and additional details can be found at www.electrolux.com/laundrysystems.
