The RH42PC60G combines elegance with functionality, featuring a powerful blower that efficiently delivers airflow in CFM. It includes bright cooking lights and an easy-to-use LCD clock. The stainless steel canopy is designed for either ducted or ductless installation options and operates at 120V with a grounded connection. 

Pre-installation calculations must take into account the height of the counter to ensure proper setup. For installations in cold weather, specific guidelines should be followed. If opting for ductless installation, conversion to the optional RHDFP60GS is necessary. Ensure to adhere to all provided installation instructions for optimal performance, including clearance requirements and specifications for both ducted and ductless options.
