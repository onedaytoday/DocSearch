### Important Safety Instructions
For safe and effective use of this appliance, please adhere to the following guidelines. If there are any uncertainties regarding its operation or specifications, contact Customer Service for clarification. This unit is heavy; appropriate handling is required. Ensure proper positioning away from heat sources and avoid exposing it to direct sunlight. Always follow the manufacturer's recommendations concerning installation and electrical specifications. If the power supply is disconnected, wait before reconnecting to allow the appliance to stabilize. 

Children should be kept away from the unit to prevent accidents. Avoid overloading the device or using it in an unstable environment, as this may lead to hazards including fire or malfunction. Do not attempt repairs or servicing unless you are a qualified technician.

### Appliance Description
The appliance features adjustable shelves and an operational thermostat. Use the thermostat to manage temperature settings effectively. To optimize performance, avoid overpacking food and ensure proper airflow inside. Regularly check that the thermostat functions correctly, especially during significant temperature changes.

### Food Storage Guidelines
To maintain food safety, avoid leaving cooked food uncovered. Ensure all items are appropriately sealed to prevent cross-contamination. Limit the time food remains at temperatures above 7°C to reduce spoilage risk.

### Defrosting Procedures
Ensure to regularly defrost the appliance to maintain optimal performance. Follow the illustrated instructions for accessing and maintaining the internal compartments.

### Maintenance and Cleaning
Before performing any cleaning or maintenance, disconnect the appliance from the electrical supply. Do not use abrasive cleaners or strong detergents that could damage internal surfaces. Keep drainage areas clear to prevent water build-up.

### Troubleshooting
If issues arise, check the following:
- Verify that the light functions correctly.
- Ensure the thermostat is set within the optimal range (10°C to 32°C).
- Investigate any blockages that may impede food circulation.

For persistent problems, please contact Service Force for assistance.

### Service and Spare Parts
For service or spare parts, contact Service Force by calling the designated number. Be prepared to provide model details and describe any issues.

### Guarantee Conditions
This appliance is guaranteed for a specified period against defects in workmanship or materials. Any repairs or servicing must be conducted by authorized personnel. The warranty may not apply if the appliance is misused or improperly maintained.

### Installation Guidelines
For optimal performance, place the appliance in an environment with temperatures between +10°C and +32°C, adhering to all installation instructions concerning dimensions and ventilation.

### Electrical Connection
**Warning: This appliance must be earthed.** Follow the correct wiring connections as outlined. Only qualified personnel should perform electrical installations to meet safety standards.

By following these instructions, you can ensure efficient operation and longevity of your refrigerator. For any further questions, do not hesitate to reach out to Customer Service.

**Installation Instructions**

To install, follow the guidance regarding the placement of (A) in relation to the required components. Apply the necessary adjustments as indicated. Note: Components DO002 and DO001 must be properly assembled.

Ensure alignment with the specified dimensions. Verify that everything fits correctly according to the provided schematic. If there are any discrepancies, adjustments may be required for the heating element.

For recessed installations, position the electrical supply as indicated to fit within the recess dimensions. The height specifications are crucial. 

To mount, use the provided screws to secure the covering to the plinth at the designated height (A=820mm). The plinth at height B=870mm should not exceed the base height. Ensure that the plinth dimensions align with specifications referenced from DO008.
