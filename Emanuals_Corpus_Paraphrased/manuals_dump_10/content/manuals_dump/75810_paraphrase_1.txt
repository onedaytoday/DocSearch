**IMPORTANT SAFETY INSTRUCTIONS:**
To ensure safety while using this appliance, follow these instructions carefully. Be aware that unauthorized service or modifications can lead to risks. Ensure that the refrigerator is correctly situated as per the guidelines to avoid exposure to moisture or heat sources. Before operating, let it stand for several hours to stabilize. The interior temperature is affected by ambient conditions and food placement, which may necessitate adjustments on the thermostat. 

### Key Safety Tips:
- Keep the appliance away from direct sunlight.
- Avoid using non-approved cleaning products.
- Ensure children do not play around or with the refrigerator.
- Seek professional assistance for repairs; do not attempt them yourself if untrained.

**APPLIANCE DESCRIPTION:**
This refrigerator is equipped with adjustable shelves suitable for fresh meat and various food items. The thermostat controls the cooling performance. Set the thermostat to an appropriate level, taking into account factors like ambient temperature and food type.

### Fresh Food Storage Tips:
- Wrap food securely using airtight materials like polythene.
- Clean shelves regularly.

**NORMAL OPERATING SOUNDS:**
You may hear sounds such as humming or clicking as the refrigerator operates. These are normal and indicate that the cooling system is functioning.

**HEALTH AND SAFETY GUIDELINES:**
To maintain food safety, monitor temperatures inside the refrigerator. The ideal temperature for refrigerated goods is around 7°C, while cooked foods should be cooled before placing them in the appliance. Always cover food to preserve freshness.

---

**DEFROSTING:**
Regularly defrost the unit to ensure optimal performance. If the buildup is excessive, it needs addressing. Clean the drip tray frequently.

### Maintenance and Cleaning:
Disconnect the appliance from electricity before cleaning. Use only mild cleaners – avoid abrasive products that can damage surfaces. Frequently check and clean the drainage system to prevent blockages.

### Light Replacement:
If the light is not functioning, first disconnect the refrigerator from the power supply. Remove the light cover and replace the bulb as instructed.

### Installation Guidelines

To ensure optimal operation, position the unit in an area where the ambient temperature is between 10°C (50°F) and 32°C (90°F), avoiding exposure to direct heat or sunlight. Ensure unobstructed air circulation around the back and top of the appliance.

**Installation Steps:**
1. Lift the appliance from the back, ensuring a gap of at least 2.5 inches between it and any obstruction.
2. Unscrew the top hinge to detach the lid.
3. Remove the foot sections as necessary.
4. Use appropriate tools for fastening screws, applying sufficient force to avoid stripping threads.

### Electrical Connection

**WARNING: THIS APPLIANCE MUST BE EARTHED.**

1. Ensure the power is off before proceeding to connect.
2. Use wires color-coded as follows:
   - Green/Yellow for Earth (E)
   - Blue for Neutral (N)
   - Brown for Live (L)

3. Connect the wires to the respective terminals carefully, ensuring that all connections are secure.
4. Use a fuse compliant with BS1362 standards.

This appliance meets E.E.C. Directives regarding electromagnetic compatibility and safety. Ensure safe handling and maintenance to prevent electrical breakdown.
