**SMART FT (RH06RE1GEU) Overview**

The SMART FT features an internal construction made from AISI high-density expanded polyurethane insulation. This unit includes a microswitch that powers down the system during operation. It is equipped with SMART Control technology, which provides a HACCP-compliant service history and operates only when necessary within a temperature range of F to C. The unit is both CFC and HCFC-free and has a coated exterior.

**Installation and Performance**  
The unit is easy to install and can be mounted with optional accessories. It offers a temperature range suitable for both meat and performance applications, operating efficiently between F and C. 

**Cleaning and Maintenance**  
Designed for easy cleaning, the cooling system ensures uniform temperature distribution. Constructed from high-quality AISI materials, it features rounded corners and a robust design from front to back, ensuring reliable performance and capacity.

**User-Friendliness and Safety**  
The SMART FT accommodates GN or light shelves and includes HACCP-compliant features. It operates quietly, with an acoustic alert when activated, ensuring safe food monitoring via its four-probe system. This technology also enables constant performance tracking over hours, enhancing food safety and service quality.

**Eco-Friendly Features**  
The SMART FT is designed with environmental considerations in mind, employing effective insulation and materials that are free from CFC and HCFC.

- **Temperature Range:** F to C
- **Unit Placement:** Top-mounted
