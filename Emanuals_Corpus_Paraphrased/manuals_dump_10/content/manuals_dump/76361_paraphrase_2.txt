**Safety Instructions:**
To minimize the risk of fire, electric shock, and property damage, please observe the following safety guidelines:
- Familiarize yourself with the user manual and hazards associated with operation.
- Ensure the washer is properly grounded.
- Avoid washing items that have been treated with flammable substances or contain certain chemicals.
- Be mindful of hydrogen gas which can be explosive. 

**Operating Instructions:**
- Load the washer without exceeding the indicated capacity to avoid decreased efficiency or damage to clothing.
- For better cleaning performance, select the proper cycles based on the fabric type and load size. 
- Use the control panel to customize settings, including wash/rinse options for different materials.

**Cycle Selection:**
- Choose your cycle based on the fabric type (e.g., heavily soiled items, sheets, delicates) for optimal cleaning results.
- Select water temperature options appropriate for the load (e.g., hot/cold, warm/cold).
- Adjust wash levels according to load size; light loads require different settings than heavy loads.

**Favorites and Custom Cycles:**
- Utilize the Favorites feature to save frequently used cycles for easy access.
- To create a custom cycle, select the desired options and save your preference.

**Maintaining the Washer:**
- Regularly check the hoses for wear and tear to avoid leaks.
- Follow the manufacturer's cleaning recommendations for longevity and performance.

**Deleting or Editing Favorites:**
- You can easily delete or modify favorite settings directly on the touchscreen for a more tailored cleaning experience.

For further inquiries or detailed troubleshooting, refer back to the library or consult customer service.

### Troubleshooting Your Maytag Neptune TL Washer

If your Maytag Neptune TL clothes washer won't tumble or respond to the touchscreen, follow these guidelines to identify the issue and determine the necessary actions. Refer to the options below for guidance. For further assistance, U.S. customers can contact Maytag Customer Service toll-free at 1-888-4-MAYTAG, including TTY services for the hearing impaired.

1. **Hose Inspection**: Ensure that both hoses are properly connected and free of any blockages.
2. **Washer Settings**: Make sure the washer is set according to the instructions, particularly for handling unbalanced loads.
3. **Normal Operating Sounds**: It’s normal for the washer to emit sounds like high-pitched whirring or clicking during operations, especially while draining. If it’s out of balance, sounds may become more pronounced as the washer adjusts to redistribute the load.

### Common Questions and Answers

- **Q: Why is my washer not selecting the right cycle?**  
  A: Check that you’re selecting the appropriate wash level for your load size, particularly if it’s heavy.

- **Q: What does it mean if my washer operates differently than normal?**  
  A: This could indicate an issue with load distribution or an overloading of heavy items.

- **Q: Can I use bleach to clean my whites?**  
  A: Yes, but only use the recommended cycle for whites and follow bleach instructions closely.
