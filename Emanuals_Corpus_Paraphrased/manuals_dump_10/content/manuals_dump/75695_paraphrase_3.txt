**SMART FT Overview**  
The SMART FT (models RH14RE4HU and RH14RE4HRU) features a construction of high-density expanded polyurethane insulation, compliant with AISI standards. It incorporates a microswitch that deactivates the unit when necessary, providing enhanced performance and safety. 

**Key Features**  
- **Temperature Control:** Operates within a temperature range of F to C, suitable for both meat and high-performance service, including HACCP compliance.
- **Environmentally Friendly:** Utilizes CFC and HCFC-free insulation materials.
- **Storage Capacity:** Accommodates 2/1 GN coated shelves with a service life of 5 years and comes with a one-year warranty.
- **Installation:** Designed for easy installation with an optional mounted setup.

**User-Friendly Design**  
- **Accessibility:** Features forced cooling for uniform temperature distribution, and the highest AISI standards with rounded corners for easy cleaning and maintenance.
- **Operational Efficiency:** Equipped with SMART technology for history tracking (HACCP) and alerts that are activated by acoustic signals, monitored via four probes. It allows for responsive temperature adjustments over specified hours.
- **Easy Maintenance:** Cooling components can be accessed from the top or back for simplified servicing.

**Environmentally Conscious:**  
The SMART FT is designed with insulation that optimizes performance while being environmentally sustainable.
