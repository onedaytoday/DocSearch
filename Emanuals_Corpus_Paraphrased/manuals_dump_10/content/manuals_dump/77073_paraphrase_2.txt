**Product Overview:** The 1517L and 1717L touchmonitors enhance the performance of Touch products with advanced features. These touchmonitors utilize active thin-film-transistor LCD technology for superior performance, complemented by an LED backlight that minimizes environmental impact as compared to traditional CCFL backlighting. Additional features include compatibility and on-screen display (OSD) capabilities, as well as optional features like magnetic stripe readers (MSR).

**Installation Instructions:** 
- **Unpacking:** The package includes the touchmonitor, a protective cover, a TouchTools CD, and essential cables (VGA, USB, AC-DC for North America).
  
- **Connecting Interfaces:** The touchmonitor has VGA input ports and corresponding VGA connectors. Ensure screws are tightened for optimal performance. Connect the USB cable to the appropriate ports and plug in the AC/DC power supply.

- **Software Installation:** The touchmonitor is compatible with Windows XP and other operating systems. Installation files are provided on the TouchTools CD available at www.elotouch.com. For more information on compatibility with other operating systems, refer to the CD.

**Mounting Information:** 
- The VESA mounting interface accommodates standard M4 screws for secure installation. It follows VESA MIS-B and C specifications. Use a screwdriver to secure the screws in the designated holes.

**Power and Functions:**
- To turn on/off the touchmonitor, use the power button. The LED indicator will show the current state (SLEEP or OFF). Tapping on the screen can wake a connected PC from sleep mode.

**On-Screen Display (OSD) Controls:** The OSD allows adjustments for brightness, clock phase, scaling, and aspect ratio. Users can select language options from English, Spanish, Simplified Chinese, and Traditional Chinese. The OSD timeout setting will determine when it automatically closes.

**Cleaning and Maintenance:** Handle the touchmonitor with care. Use a soft, lightly damp cloth for cleaning. Avoid using abrasive materials or solvents that may damage the touchscreen.
