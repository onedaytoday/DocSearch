**PSLVR1916 and PSLVR1916CS Specifications**

**General:** Both models feature a self-rimming design with seamless bearing construction. 

**Design Features:** The depth and recess are polished to a bright, aesthetically pleasing finish, ensuring durability and sound performance.

**Other Details:** The PSLVR1916 is compatible with the LKF293 assembly, while the PSLVR1916CS includes the LKF293 overflow assembly. Each unit has a three-inch outer diameter at the top of the countersink and is designed to accommodate typical fittings. These products conform to ANSI A112.19.3M standards and are recognized by the International Association of Plumbing and Mechanical Officials (IAPMO) as compliant with the Uniform Plumbing Code (UPC).

**Sink Dimensions (Inches):** 
- PSLVR1916: Dimensions provided with overflow center measurements. 
- PSLVR1916CS: Same as above but adjusted for layout. 

All dimensions are given in inches; for conversion to millimeters, multiply by 25.4.

These models, while functional, integrate design elements found in similar offerings from other manufacturers. The PSLVR1916CS model is proudly printed in the U.S.A. 69D.
