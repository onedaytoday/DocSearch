### LK411A and LK411ABH4 Specifications

**General Description:**
The LK411A features a chrome-plated mount equipped with a high flow rate compatible with the AE13A coupling. The LK411ABH4 is similar to the LK411A but includes the BH4A wrist for enhanced functionality.

**Optional Accessories:**
- **AE19A**: Anti-hose
- **BH6A**: Wrist
- **LKMK462**: Mounting backsplash

**Maximum Flow Rates:**
- GPM at 9.5 P.S.I.G
- GPM at 8.3 P.S.I.G
- GPM at 7.6 P.S.I.G
