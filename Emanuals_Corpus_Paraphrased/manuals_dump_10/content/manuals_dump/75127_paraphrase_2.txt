### Features of the XBE80

The XBE80 is a high-performance mixer designed for efficient dough preparation. It features a robust whisk option suitable for heavy mixing tasks and includes a geared reducer to enhance mixing capabilities. The equipment is mounted on a protected trolley with durable castors for easy mobility.

#### Specifications
- **Model:** XBE80
- **Working Height:** Customized for various dough types including Choux, Shortcrust, Cold Brioche, and Meat Puree.

#### Installed Electric Specifications
- **Voltage:** 3N

#### Accessories Included
- **Bowl:** 80 liters for the mixer
- **Trolley:** Suitable for 40/60/80-liter planetary mixers
- **Dough Hook, Paddle, Whisk:** Designed for the 80-liter mixer

#### Optional Accessories
- 40L Bowl, Hook, Paddle, Whisk for 60L Mixer
- 60L Bowl, Hook, Paddle, Whisk for 80L Mixer
- Bowl Scraper for 80L Bowl
- Electric Heating for 60/80L Planetary Mixer
- Heavy Duty Hook for 80L Planetary Mixer
- Meat Mincer with 70mm Hub, including three discs
- Puree Strainer for Hub with Grids
- Vegetable Cutter Hub with Ejector
