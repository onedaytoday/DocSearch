**Emerson Personal CD, CD-R/RW Player with Lightweight Stereo Headphones User Manual HD7999**

Thank you for purchasing this Emerson CD player. This unit features CD track memory, allowing you to choose specific tracks or play them indefinitely. Please read this manual thoroughly to understand the features of this advanced CD player.

**Important Safety Notes**
**Warning:** To avoid the risk of fire or electric shock, do not expose this unit to rain or moisture. Place it away from direct sunlight and heat sources such as heaters.

**Class Laser Product:** This CD player utilizes an invisible laser light and is equipped with safety switches to prevent exposure when the door is open or safety interlocks are bypassed. Tampering with safety switches is hazardous.

**Caution:** There is dangerous voltage inside this unit that can cause electric shock. Adhere to all operating instructions and ensure compliance with FCC regulations to minimize interference.

**Preparation for Use**
**Removing the Transit Card:** Before using the CD player, remove the transit card that protects the CD mechanism. Open the door using the OPEN switch.

**Power Sources:** The CD player operates on AA batteries (included) or with an optional AC/DC adapter. If using an AC adapter, it should be UL-listed and provide 4.5V DC with the correct polarity.

**Installing the Batteries:** Insert AA batteries into the compartment, matching the polarity indicated. Incorrect installation may result in malfunction.

**Replacing the Batteries:** If the battery indicator flashes, it’s time to replace the batteries with fresh ones.

**Connecting the Optional AC Adapter (Not Included):** Connect the AC adapter to the DC IN port. For uninterrupted use, it’s best to remove the batteries when using the AC adapter.

**Battery Precautions:** Use only the specified battery type. Do not mix old and new batteries, and do not attempt to recharge non-rechargeable batteries, as they may overheat.

**Connecting the Stereo Headphones:** Plug the headphones into the green headphone jack. Adjust the volume to a safe level to prevent excessive sound levels.

**Location of Controls and Indicators:** Key controls include the OPEN switch, HOLD switch, MODE selector, PLAY/PAUSE, STOP, and volume adjustments. The LCD display provides various indicators for playback status.

**Operating Instructions**
**Special Functions:**
- **HOLD Switch:** Prevents accidental playback control when engaged.
- **BASS Switch:** Activates bass enhancement during playback.
- **Normal CD Playback:** Ensure the HOLD switch is off, insert a CD, and press PLAY/PAUSE to start playback.

**Playback Options:**
- **Repeat Playback:** Enables continuous playback of a specific track or all tracks.
- **INTRO Scan Playback:** Plays the first few seconds of each track for quick selection.
- **Random Playback:** Shuffles the track sequence for varied listening.

**Programmed Playback:** Allows the user to create a custom track order. You can store up to 20 tracks in memory.

**Line Out Jack (Cable Not Included):** This port allows you to connect the CD player to external devices such as recorders or audio systems.

**Care and Maintenance**
To clean the cabinet, use a soft cloth. For lens cleaning, use a blower for dust removal and a soft cloth for fingerprints. Avoid harsh chemicals.
