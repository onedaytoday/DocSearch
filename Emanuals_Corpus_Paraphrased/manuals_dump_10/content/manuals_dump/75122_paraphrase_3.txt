**Dough PSR100** 

The PSR100 stands out due to its adaptability and the quality of the dough produced. Among its features, the unit is built with a painted exterior that protects the dough during processing. The design facilitates easy handling of the dough once it has been kneaded, and the cover allows for efficient operation. The surface is smooth, making it simple to clean.
