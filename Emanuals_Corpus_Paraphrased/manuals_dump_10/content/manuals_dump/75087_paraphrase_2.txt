**EM-80 Planetary Mixer Short Specification**

The EM-80 Planetary Mixer (model EM80) features a planetary mixing head designed for efficient performance, especially when in the lowered position. It is equipped with a three-phase electric system and includes user-friendly controls with OFF and ON buttons. This compact mixer has built-in safety mechanisms to prevent incorrect operation, ensuring secure locking while in use.

With its high-efficiency, self-lubricating gears, the EM-80 is suitable for a variety of mixing tasks, offering compatibility with numerous attachments: Dough Whip, Beater, and Mixing Scraper, among others. The mixer is ETL and CETL listed and comes with a warranty.

The EM-80 is capable of handling a range of materials, including shortcrust, cold brioche, meat, and purees. Electrolux reserves the right to modify product specifications without prior notice. For more information, visit www.electroluxusa.com/professional.
