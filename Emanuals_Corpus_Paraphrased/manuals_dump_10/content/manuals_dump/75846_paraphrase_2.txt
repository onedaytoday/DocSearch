**OPERATING INSTRUCTIONS**  
To operate the refrigerator, familiarize yourself with the controls:

- **Thermostat Control (C)**: The thermostat should be turned clockwise to adjust the temperature. 
- Indicator Light (A)**: Remains on as long as the appliance is powered.
- **Defrost and Switching Off**: Turn the thermostat to the 'O' position to switch off or to activate defrosting.

Set the thermostat appropriately to maintain food freshness, especially for high moisture items. Allow adequate time for the temperature to stabilize after installation.

**THAWING AND COOKING INSTRUCTIONS**  
When defrosting food, ensure items are thoroughly cooked or consumed promptly. Certain food types can be thawed in a microwave. Follow the specific cooking instructions for various food types to avoid contamination or food safety issues. 

**SHELF POSITIONING**  
Position shelves based on food size and storage needs. Ensure they are properly secured and not obstructing airflow.

**HINTS AND TIPS FOR FOOD STORAGE**  
Avoid overcrowding the shelves and store food in airtight packaging to preserve freshness. Maintain an organized layout for optimal air circulation.

**MAINTENANCE AND CLEANING**  
Before cleaning, disconnect the appliance from the power supply. Clean the interior with mild solutions only; avoid abrasive cleaners or products that could damage surfaces. Annual maintenance should include dusting and checking the cooling system for optimal performance.

### Installation

**Warning:** If you are discarding an old appliance with a lock or catch on the door, ensure it is rendered unusable to prevent trapping small children inside. Choose the installation location based on the specified operating range: 

- For SN: +10°C to +32°C
- For N: +16°C to +32°C
- For ST: +18°C to +38°C
- For T: +18°C to +43°C

If the surrounding temperature exceeds these ranges, proper functioning may be compromised. Responsibilities for installation and placement should be referenced in the provided instructions. 

**WARNING:** This appliance must be earthed. If the appliance needs to be disconnected, ensure it is done properly, and the wiring is correctly set to match colored terminals: 

- Earth (E): Yellow/Green
- Neutral (N): Blue
- Live (L): Brown

Ensure all connections comply with BSI standards. Be aware of the E.E.C. Directive regarding electromagnetic interference.

### Built-in Instructions

To properly install the appliance, follow these steps:

1. Unscrew the main cover (A).
2. Remove spacers (C) and unscrew (B).
3. Position the housing (A) and ensure it does not touch any structures.
4. Use included screws (C) to secure components.
5. Adjust covers (A), (B), (C) as indicated in the instructions.

Check that each part is properly aligned, and tighten screws as necessary for optimal operation. If any adjustments are required, ensure adequate clearance is maintained for efficient functionality.
