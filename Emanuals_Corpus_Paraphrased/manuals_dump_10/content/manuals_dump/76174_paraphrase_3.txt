### Electrolux Cooker Hood EFT UK Instruction Manual

This manual is designed to provide effective guidance on using and maintaining your Electrolux Cooker Hood. For additional information or product support, please contact Customer Service at http://www.electrolux.se.

#### Appliance Information
To familiarize yourself with the appliance, please follow the provided guidelines. This includes instructions for extraction, cleaning synthetic grease, and replacing charcoal filters (optional). Refer to the Specific Service Conditions for any issues.

For installation, consult the relevant sections of this document. It's crucial to adhere to local regulations, including BS.6172, BS.5440, BS.6891, and BS.5482 for liquid petroleum gas. Ensure that the appliance's installation meets the specified clearance requirements to prevent overheating. If the cooker hood becomes excessively hot, switch it off and allow it to cool before resuming use.

#### Safety Information
Always operate the appliance in accordance with safety standards. Children should not be permitted to operate or play with the appliance due to potential hazards, including fire risks when overheated. Regular maintenance of filters and grease cleaning is strongly advised to ensure safe operation.

#### Operating Instructions
For optimal performance, follow the guidelines provided for using the cooker hood's light and extraction features. The appliance vents grease and smoke effectively into the atmosphere. Ensure to switch off the appliance and disconnect it from the power supply before cleaning.

#### Maintenance and Cleaning
Synthetic grease filters should be changed as needed. The filters turn from white to a color indicating they must be replaced. Press the catches (A) to remove the filters from the front. Regularly clean or replace charcoal filters, ideally every three months, to maintain efficiency.

#### Troubleshooting
If you encounter operational issues, ensure the power supply is connected, and check for any disruptions. For further assistance, contact Service Force. 

#### Warranty Information
The appliance is guaranteed against defects in workmanship for a specified time following purchase. The warranty covers service or replacement costs if problems arise, as long as proper use and procedures are followed.

### Installation Guidelines
Ensure compliance with local regulations during installation. The installation area must be adequately reinforced for the weight and fixing screws of the cooker hood. Maintain the specified clearance heights to ensure safe operation, especially when ducting externally. 

Use the installation template and provided screws to secure the appliance properly. Tighten screws adequately during installation to guarantee stability. 

For any further clarifications, refer to the relevant sections of the instruction manual or contact customer service.
