### Care and Cleaning Instructions

#### Cooling and Power Supply
Before cleaning, ensure the appliance is turned off and allowed to cool completely.

#### Cleaning Materials
Use only recommended cleaning agents. Avoid cleaners containing bleach or harsh chemicals, as they may damage the finishes.

#### Exterior Cleaning
Do not use abrasive materials or cleaners on the appliance’s exterior finishes. Use a soft cloth for cleaning.

#### Cleaning the Door Glass Panels
For the outer and inner glass panels, use household cleaners that do not contain bleach or harsh chemicals.

#### Removing the Outer Door Glass
To access the outer door glass, slightly loosen the screws at the top with a screwdriver. While holding the screws and washers in one hand, securely remove them. Gently disengage the glass panel from its location.

#### Cleaning the Outer Door
If the glass becomes chipped or scratched, it is advisable to contact Service Force for assistance. Avoid using abrasives that could shatter the glass.

#### Replacing the Outer Door Glass
To replace the outer door glass, hold it in place while securing the screws with a screwdriver, ensuring everything is tight. Do not clean the inner glass with abrasive materials.

#### Oven Interior Cleaning
The top and back interiors are coated with Stayclean finish, which should be cleaned manually, as automatic cleaning does not apply here.

#### Removing the Wirework Runners
Unhook the shelves and runners from the cavity by disengaging them from the top. 

#### Cleaning Shelves and Cookware
Shelves can be washed in the dishwasher. For Stayclean surfaces, manually clean areas that have been splashed with food to maintain their finish.

#### Oven Cleaning Cycle
Set the oven to 220°C and allow it to run for an hour. Ensure the cooling fan operates correctly post-cleaning.

#### Tips for Reducing Soilage
For better results while roasting, cover meats to prevent splashing. This helps prevent over-browning and ensures thorough cooking.

#### Replacing an Oven Light Bulb
Turn off the electricity supply. Use a screwdriver to remove the cover and replace the bulb.
