### Instructions for Mounting Locations

Please follow these instructions carefully. 

1. **Mounting Position**: Position the assembly laterally as indicated, either to the left or right of the designated area.
2. **Assembly Procedure**: Attach the mounting plate using the screws provided, ensuring they are fastened as shown in the illustrations. The plate should offset correctly from the head component.
3. **Tightening**: Secure both screws tightly to prevent any misalignment. Use a ratchet tool for this process if necessary.
4. **Measuring**: Measure the width from the mounting point to the fence to ensure accurate placement.
5. **Final Adjustments**: Adjust fixing screws for the models UK and PK or PKF to ensure they are positioned exactly as required.

Ensure all screws are tightened as specified to avoid any operational issues.
