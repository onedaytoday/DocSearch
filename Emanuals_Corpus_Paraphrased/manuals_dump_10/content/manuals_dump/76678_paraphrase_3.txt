**Quick User Guide**

**Screen A:** To mount the screen, locate the wall position and determine where to install the screws. The mounting bracket should be positioned approximately one inch from the wall. Use the provided anchors and screws for installation. If utilizing a different mounting method, refer to the instructions in section B.

**Operation:** The screen can be operated via included chains or a remote control. Ensure the screen extends straight down for proper functioning. The warranty covers issues related to proper usage. Keep the wrist and back positioned within the specified distance from the housing while retracting without releasing the mechanism. If additional force is necessary, maintain a straight pull.

For further information, visit www.elitescreens.com or access the user guide at www.elitescreens.com/UG. Model number: 080513-MB.
