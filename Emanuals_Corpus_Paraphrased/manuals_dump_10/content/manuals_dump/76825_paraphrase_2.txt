### Backsplash-Mounted Battery LK722B Specifications

#### General Information
The LK722B is a sensor-operated, backsplash-mounted fixture that activates water flow based on sensor detection. It is powered by AA batteries and features a chrome-plated finish, providing a high-quality aesthetic from top to bottom. The unit includes a flow control mechanism that operates slowly and can be adjusted for a range of times, including settings to prevent overflow.
