**Celebrity CR3321 and CR4322 Specifications**

**General:**
- Type: Self-rimming

**Design Features:**
- Depth: Coved corners and recess below.
- Finish: High-polished, bright finish with undercoating.

**Components:**
- Supplied with four holes as depicted, unless stated otherwise.

**Models:**
- Recommended: CR3322, CR4322. 
- Optional Accessories: 
  - LKRB1611 for CR3321 
  - LKRB1614 for CR4322 
  - LKERBSS for both CR3321 and CR4322 
  - Cutting Board: CB1516 for CR3321.

**Compliance:**
- These models adhere to ANSI A112.19.3M and are certified by the International Association of Plumbing and Mechanical Officials (IAPMO) in accordance with the Uniform Plumbing Code.

**Sink Dimensions (inches):**
- Designs conform to countertop specifications, featuring coved corners. 
- Measurements for CR3321 and CR4322 are provided from front to back and side to side. 

**Conversion:**
- All dimensions are in inches. For conversion to millimeters, multiply by 25.4.

*Printed in the U.S.A. 1-14D*
