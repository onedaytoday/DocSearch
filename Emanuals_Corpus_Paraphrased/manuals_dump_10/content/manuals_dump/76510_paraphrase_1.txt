**SPA Instruction Notice**

This document provides guidelines and information pertaining to compliance with standards. It includes instructions for M6 and M8 hexagon socket screws, as well as specifications for wooden materials and wood dust management. The notice outlines the frequency and tension parameters relevant to the equipment.

The conformity declaration is available in multiple languages, including Dutch, German, English, French, Italian, Spanish, Portuguese, Swedish, Finnish, Norwegian, Danish, and Polish.

This declaration confirms that the equipment adheres to the following directives: 98/37/EC, 73/23/EEC, 89/336/EEC, and 93/68/EEC. We accept full responsibility for ensuring compliance with the standards specified in this unit.
