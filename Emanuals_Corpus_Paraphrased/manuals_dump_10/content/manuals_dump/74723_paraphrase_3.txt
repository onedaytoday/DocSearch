## Important Safety Instructions
To ensure safety, this appliance must be installed and used according to these specifications. If you're uncertain about any of the instructions, please consult an authorized service technician. 

- **Heavy Appliance**: Use caution when moving.
- **Service**: Only authorized personnel should service the appliance. Avoid repairs by untrained individuals.
- **Heat Sources**: Keep the appliance away from heat sources or direct sunlight.
- **Cooling System**: The appliance must be properly ventilated based on supplied guidelines for optimal performance.
- **Child Safety**: Prevent children from playing with or inside the appliance to avoid suffocation risks.

- **Controls**: 
  - **Thermostat**: Adjusts temperature settings.
  - **Warning Lights**: Indicates operational status and alerts for temperature extremes.

## Operating Instructions
1. Activate the appliance using the ON/OFF button.
2. The thermostat regulates the internal temperature; refer to the manual for settings.
3. Monitor the warning lights:
   - A red light indicates a critical temperature has been exceeded.
   - A yellow light signals operational readiness.
4. Load fresh food in small amounts and ensure proper air circulation.
5. To optimize freezing, do not exceed the recommended food load limits.
6. The appliance should be cleaned regularly for efficient performance.

---

## Health and Safety Guidelines
Maintain proper food safety practices by:
- Keeping cooked and uncooked foods separate.
- Storing foods in airtight containers to preserve quality.
- Cooling cooked food before placing it in the freezer.

---

## Defrosting Instructions
Regular defrosting may be required:
1. Turn off the appliance.
2. Allow the frost to melt naturally, using absorbent materials to soak up any water.
3. Clean and dry thoroughly before restarting.

---

## Maintenance and Cleaning
Ensure the appliance is disconnected from the power supply for cleaning:
- Use mild, non-abrasive cleaners.
- Avoid harsh chemicals or products that may damage surfaces.

---

## Troubleshooting
If issues arise:
- Check for common problems such as frost build-up or power supply issues.
- Ensure the thermostat is set correctly.
- Contact a service technician for persistent problems.
