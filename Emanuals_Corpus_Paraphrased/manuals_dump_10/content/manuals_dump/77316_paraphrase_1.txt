**D725/D525 Specifications**

Copyright Incorporated. The D725/D525 is equipped with integrated hardware components including a computer system and associated branding. It supports recovery options and provides documentation in PDF format, along with features for LCD display, HDD, and wireless connectivity options (LAN).

Additional features include memory card support (SD, MMC, MS, and MS PRO), as well as AC and DC power options. The device offers multiple connectivity options, including Ethernet (RJ-45), USB, and support for CD or DVD drives.

Key specifications include the GL40 chipset (supporting L, P, H configurations), ACPI BIOS compatibility, integrated sound capabilities, and compliance with IEEE standards for WLAN and LAN. ENERGY STAR compliant, the device features several USB ports, VGA output, and a robust PC interface.
