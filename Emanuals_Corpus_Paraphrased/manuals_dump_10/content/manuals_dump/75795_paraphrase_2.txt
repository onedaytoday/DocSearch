**IMPORTANT SAFETY INSTRUCTIONS**  
These instructions emphasize safety to protect both users and the appliance. If there are questions regarding any safety protocols, customers should consult the manual. The appliance is heavy, and proper care is advised when moving it. Do not expose the appliance to water, heat, or other potentially damaging elements. Always ensure the back of the appliance has adequate space as detailed in the instructions. The appliance should be allowed to operate for several hours after being powered on for optimal performance. Be mindful of high ambient temperatures, which may affect appliance functionality. Avoid leaving children unsupervised around the appliance to prevent suffocation hazards. Regular maintenance should only be performed by authorized technicians to avoid electrical hazards. Do not attempt repairs without proper experience as this may lead to serious issues.

**DESCRIPTION OF THE APPLIANCE**  
The appliance includes fresh food shelves and a thermostat for temperature regulation. Adjust the thermostat accordingly, keeping in mind that food types, load, and environmental factors can affect performance. For optimal cooling, adjust settings based on external weather conditions and do not overload shelves. Protect food by using proper coverings, such as airtight materials, to prevent contamination. Organize food items based on height and compatibility, ensuring cross-contamination does not occur.

**HEALTH AND SAFETY GUIDELINES**  
Ensure food is stored at safe temperatures (below 22°C) to prevent spoilage. Cool cooked food before storing, and avoid placing hot items directly in the appliance. Always cover food to maintain hygiene and prevent odors.

---

**DEFROSTING**  
Regularly defrost the appliance to maintain efficiency. Always disconnect from the electricity supply before cleaning or servicing.

---

**MAINTENANCE AND CLEANING**  
Disconnect the appliance from electricity before performing any cleaning. Avoid using detergents, abrasive materials, or heavily perfumed products on interior surfaces, as these may damage the appliance or leave residual odors. Ensure all drainage channels are clear.

---

**TROUBLESHOOTING**  
For any issues, first check that the appliance is correctly installed and that the electricity supply is intact. Check for obstructions or improper settings. If problems persist, seek professional service.
