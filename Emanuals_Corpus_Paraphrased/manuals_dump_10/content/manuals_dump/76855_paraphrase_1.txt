**Specifications for LLVR19 and LLVR19CS**

**General Description**: Self-rimming and of the highest quality type.

**Design Features**: 
- Depth and Recess: Finished with a polished, bright surface for aesthetic appeal and sound mitigation.

**Additional Information**:
- Both the LLVR19CS and LLVR19 models include an overflow assembly (LKF296) positioned at the back, complying with ANSI A112.19.3M standards and the specifications of the International Association of Plumbing and Mechanical Officials (IAPMO) as part of the Uniform Plumbing Code (UPC).

**Sink Dimensions (Inches)**:
- LLVR19CS:
  - Countertop with Overflow: Centers
- LLVR19:
  - Centers: right front to back
  - Width: appropriate to specifications with IL as a template

**Notice**: All dimensions are in inches. To convert to millimeters, multiply by 25.4. These features are in line with offerings from other manufacturers.

**Printed in the U.S.A.** Model No. 1-55C.
