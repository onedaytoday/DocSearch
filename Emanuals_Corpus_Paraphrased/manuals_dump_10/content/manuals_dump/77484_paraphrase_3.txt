Prevent fire or shock hazards by keeping this appliance away from rain or moisture. Follow all safety instructions carefully.

### Product Overview

Thank you for purchasing the Emerson Compact AM/FM Clock with dual alarms and a nap timer. The LED display is easily visible, and the controls are conveniently located for user accessibility. Ensure to keep the unit in a location free from direct sunlight, heat, and humidity for optimal performance. 

### Battery Backup Feature

This model includes a Sure-Alarm battery backup system that temporarily restores power during interruptions. When the power is lost, the clock may begin flashing, indicating a need to reset the time.

### Power Source

Connect this device to a standard 120V AC outlet only. Using an incorrect power source may void the warranty.

### Control Locations

- **Front Panel:** Includes ON/OFF and SLEEP functions.
- **Left Panel:** Features controls specific to the alarm settings.
- **Right Panel:** Offers volume control and tuning for AM/FM frequencies.
- **Top Panel:** Contains indicators and compartments for functionality.

### Setting Up the Clock

To set the clock, connect the AC cord and adjust the time using the TIME and UP/DOWN buttons until the correct hour appears on the display.

### Alarm Settings

To set the wake-up time for either alarm, press and hold the respective ALM button while adjusting with the UP or DOWN buttons. The display will indicate AM or PM as you set the time.

### Listening to the Radio

To listen to the radio, turn it on and select the desired band (AM or FM). Adjust the volume and fine-tune the station as needed. The antenna is built-in for optimal reception.

### Wake-Up Options

You can wake to either the radio or a buzzer. Choose your option using the ALM switches, and the alarm will turn off automatically after the set duration.

### Sleep Timer Functionality

The sleep timer allows you to listen to music before the radio turns off automatically. Set the timer using the UP or DOWN buttons, and the display will count down.

### Dimmer Control

The clock features a dimmer control to adjust the brightness based on personal preference.
