**Model 4218N Clock Radio Overview**

**Product Model: 4218N (Various Language Labels)**

**Control Layout:**
- **Hour Button** (STUNDEN)
- **Minutes Button** (MINUTEN)
- **Sleep Button** (EINSCHLAFEN)
- **Snooze Button** (SCHLUMMERN)
- **Alarm Button** (ALARM)
- **Time Set Button** (ZEITEINSTELLUNG)
- **Tuning Control** (SENDERWAHL)
- **Volume Control** (LAUTSTRKE)
- **Frequency Dial**
- **Clock Display**
- **Alarm Indicator**
- **FM Wire Antenna**
- **Power Switch** (ON/OFF/AUTO)
- **Battery Compartment** 

**Power Supply and Setup:**
- Operates on AC with backup from a 9V battery (6F22/0006P/6LR61).
- Ensure a secure connection to an AC outlet for operation. The backup battery maintains clock memory during power interruptions.

**Safety Warnings:**
- Risk of electric shock; do not open the device.
- To minimize electric shock risks, refrain from removing front or back covers. Internally, there are no user-serviceable parts. Consult qualified personnel for repairs.

**Clock and Alarm Settings:**
- **Clock Setting:** After pressing the Hour button, the clock will display "12:00". Adjust using the Minutes button.
- **Alarm Setting:** Press the Hour button to set the desired alarm time. 
- **Wake to Radio:** Set the ON/OFF/AUTO switch to AUTO for radio alarm.
- **Wake to Buzzer:** Change the switch to AUTO to wake with a buzzer sound.
- **Snooze Function:** Use the Snooze button for a temporary off. The alarm will automatically re-engage after a short period.
- **Sleep Function:** Engage sleep mode for gradual shut-off.

**Radio Operations:**
- Use the ON/OFF/AUTO switch to activate the radio. Adjust the volume and tuning as needed.
- For optimal reception, ensure the antenna is fully extended.

**Maintenance:**
- Wipe the cabinet with a cloth to maintain cleanliness. Avoid contact with chemicals.
- Ensure the device is not exposed to excessive heat sources.

**Recycling Information:**
- Properly dispose of the unit and its batteries. Consult local authorities for recycling options. 

**Note:** Always keep the device out of reach of children and utilize hearing protection when using headphones.
