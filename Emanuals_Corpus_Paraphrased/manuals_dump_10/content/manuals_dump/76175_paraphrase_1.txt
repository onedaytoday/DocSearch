### Electrolux Cooker Hood EFT UK Instruction Manual

Thank you for choosing this Electrolux cooker hood. This manual provides essential instructions and guidance for effective use and maintenance. For additional information or inquiries regarding the product, please visit our website at [www.electrolux.co.uk](http://www.electrolux.co.uk).

### Important Information

1. **Installation and Compliance**: Ensure that the installation complies with relevant standards, such as BS.6172, BS.5440, and the Building Regulations. The cooker hood must achieve the recommended ducting clearance height to function correctly and avoid overheating.

2. **Safety Precautions**: 
   - Do not operate the cooker hood with children present.
   - Avoid exposure to overheating, which may cause fire hazards. 
   - Use protective gear when cleaning, and be cautious with harsh cleaning agents.

3. **Maintenance Guidelines**: 
   - Change the synthetic grease filter regularly and clean surfaces to prevent fire risks. 
   - The charcoal filter should be replaced every three months, or more frequently if used intensively.

4. **Operation**: 
   - Ensure the cooker hood is connected properly to the power supply for optimal performance. 
   - In case of malfunction, consult the troubleshooting section or contact Service Force.
