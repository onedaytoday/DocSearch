### FEATURES

The XBM20 is designed for specialty breads, compatible with recommended dough and whisking tasks. The XBM20 operates flawlessly, enhancing the functionality of DITO mixers by improving product handling and minimizing processing time. Its user-friendly touch interface allows for easy adjustments and product feeding.

### Specifications

**MODEL:** XBM20  
**Capacity Height:**  
- Shortcrust: 20L  
- Cold Meat: 20L  
- Puree: 20L 

This unit is equipped with EN beater-mixers, available in models XBMF20ST5 and XBMF20ST45.
