The 900XP is engineered for optimal performance, reliability, and operation. Its modular design allows it to be configured as a countertop, free-standing unit, or plinth. With advanced features, the 900XP delivers exceptional productivity. Installation is straightforward, and the unit is easy to clean, featuring a Scotch Brite finish. Rounded corners enhance cleaning efficiency, while the built-in heating system allows for precise temperature control ranging from 120°C to 300°C, making it suitable for poaching, steaming, and other culinary methods. The 900XP is designed for easy maintenance, equipped with IPX5 protection, and is environmentally friendly, being recyclable and CFC-free.
