### Specifications Overview

**General Features**  
This unit features a top mount with adjustable thickness height, compatible with IPS supply connections. The warranty covers both the brushed and chrome finishes:  
- Brushed: LKS6475NK, LKX6475NK  
- Chrome: LKS6475CR, LKX6475CR  

Flow rates are as follows: 9.5 GPM at 60 PSI and 8.3 GPM at 60 PSI. Installation is recommended for under-mount or countertop setups.
