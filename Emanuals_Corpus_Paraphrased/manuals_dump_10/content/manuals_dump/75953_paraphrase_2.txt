### SANTO Operating Instructions

#### Customer Information

Please refer to these operating instructions for proper operation. These guidelines are intended for users and owners and contain useful technical information. They include warnings and notes relevant to the functionality of the appliance. 

#### General Safety Note

Be aware of supplementary information related to the appliance's sound and operation. In the event of malfunctions, consult the "What to Do" section for troubleshooting steps. For further assistance, contact Service Force.

#### Appliance Installation and Operation

- **Installation Location**: Ensure the appliance is installed in a well-ventilated area, away from direct sunlight and heat sources.
- **Setup the Appliance**: Follow the building-in guidelines provided. Pay attention to the clearance required to prevent obstruction and ensure optimal performance.
- **Initial Operation**: After installation, switch on the appliance and set the thermostat to the appropriate temperature for food storage.

#### Cleaning and Maintenance

Regular cleaning is essential. Disconnect the appliance from the electricity supply before cleaning to avoid electrical shock. Clean the interior surfaces, including shelves, using suitable cleaners, avoiding caustic agents. 

#### Defrosting

Monitor frost buildup, as excessive frost can hinder performance. Clean the frost when necessary, as instructed in the defrosting section.

#### Troubleshooting Malfunctions

If issues arise, consult the "What to Do" section for potential solutions. If problems persist, contact Service Force for professional repair.

#### Electrical Safety Warning

This appliance must be earthed. Ensure that electrical connections conform to local regulations, and that all wiring is correctly connected to the designated terminals to avoid risks of electrical shock.

### Conclusion

It's crucial to adhere to these operating instructions to ensure safe and efficient use of the appliance. Regular maintenance and prompt attention to any issues will facilitate optimal performance.

**Caution: Risk of Electric Shock!** Disconnect the light switch from the power supply. To turn off the light, remove the screws holding the cover in place. Press down on the light cover as indicated and slide it backwards. Ensure the screws are secured and check that the light functions properly. 

You should hear a click when the switch is turned on or off. During operation, you might hear a faint noise, such as a low hum or splashing, especially when the unit is powered down. 

**Regulatory Compliance:** This appliance adheres to applicable household legislation and safety regulations, including GSG, VBG, and VDE standards, as well as EU directives 73/23/EEC and 89/336/EEC (including 92/31/EEC) for electromagnetic compatibility (EMC).

**Technical Details:** The appliance is designed for cooling, efficiently removing heat from food or other items. The installation should ensure adequate airflow for optimal performance, as this unit is typically mounted against a wall, allowing heat exchange with the surrounding air.
