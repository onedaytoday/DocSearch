**Universal WhiteBoardScreen Installation and Warranty Information**

To proceed with the installation of the WhiteBoardScreen Universal, please refer to the following steps. First, ensure you have the required materials: wall screws, hollow anchors, and three M3 screws.

1. **Positioning**: Determine the ideal location for the WhiteBoardScreen on your wall.
2. **Mounting Screws**: Utilize the provided screws to secure the screen. It is strongly advisable to use the included anchors if mounting on drywall.
3. **Securing the Screen**: Attach the WhiteBoardScreen to the wall using the three M3 screws as illustrated.
  
The models referenced include WB58VW, WB77VW, WB87XW, and WB94HW.
