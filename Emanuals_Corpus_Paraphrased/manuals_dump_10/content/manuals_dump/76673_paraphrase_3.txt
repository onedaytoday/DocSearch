### LITE CREENS INFRARED REMOTE CONTROL CODE

**Models:** VMAX2 Home2(3) and Tension2(3)

**Frequency:** 38KHz

**Commands:**
- **Stop:** 1111
- **Down:** 1111

**For VMAX CineTension Only:**
- **Commands:** UP, STOP, DOWN

**MCU RF Remote Control:**
- **Head Operation:** Head counts with a clock rate of 32,767 counts per second
- **Counter Configuration:** 
  - "0" = 12 Counts
  - Clock set at 32,767 counts per second for both UP and DOWN operation commands for VMAX and CineTension

**Infrared Configuration:**
- Frequency: 38KHz
- Commands: UP, STOP, DOWN
