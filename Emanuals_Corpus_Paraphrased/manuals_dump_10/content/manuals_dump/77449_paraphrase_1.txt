### USER MANUAL FOR EMERSON HD7097 PERSONAL CD, CD-R/RW PLAYER

Thank you for purchasing the Emerson HD7097 CD, CD-R/RW player, equipped with advanced features such as joggable anti-skip protection and a long-lasting battery. This player allows you to conveniently choose and repeat tracks indefinitely. Memory functionality enhances audio quality and provides a boost to bass, complemented by included lightweight headphones. Please read this manual thoroughly to understand the operation and features of your modern CD player.

### IMPORTANT SAFETY INFORMATION
**WARNING:** To minimize the risk of fire or electric shock, do not expose the unit to rain or moisture. Ensure you avoid placing it in direct sunlight or near heat sources like heaters.

**LASER PRODUCT NOTICE:** This CD player uses an invisible laser beam and is equipped with safety switches to prevent exposure when the door is opened or safety mechanisms are bypassed. Tampering with these safety features is dangerous.

**ELECTRIC SHOCK HAZARD:** Uninsulated parts inside the unit could cause shocks. Follow operating instructions carefully.

### CONTROL LOCATIONS
- **DBBS On/Off Switch:** Activates to enhance bass.
- **VOLUME Control:** Adjusts sound level.
- **PHONES Jack:** Connects included headphones.
- **LINE OUT:** Connects to external audio devices.
- **STOP Button:** Halts playback.
- **PROG Button:** Accesses CD memory settings.

### OPERATIONAL MODES
1. **Normal Playback:** Insert the CD with the label side facing up and press PLAY/PAUSE to start playback.
2. **Auto-Resume Playback:** Playback resumes automatically after stopping, unless the CD is ejected.
3. **Control Playback:** Use SKIP buttons to navigate between tracks.
4. **Repeat Playback:** Choose specific tracks or all tracks for repetition.
5. **Intro Scan:** Plays the first few seconds of each track for quick selection.

### CONNECTION TO AUDIO SYSTEMS
Connect the player to an amplifier or receiver using the LINE OUT port to enjoy audio through external speakers.

### CARE AND MAINTENANCE
- **Cleaning the Cabinet:** Use a soft cloth; for tougher grime, lightly dampen it.
- **Cleaning the Lens:** Use a blower or soft brush to remove dust. For smudges, use a gentle cleaning cloth.

### TROUBLESHOOTING
Should you experience issues, refer to the troubleshooting section below:

- **No Power:** Check batteries or AC connection.
- **Playback Issues:** Ensure the CD is properly inserted and not scratched. 
- **Sound Quality:** Adjust volume or check headphone connections.
