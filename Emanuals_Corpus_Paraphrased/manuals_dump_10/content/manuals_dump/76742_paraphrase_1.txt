**Celebrity CR3321 and CR4322 Specifications**

**General:** 
The Celebrity sinks are self-rimming types.

**Design Features:** 
- **Depth:** Standard depth with coved corners
- **Recess:** Positioned below the rim
- The exposed surfaces are finely polished to a bright, highlighted finish and undercoated.

**Optional Accessories:** 
Available accessories include:
- LKRB1611 for CR3321
- LKRB1614 for CR4322
- LKERBSS for both CR3321 and CR4322
- Optional board: CB1516 for CR3321

These products comply with ANSI A112.19.3M standards and are recognized by the International Association of Plumbing and Mechanical Officials (IAPMO) under the Uniform Plumbing Code (UPC).

**Sink Dimensions (Inches):**
- Specific measurements include the dimensions from the countertop and coved corners.
- Models CR3321 and CR4322 can be oriented as specified.
- Additional dimensions are provided from front to back and side to side.

**Note:** To convert dimensions from inches to millimeters, multiply by 25.4.

This information aligns with the specifications of other manufacturers, incorporating the notable features of the CR3321 model.
