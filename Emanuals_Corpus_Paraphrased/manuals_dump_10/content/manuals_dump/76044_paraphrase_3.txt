### EL2080 to EL2089 Instruction Summary

**General Guidance:**  
Refer to all instructions carefully before operating this vacuum cleaner.

**Warnings:**
- To minimize the risk of fire and electric shock, avoid exposing the vacuum and its charger to moisture or using it near flammable materials.
- Use only the recommended charger model (SW-250050A) and avoid damaging the power cord.
- Do not mutilate or incinerate batteries, as they may explode at high temperatures.
- Always keep terminals and the unit clear of debris to maintain proper operation. 
- Ensure the vacuum is cool and turned off when cleaning or servicing.

**Safety Notice:** California residents are warned that some chemicals may cause cancer or reproductive harm. Always wash hands after handling.

**Components and Accessories:**
- Features include on/off switch, high/low settings, pre-release buttons, and brush/crevice tools.

**Unpacking Instructions:**
- Carefully remove contents from the box and check for all components.
- Use caution when assembling and securing any locking screws.

**Charging Procedure:**
- Charge the vacuum for several hours until fully charged. The indicator lights will show the charging status, turning on as charging proceeds.
- If the unit shows overload, clear any blockages before operating.

**Cleaning Functionality:**
- The vacuum includes a BRUSHROLLCLEAN TECHNOLOGY function that can assist in self-cleaning the brushroll and hose.
- Note that thick materials may become entangled; keep the unit clear to ensure effective operation.
- Regularly empty and clean the vacuum to maintain performance.
