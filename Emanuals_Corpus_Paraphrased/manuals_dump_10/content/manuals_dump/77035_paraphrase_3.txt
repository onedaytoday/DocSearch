**ELMO ESD-CC2X Specifications**  
The ELMO ESD-CC2X features an RS-485 interface for LCD connectivity, along with a PTZ interface and integrated protection functions. It includes an RJ11 joy connector and supports RS232 and RS485 communication protocols. The device is equipped with a graphic LCD and a real-time clock (RTC-IC), operating on a DC power supply of 12V at 1.5A.
