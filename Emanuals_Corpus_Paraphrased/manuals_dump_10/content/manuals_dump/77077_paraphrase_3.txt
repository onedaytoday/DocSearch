### Touchmonitor Usage and Specifications

**Touchmonitor Usage Instructions**  
To maintain your touchmonitor, avoid using any harsh chemicals or cleaners. Instead, use a soft cloth lightly dampened with water to clean the surface. Ensure not to apply any cleaning agent directly onto the touchscreen, as this may cause scratches or damage. Always disconnect the device from the power outlet before cleaning.

**Touchmonitor Technical Specifications**  
- **Model**: ET1524L  
- **Display Type**: LCD TFT  
- **Active Area**: 304mm (H) x 228mm (V)  
- **Pixel Pitch**: 0.297mm (H) x 0.297mm (V)  
- **Resolution**: VGA to XGA  
- **Brightness**: Up to 16.2M colors  
- **OSD Features**: Options for contrast, brightness, horizontal position, phase, and clock adjustments via DDC1/2B  
- **Power Supply**: AC 100-240V (operating conditions from 0°C to 50°C)  
- **Humidity and Standards Compliance**: Complies with UL, C-UL, FCC-B, CE, TUV, VCCI, C-Tick, MPRII standard regulations

**AccuTouch Touchscreen Specs**  
- **Construction**: Hard-surface top coating with clear or resistive finishes  
- **Touchpoints**: Capable of recognizing multiple touchpoints  
- **Activation Force**: Typically measured under ASTM D1044 standards, achieving a surface hardness of 3H  
- **Performance Testing**: Meets ASTM D1003 for light transmission quality and ASTM D2457 for the surface finish
