### ELUH3116 Specifications

**General Overview**  
This model features a premium heavy-duty sound-deadening design with a distinctive exposed finish.

**Design Features**  
- **Depth:** Engineered to enhance durability and sound insulation.
- **Other Features:** Designed for easy integration into countertops.

**Optional Accessories:**  
- Includes options such as LKRB1414 and LKERBSS, as well as LKBG1414, all compliant with ANSI A112.19.3M standards recognized by the International Association of Plumbing and Mechanical Officials (IAPMO) and the Uniform Plumbing Code (UPC).

**Dimensions (Inches):**  
Each ELUH3116 countertop measures accurately from front to back. A template and mounting brackets are provided for installation, ensuring compliance with the specified dimensions without compromising compatibility with other products. 

**Shipping Information:**  
Printed in the U.S.A. with an IL 1-132C template included. All dimensions are in inches; to convert to millimeters, multiply by the conversion factor.

**Custom Mounting Requirements:**  
The ELUH3116 contour edge is tailored to customer specifications. Note that mounting bracket fasteners and silicone caulk are not included with the sink.
