Continuous Compartment Specifications for LK76:

- General: The LK76 features a plated outer diameter of three, along with a coupling and a plated ship component.
- Requirements: 
  - Plastic washer is required.
  - Waste arm made of chrome-plated brass.
  - Coupling nut also in brass is required.
  - Tee in chrome-plated cast brass is required.
  - Tailpiece of chrome-plated brass.
- Manufactured in the USA.
