#### Important Safety Instructions

To ensure safe operation, adhere to the following guidelines:

- **Read all instructions thoroughly before using this vacuum cleaner.**
  
- **Warning:** To minimize the risk of fire or electric shock:
  - Turn off and unplug the vacuum cleaner before maintenance or servicing.
  - Do not use the vacuum cleaner as a toy.
  - Operate only as outlined in this manual.
  - If the vacuum cleaner malfunctions, do not attempt repairs; contact customer service.

- **Cord and Plug Safety:**
  - Avoid overheating by inspecting cords regularly.
  - Keep cords away from heated surfaces and ensure proper outlet usage.
  - Do not yank or pull on the cord.
  
- **Preventing Fire Hazards:**
  - Keep flammable items like matches and hot ashes away from the vacuum.
  - Allow the cleaner to cool down before servicing.

- **Thermal Protection:** This device includes a thermal protector to prevent overheating. Should the appliance shut off, turn the switch off and check for obstructions in the hose or other components.

### Maintenance Instructions

For appliances with double insulation:
- Double insulation does not require a grounding connection.
- Only qualified personnel should service double-insulated appliances.

### Assembly Instructions

The assembly packet contains screws for attaching the handle and other components. Follow these steps:

1. Ensure the vacuum cleaner is powered off.
2. Secure the handle using the provided screws.
3. Connect the hose to the back of the cleaner ensuring it fits tightly.

### Operating the Vacuum

#### Height Adjustment

The height adjustment feature allows for optimal cleaning on various surfaces. Adjust the height setting according to the flooring type.

#### Power Switch

The power switch is located on the cover of the unit, clearly marked for ON and OFF. 

### Emptying the Dust Cup

To empty the dust cup:
- Twist the cover counterclockwise and remove.
- Clean the interior to maintain performance.

### HEPA Filter Maintenance

The pre-motor filter and HEPA filter should be cleaned or replaced as needed to ensure optimal filtration.

1. **Cleaning the HEPA Filter:** Remove and rinse under running water. Allow to dry fully before reinstalling.
2. **Replacing the HEPA Filter:** Follow the removal steps, ensuring a secure fit when reinstalling.

### Brush Roll Belt Replacement

For replacing the brush roll belt:
- Ensure the vacuum is turned off.
- Access the belt compartment by removing the screws. 
- Replace the belt if it shows signs of wear such as stretching or fraying.
