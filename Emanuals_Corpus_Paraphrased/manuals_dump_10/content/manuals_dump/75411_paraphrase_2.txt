**S90 C-Board Specifications**

The S90 C-Board (WKGROFOOOO) offers high efficiency for starch cooking processes. It features rounded corners and is designed for both standalone and back-to-back installation. The heavy-duty model allows for interconnection, creating a seamless top surface. 

The unit comes with a two-year service warranty and a one-year performance guarantee from the installation date. Detailed information is available at www.electroluxusa.com/professional.

- Internal thermocouple located at the front for ease of use.
- Low-water shut-off and overheating protection.
- Capable of seamless water drainage.
- Options available for LPG conversion and connection to cold water supply.
