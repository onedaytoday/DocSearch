### PSLVR1916 and PSLVR1916CS Specifications

**General**: These are self-rimming type bearing sinks. 

**Design Features**: 
- Depth and recess are polished to a bright finish, enhancing aesthetics and sound insulation.

**Other Specifications**: 
- The PSLVR1916 model is compatible with the LKF293 assembly.  
- The PSLVR1916CS variant includes the LKF293 overflow assembly.  
- Features a top outer diameter designed for countersinks and fittings. 
- Compliance: Both models meet ANSI A112.19.3M standards and align with the regulations set by the International Association of Plumbing and Mechanical Officials (IAPMO) and the Uniform Plumbing Code (UPC).

**Sink Dimensions (Inches)**: 
- For sinks with overflow, the dimensions are as follows: PSLVR1916 and PSLVR1916CS. 
- Measurements go from the front to back and right to left without any countertop. 

**Note**: All dimensions are in inches. To convert to millimeters, multiply by 25.4.

These models are designed to be compatible and functional alongside offerings from other manufacturers, incorporating features from both PSLVR1916 and PSLVR1916CS. 

**Printed in the U.S.A. - Code: 69D**
