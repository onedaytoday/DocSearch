### OPERATION
To operate the thermostat, adjust it between the OFF (O) and the desired temperature range. The selection should be based on the type and quantity of food stored. For effective defrosting, set the thermostat correctly to allow for proper air circulation. Ensure food is properly wrapped to maintain freshness and prevent cross-contamination. Follow these guidelines for optimal performance.

### HEALTH AND SAFETY GUIDELINES
To avoid foodborne illnesses, regularly shop for fresh food and store it at proper temperatures. Cooked food should cool at room temperature before refrigerating, and avoid leaving food uncovered. Ensure thorough cleaning of food contact surfaces and utensils.

### DEFROSTING
