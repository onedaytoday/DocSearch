**Washers EWFLW65H TS, EWFLW65H SS, EWFLW65H IW Overview**

The EWFLW65H series comes equipped with Wave-Touch technology, offering more features than typical washers, including a three-cubic-foot capacity. These washers are designed for smooth, efficient operation, making them an ideal choice for energy-conscious consumers. 

**Key Features:**
- **Energy Efficiency**: ENERGY STAR and NSF certifications ensure optimal energy use.
- **User-Friendly Interface**: Soft-Arc Wave-Touch control with an LCD display for easy navigation.
- **Versatile Washing Options**: Options include settings for various fabric types: Heavy Duty, Whites, Colors, Normal, Active Wear, Delicates, and Custom cycles. Each cycle allows for multiple wash/rinse combinations.
- **Sound Design**: Features an end-of-cycle chime for user convenience.
- **Water Heating Options**: Equipped with settings for pre-wash and bleach applications.

**Technical Specifications:**
- Designed for built-in installations and compatible with 120V electrical service.
- A three-prong grounded supply cord is required.
- Clearance requirements: Adequate space is necessary for both installation and proper airflow around the appliance.

**Installation Considerations:**
- For custom-built installations, guidance is provided with included instructions.
- Clearance around the washer must meet specified standards to ensure proper functioning.

All specifications are subject to change, and it is advisable to refer to the product manual for detailed installation and operational guidelines.
