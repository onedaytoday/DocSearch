#### Important Safety Instructions

Observe all warnings and safety precautions throughout this document. Noncompliance may result in severe injury or death. 

**To reduce the risk of grease fires:**
- Monitor cooking closely, especially with high heat.
- Clean the area frequently to prevent buildup.
- If a fire occurs, smother it with a lid or use an appropriate fire extinguisher. Do NOT use water.

#### Operating Your Hood
