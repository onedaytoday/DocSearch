#### Important Safety Instructions
It is crucial to adhere to these guidelines for safety. If you have any questions about the installation or use of this appliance, please consult the customer service. 

This appliance is heavy; always ensure proper handling. If you have any concerns about specifications or operation, consult an authorized service technician. 

Avoid exposing the appliance to heat sources or direct sunlight. Ensure the back of the appliance is properly ventilated as per the installation instructions. Allow the appliance to run for a few hours before loading it to enable proper cooling. 

High humidity levels can affect its performance. Ensure children do not interact with the appliance to avoid suffocation hazards. 

Never attempt repairs yourself. Only authorized technicians should service the appliance to prevent damage or safety hazards.

#### Operation
To start the appliance, press the ON/OFF button. The warning light indicates the status. Adjust the thermostat as needed to maintain desired food temperatures. 

For optimal food preservation, load the appliance correctly and avoid overload. Follow the recommended guidelines for defrosting and cooking any thawed items.

#### Health and Safety Guidelines
Keep raw and cooked foods separate to prevent cross-contamination. Always cover and store food properly. Cool food promptly and do not leave it uncovered.

#### Defrosting Instructions
Frost build-up may occur; defrost when layers become thick. Turn off the power supply and follow the outlined procedures. 

Once defrosting is complete, return the thermostat settings to the desired level. Clean up any water collected during the process.

#### Maintenance and Cleaning
To clean the appliance, first disconnect it from the electricity supply. Use appropriate cleaning products, avoiding harsh detergents and abrasives which may damage surfaces.

#### Troubleshooting
If issues arise, refer to the troubleshooting guide. Common symptoms include excessive frost or improper cooling. 

For issues beyond simple troubleshooting, contact customer service.
