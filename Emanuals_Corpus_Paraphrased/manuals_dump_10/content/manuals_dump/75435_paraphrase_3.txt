### Installation Instructions for Freestanding Electric Range

**Important Notice:**
Installation and service must be conducted by a qualified installer. Retain this document for use by the local electrical inspector. Ensure to read and save these instructions for future reference.

#### Location and Clearance Requirements
Verify the location of the power supply and its clearances as specified. Maintain a MINIMUM CLEARANCE between the cooking surface and the bottom of any unprotected wood or metal cabinet. If the cabinet's bottom is protected by at least flame-retardant millboard covered with a minimum of No. MSG sheet steel, stainless steel, aluminum, or copper, a reduced clearance may be permissible.

Ensure all dimensional requirements are adhered to in order to prevent property damage, fire hazards, and inaccurate cuts for countertops and cabinets. To minimize the risk of burns or fires from reaching over heated surfaces, avoid placing storage cabinets above the cooking units. If overhead storage is necessary, mitigate risks by installing a range hood that extends horizontally at least a specified distance beyond the cabinet bottom.

#### Safety Instructions
Failure to follow these instructions precisely may result in fire, electrical shock, property damage, or even fatality. Ensure compliance with local wiring codes and ordinances. This manual should accompany the owner's reference materials for future use. Operators must adhere to safety measures when using the appliance.

#### Installation Process

1. **Preparation**: Prior to installation, gather necessary tools, including a wrench and screwdriver.
   
2. **Anti-Tip Bracket Installation**: 
   - *Safety Warning*: To reduce the risk of the range tipping over, install the anti-tip bracket according to the provided instructions. Ensure screws are tightly secured.

3. **Electrical Connection Requirements**: 
    - Ensure that the range is properly grounded according to ANSI NFPA standards. The installation should utilize a supply cord that meets all rating requirements.
    - For permanent wire connections, carefully follow the instructions provided. 

#### Final Steps
- After following all installation requirements, carefully slide the range into its designated location while ensuring full engagement of the anti-tip bracket.
- Maintain the necessary clearances during installation, and do not loosen factory-installed screws or connections to ensure safety and proper functionality.

**Important Note**: Any modifications or disregard for installation details can result in hazards such as fire or electrical shock. Always consult a qualified technician for assistance if needed.

### Instalación de la Estufa Eléctrica 

**Instrucciones**: Para los modelos que requieren un juego de cable de alimentación eléctrica, es fundamental seguir las pautas de instalación. Un uso incorrecto de un cable de estufa con un calibre inadecuado puede resultar en riesgos de incendio o choque eléctrico. Asegúrese de utilizar el soporte adecuado para los cables.

**Conexión Eléctrica**:  
1. En la parte trasera de la estufa, identifique los bornes y el tornillo.
2. **Nota**: Las conexiones son de tipo KW (14-50R y 10-50R).  
3. **Atención**: No afloje los bornes.

**Conexiones del Cable de Alimentación**:  
4. Conecte el alambre negro, el alambre blanco (neutro) y el alambre rojo a los bornes correspondientes.
5. **Nota Importante**: Los bornes deben permanecer apretados.

**Estufa**:  
6. Deslice la estufa con cuidado hasta su posición final, asegurándose de fijarla correctamente en su soporte antivuelco.  
7. Asegúrese de que el tornillo de conexión a tierra esté bien ajustado y conectado.

**Conexión a Tierra**:  
8. Conecte la cinta de conexión a tierra y asegúrese de apretar bien todos los terminales.  
9. Verifique que la conexión a tierra sea adecuada para una instalación permanente de tipo tetrafilar.

Siga todas las instrucciones para garantizar una instalación segura y efectiva.
