**XEM-20 Counter Specifications**

The Planetary XEM-20 Counter (model XEM20ST) is designed with advanced features that ensure mechanical stability and water protection during operation. It is suitable for various dough applications, catering to both professional and commercial needs.

- **Electrical Rating**: NEMA 5-20P
- **Controls**: Equipped with ON/OFF buttons
- **Protection Features**: Prevents incorrect usage and has a locking mechanism for safety when lowered.
- **Construction**: Built with high-quality, self-lubricating components for durability.
