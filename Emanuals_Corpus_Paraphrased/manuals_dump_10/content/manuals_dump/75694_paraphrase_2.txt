**SMART FT Short Specification**

The SMART FT (models RH06RE2HU and RH06RE2HRU) features a robust design incorporating AISI high-density expanded polyurethane insulation with varying thickness. It includes a microswitch to deactivate the unit when not in use. The SMART Control system allows for historical tracking (HACCP compliance) and high service availability. The unit is CFC and HCFC-free and accommodates 2/1 GN coated shelves. It comes with a two-year service warranty and a one-year performance warranty from installation.

**Installation and Performance:**
The unit is designed for easy installation, with the alternative of being mounted. It has a temperature range suitable for both meat and general food service needs, operating from F to C.

**Cleaning and Maintenance:**
Easily maintainable, the unit has forced cooling to ensure uniform temperature distribution. Constructed from high-quality AISI stainless steel with rounded corners, it minimizes cleaning efforts. 

**User Features:**
The SMART system activates acoustic alarms when necessary. It is equipped with four probes to monitor conditions and maintains food safety by recording performance data over time. 

**Environmental Considerations:**
The unit features eco-friendly insulation and operates only when needed, enhancing efficiency. Optional HACCP compliance is built into the design, with several variants available (including coated models).
