### STEREO TURNTABLE WITH SPEAKERS
#### Instruction Manual

**WARNING**: To reduce the risk of electrical shock, do not expose the device to rain or moisture.

**Safety Precautions**:
- Do not remove the front or rear covers.
- This device contains no user-serviceable parts. Maintenance should only be performed by qualified personnel.
- Always be cautious to avoid the risk of electric shock; do not open the unit.
- High sound levels can damage hearing; keep the unit away from children and ensure appropriate use of headphones.

**Safety Instructions**:
- Read and retain these instructions for future reference.
- Follow all warnings and instructions.
- Avoid water and moisture exposure.
- Ensure proper ventilation; do not block air vents.
- Keep the unit away from heat sources like radiators and stoves.
- Use a power supply as specified; ensure grounding precautions are followed.
- Protect the power cord from being pinched or walked on.
- Avoid allowing foreign objects or liquids to enter the unit.
- In case of malfunction or damage, seek serviced support from qualified technicians.

**Control Locations**:
- Power Switch
- FM Antenna
- AC Power Input
- Auto Stop
- AUX Output (L/R)
- Phono Cover
- Internal Speaker (On/Off)
- Volume Control Knob (Min/Max/Off)

**Operation**:
- Ensure proper power supply connection.
- Select the appropriate speed for playback.
- Handle the tonearm with care to avoid damage to records.
- Auto Stop feature can be toggled between ON and OFF positions.
- To replace the cartridge, carefully detach it from the tonearm.

**Specifications**: 
- General Operating Voltage: AC 230V
