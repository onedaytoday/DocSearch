**Connecting LCD TVs to A/V Devices**

To achieve optimal high-definition performance, connect your A/V devices, such as DVD players, to the LCD TV via the HDMI input. This connection will provide the best quality for HD content. For detailed instructions on connecting HD devices using HDMI, refer to the user manual.

**Connection Options:**
- HDMI IN
- S-VIDEO IN
- VIDEO IN
- VGA IN
- PC AUDIO IN

**Included with your TV:**
- Component and composite cables
- Power cord
- Remote control
- AAA batteries
- RF cable for basic connections
- Coaxial input for VHF/UHF antenna

For further details, please visit www.elementelectronics.com.
