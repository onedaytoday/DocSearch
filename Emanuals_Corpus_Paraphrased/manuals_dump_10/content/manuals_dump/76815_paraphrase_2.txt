### LK411A and LK411ABH4 Specifications

**General:**
The LK411A features a chrome-plated mount designed for use with the AE13A coupling. The LK411ABH4 retains all characteristics of the LK411A but includes a BH4A wrist assembly.

**Optional Accessories:**
- AE19A anti-hose 
- BH6A wrist assembly
- LKMK462 backsplash mounting

**Maximum Flow Rates:**
- GPM at 9.5 PSI
- GPM at 8.3 PSI
- GPM at 7.6 PSI
