**SAFETY INFORMATION**  
Please retain these instructions for future reference. If the appliance changes ownership, these guidelines should accompany it. It is essential to prevent the appliance from becoming hazardous, especially to children. Ensure children are supervised near it and do not allow them to use it unsupervised. Regular maintenance and cleaning procedures should be followed, including switching off when required. Avoid direct sunlight exposure and maintain adequate spacing around the appliance for heat dissipation. Only authorized service technicians should perform repairs. 

**OPERATIONAL TIPS**  
When cleaning, use appropriate tools and methods to avoid damaging the appliance. The quick-freezing feature assists in food preservation; turn the thermostat to the recommended temperature for optimal cooling. During operation, ensure airflow is unobstructed for efficient energy use. 

**FOOD STORAGE GUIDELINES**  
Proper food placement and handling are critical. Avoid overcrowding, and store foods appropriately to maintain freshness. Defrosting should be performed safely, following best practices to prevent health risks. Monitor food temperatures carefully and allow adequate time for cooling. 

**MAINTENANCE**  
Ensure the appliance is disconnected from the power source before cleaning. Use non-abrasive cleaners that won't damage surfaces or leave odors. Regularly defrost the appliance to maintain food safety and performance. Follow specific guidelines for various food types when freezing or cooking. 

**TROUBLESHOOTING**  
If problems arise, consult the troubleshooting section for common issues. If the appliance is not functioning correctly, verify settings and check for obstructions. Contact service professionals if issues persist.

**INSTALLATION**

**Warning:** When disposing of an old appliance with a locked or secured door, ensure it is rendered unusable to prevent accidents with young children.

**Positioning Instructions:** Place the appliance in a suitable location as specified by its rating. 

**Ambient Temperature Ranges:**
- **SN:** +10°C to +32°C
- **N:** +16°C to +32°C
- **ST:** +18°C to +38°C
- **T:** +18°C to +43°C

The appliance should be positioned away from direct sunlight and not in unheated areas where temperatures drop below +10°C (50°F). Ensure there is adequate airflow around the appliance, particularly at the back and top. 

**Installation Guidelines:** 
- Avoid obstructing the appliance's feet or screws with other objects, which can restrict airflow.
- Ensure the rear spacers are correctly installed as depicted in the manual.

**Warning:** This appliance must be properly earthed. Make sure the electrical connection is safely installed. If disconnecting the wires, note the following color coding for reconnection: 

- **E (Earth):** Yellow/Green
- **N (Neutral):** Blue
- **L (Live):** Brown or Red

Use a molded plug that complies with BSI362 or ASTA guidelines.

This appliance adheres to relevant E.E.C. Directives concerning electromagnetic interference.
