### Features of the XBM20 Specialty Bread Mixer

The XBM20 is designed for preparing specialty dough with precision, ensuring faultless results for various products and sauces. It leverages the advanced features of DITO mixers to enhance production efficiency while significantly reducing preparation time. The mixer offers straightforward controls and exceptional performance, including options for whisking and mixing.

**Model Options:**

**Capacity and Applications:**
- Suitable for mixing Choux, Shortcrust, Cold Brioche, Meat, and Puree.
