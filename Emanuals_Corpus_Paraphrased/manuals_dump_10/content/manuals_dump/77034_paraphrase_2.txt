**INSTRUCTION MANUAL**

**LED DIP Switch Configuration**  
Set DIP Switch (4) to ON to activate power to the device. 

**AC CONNECTION**  
(Supply included) 

**OPERATION**  
The RSU-200 wirelessly connects to infrared devices. The IR LED on the RSU-200 will illuminate when the system is active, indicating "POWER." It is advisable to identify wireless settings when RS-485 is engaged. 

**PC DIP Switch Settings for RSU-200**  
Connections include BNC and RCA (both provided), along with BNC/RCA conversion for the models: PTC-200C, PTC-100, and PTC-110R. 

**INTERNAL SWITCH**  
Engage the "LONG" switch on the RSU-200. It is recommended to operate within the specified range when using the PTC at 5C-2V.
