**Service ESS20070 Specifications**

**General**: The ESS20070 service type features coved corners and a thick backsplash. It is designed to meet shipboard standards outlined in MIL-S-2143B (Ships) and includes painted finishes for durability and soundproofing. 

**Design Details**: The unit has rolled rim front ends with specified diameters. Note that rough-in dimensions can be found on the reverse side. It includes a bracket plate with two holes and angle iron supporting brackets, which are provided loose. All components are welded on.

**Rough-in Dimensions**: The floor line includes slot dimensions and wall hanger details, with an indicated hole diameter.
