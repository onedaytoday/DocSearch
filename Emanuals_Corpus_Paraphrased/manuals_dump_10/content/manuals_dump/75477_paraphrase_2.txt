### Induction Appliance Instructions 67670KF-N

**Dear Customer,**

Please review these instructions carefully to ensure proper operation of your appliance. The guidelines below include warnings, notes, and important information regarding safety and operation.

**Operation Instructions for the Induction Appliance:**
- **Touch Controls**: Use touch heat controls to switch the appliance on and off, select heat settings, and activate the STOP+GO function.
- **STOP+GO Function**: This feature pauses cooking temporarily and can quickly return to the selected heat level.
- **Cleaning and Maintenance**: Ensure the appliance is turned off before cleaning. Avoid using high-pressure cleaning methods, and avoid scratching surfaces. Care should be taken to prevent burns from heat and cookware.

**Specifications**:
- Induction cooking power: 2300W with a boost up to 3200W; heat selection includes 1500/2400W and 1200W.
- Features include an On/Off switch, heat selection, and Timer functionality.

**Child Safety Features**: 
- A child lock function is available to prevent accidental operation. Sounds will indicate the activation and deactivation of this safety feature.

**Troubleshooting**: 
- If a malfunction occurs, the appliance will switch off automatically. Ensure that cookware is compatible to avoid overheating.

**General Usage Tips**:
- Use cookware that is suitable for induction cooking to ensure effective heat transfer.
- Avoid using damaged cookware and ensure that it is in good condition before operation.

**Cleaning Tips**:
- Regularly clean the appliance using a soft, damp cloth. For stubborn residues, use approved scrapers or cleaners available from our service outlets.

For warranty information, compliance with EU regulations (73/23/EEC, 89/336/EEC, etc.), and service details, please refer to the full instruction manual.

Thank you for your attention to these important details for proper use and safety during operation.
