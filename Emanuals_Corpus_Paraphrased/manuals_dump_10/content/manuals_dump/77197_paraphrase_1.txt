**PM UNDER-CABINET CLOCK RADIO WITH PLL TUNER**

This under-cabinet clock radio is equipped with a PLL tuner, designed for mounting beneath cabinets. The unit includes controls for various functions such as power (ON/AUTO/OFF), a countdown timer, and a battery compartment that requires UM-4 (AAA) batteries. 

**IMPORTANT SAFETY NOTES:**
1. **Electric Shock Hazard**: Opening the unit may expose you to electric shock. Do not remove covers, as there are no user-serviceable parts inside. Maintenance should be performed by qualified personnel only.
2. **Fire and Shock Prevention**: Avoid exposing the device to moisture or rain to mitigate the risk of fire or electric shock.

**SETTING THE CLOCK:**
1. Rotate the function switch to OFF to set the time. 
2. Press the hour button to set the current hour, confirming with the Hour button.

**RADIO OPERATION:**
- To operate the radio, switch the function to ON. Use the station search feature to find and store your favorite stations.
  
**ALARMS:**
- The unit can wake you using either the radio or a buzzer. Set the alarm time by adjusting the hour and activating the desired alarm function.
  
**COUNTDOWN TIMER:**
- Activate the countdown timer by turning it ON and adjust the timer settings as needed. 

**INSTALLATION TIPS:**
- Ensure the unit is securely mounted under cabinets, avoiding any obstructions that could impede ventilation.

**TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS:**
- Power Supply: AC and 1.5V battery (UM-4 AAA).
- FM tuner included.

**ADDITIONAL INFORMATION:**
- High volumes can damage hearing. Keep the device out of reach of children and avoid long exposure to loud sounds through headphones.
- For recycling and disposal, follow local guidelines to protect the environment.

For detailed operational and safety instructions, refer to this manual or contact qualified support personnel.
