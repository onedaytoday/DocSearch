#### OPERATION
To power ON/OFF the appliance, use the designated display. Adjust the thermostat to +5°C for optimal food preservation. Various functions can be activated by pressing specific buttons. A countdown timer will be displayed during the activation of certain features.

Properly arrange food in the appliance, ensuring items are not overcrowded to allow for optimal air circulation. Cover food with appropriate materials and avoid sharp objects for frost removal.

#### MAINTENANCE AND CLEANING
Prior to cleaning, disconnect the appliance from the electricity supply. Use mild cleaning solutions and avoid abrasive or heavily perfumed products, as these can damage surfaces and leave lingering odors. For defrosting, follow the guidelines provided in the manual.

#### TROUBLESHOOTING
If the appliance does not operate as expected, consult the troubleshooting section of the manual or contact Service Force. Common issues include power supply failures, thermostat settings, and blockages.

### Installation Warning

**IMPORTANT:** When disposing of an old appliance with a door lock or catch, ensure it is rendered inoperable to prevent young children from becoming trapped inside. 

**Positioning Instructions**  
Install the appliance in a location that maintains the ambient temperature according to its rating. The suitable temperature ranges are:  
- SN: +10°C to +32°C  
- N: +16°C to +32°C  
- ST: +18°C to +38°C  
- T: +18°C to +43°C  

Ensure the unit is shielded from direct sunlight, excessive heat, and enclosed spaces like outhouses.

**Shelving Instructions**  
To adjust the shelves, follow the orientation indicated by the arrows. Release the shelves from their slots, allowing for easy adjustment and installation.

**Electrical Connection Warning**  
THIS APPLIANCE MUST BE EARTHED. Ensure proper grounding for safety. If replacing or fitting a plug, connect the wires as follows:  
- Earth (E): Green/Yellow  
- Neutral (N): Blue  
- Live (L): Brown  

For safety compliance, use a plug that incorporates BSI362 or ASTA standards. 

This appliance meets the E.E.C. Directives regarding electromagnetic interference and other modifications.

**Reversibility Instructions**  
To reverse the door, unscrew the hinges and reinstall them on the opposite side. Follow the provided instructions carefully for the process.

**Built-in Installation**  
Ensure that there is adequate clearance for ventilation as specified. Mount the appliance securely using the screws included with the installation kit.
