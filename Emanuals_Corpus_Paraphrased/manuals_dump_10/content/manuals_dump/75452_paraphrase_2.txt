**S90 Char-Broiler Specifications**

The S90 Char-Broiler (model WDGRAAOOOO) is designed for high-performance cooking, featuring a robust base. It boasts high BTU output and is suitable for various installation types, facilitating easy cleaning. The unit includes a grease collection system, ensuring efficient operation. Its heavy-duty internal construction connects multiple tops, allowing for synchronized performance.

This model is backed by a two-year service warranty and a one-year performance warranty from Professional, Inc. For more information, visit www.electroluxusa.com/professional.

Key features include:

- High efficiency with straightforward operation.
- Effective grease management and easy cleaning, including a thermocouple and a splash guard at the rear.
- Options such as a round plinth and scraping cleaning tools.
- A right-side portioning shelf is also available.
