**Product Overview:**
The ET1915L LCD Touchmonitor features an active thin-film-transistor (TFT) display designed for optimal performance with SXGA graphics. The unit supports multiple resolutions, including VGA, SVGA, XGA, and non-interlaced SXGA, and maintains VESA DPMS compatibility along with DDC1/2B support. An integrated OSD (On-Screen Display) and wall-mounting capability enhance functionality.

**Unpacking Instructions:**
This section provides guidance for unboxing the LCD touchmonitor, which includes the unit, VGA cable, USB cable, and QuickStart software CD.

**Installation and Setup:**
To connect the touchmonitor to a PC, ensure both devices are powered off. Attach the video and touchscreen connections (USB or and VGA as applicable). Secure connections by tightening screws. Refer to the installation software on the included CD for various operating systems including XP and NT. 

**Touch Interface Setup:**
Load the appropriate drivers for plug-and-play functionality. Should software installation fail, ensure the connection method (USB or VGA) is correct and follow troubleshooting steps as needed.

**Operating Procedures:**
The OSD manages display functions such as brightness and position adjustments. To navigate the OSD, use designated control buttons to select or adjust settings. 

**Performance and Specifications:**
Ensure the touchmonitor operates under optimal conditions according to factory specifications, addressing any performance issues promptly. 

**Troubleshooting Common Issues:**
For issues impacting display performance, refer to resolution guidelines for input matching (VGA, SVGA, XGA, SXGA, etc.). Ensure connections are secure and follow cleaning and maintenance recommendations to avoid damage. 

**Safety Precautions:**
The touchmonitor should be connected through a 3-wire grounding cord to a properly grounded outlet to avoid electrical hazards. Consult safety guidelines to prevent unauthorized modifications or repairs.

### Touchmonitor Specifications and Care Guidelines

#### Touchmonitor Model: B-28

For optimal functionality of the B-28 Touchmonitor, avoid exposing it to shock or using inappropriate cleaning agents. Use a lightly dampened cloth for cleaning; do not apply cleaners directly to the touchscreen as this may cause scratches.

#### Technical Specifications (Appendix C)

- **Model:** ET1915L LCD TFT 
- **Active Area Dimensions:** 
  - Horizontal: 376.32 mm 
  - Vertical: 301.06 mm 
  - Pixel Pitch: 0.294 mm (H) x 0.294 mm (V)
- **Supported Resolutions:** VGA, SVGA, XGA, SXGA 
- **Color Depth:** 16.2 million colors (non-touch)
- **Brightness Levels:** Various modes available
- **Connectivity:** Supports RGB TTL or negative connectors;
  - Includes OSD controls for brightness, H-position, sharpness, phase, and clock adjustments; available in English and Spanish.
- **Power Requirements:** AC 100-240V; operating temperature ranges and humidity details provided.
- **Certifications:** UL, c-UL, FCC-B, CE, VCCI, C-Tick, MPRII, TUV.
