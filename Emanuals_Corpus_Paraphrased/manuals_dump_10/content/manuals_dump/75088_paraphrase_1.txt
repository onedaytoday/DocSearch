**PRODUCT FEATURES**

The DITOMIX CHROME Professional planetary mixer, featuring the DITOMIX5, is designed for intensive mixing tasks. This model excels at whipping egg whites, creating chantilly cream, and preparing shortcrust pastry. The included accessory enhances efficiency by optimizing the activation and adjustment of the mixing speed.
