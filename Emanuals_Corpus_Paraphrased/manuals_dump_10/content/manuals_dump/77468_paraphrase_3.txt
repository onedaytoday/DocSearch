### CD Playback Instructions:
1. To play a CD, press the "CD OPEN" button and load your disc.
2. Use the "Play/Pause" button to start or pause playback.
3. Press "Stop" to end playback.
4. Use the forward and reverse buttons to navigate tracks.

**Program and Random Playback:**
- Use the "PROGRAM" button to create a custom playback sequence and to replay tracks.
- The "RANDOM" setting will play tracks in a random order.
