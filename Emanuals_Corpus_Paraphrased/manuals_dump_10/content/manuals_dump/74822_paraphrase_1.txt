### Installation Warning

**Caution:** When disposing of an old appliance with a door lock or catch, ensure it is rendered inoperable to prevent children from becoming trapped inside.

**Positioning:** The appliance should be placed in a location that meets the specified temperature ratings: 
- **SN:** +10°C to +32°C 
- **ST:** +18°C to +38°C 
- **T:** +18°C to +43°C 

Avoid areas exposed to direct sunlight or environments with temperatures below 10°C (50°F). Ensure there is adequate space around the back and top of the appliance for proper ventilation.

**Installation Instructions:** Do not obstruct the airflow from the appliance. Make sure there is sufficient space for the rear spacers as outlined. 

**Warning:** This appliance must be earthed. If not properly grounded, it may result in a malfunction. The electrical supply should be turned off before installation.

**Wiring:** Connect the wiring as follows:
- Earth (green/yellow) to E terminal
- Neutral (blue) to N terminal 
- Live (brown) to L terminal

Use a plugged connector that conforms to BSI362 ASTA or BSI standards. This appliance meets E.E.C. Directives on electromagnetic interference.

**Storage and Accessories:** Ensure shelves are installed correctly, aligning with the arrows for proper positioning. 

**Reversibility Operations:** To carry out reversible operations, it is necessary to follow the instructions carefully.
