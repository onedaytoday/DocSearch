#### IMPORTANT SAFETY INFORMATION
This instruction manual must be kept for future reference. If the appliance is transferred to a new owner or location, ensure that this manual accompanies it to maintain proper functionality. Adhere to all safety guidelines stated herein.

Follow the DEFROST and CLEANING instructions meticulously. Any repairs should be conducted by authorized personnel only, as improper servicing may lead to operational issues. Use the appliance properly and responsibly to prevent safety hazards.

### OPERATION
To operate the appliance, adjust the thermostat to the appropriate setting. The cooling performance can be influenced by factors such as ambient temperature and food load. If food is stored for prolonged periods or during high ambient temperatures, adjust the thermostat accordingly.

For optimal cooling, ensure that food items are within the recommended temperature range. Foods should be consumed promptly after thawing to maintain safety and quality.

For fresh food storage, utilize protective materials to cover items on the shelves. Organize food in a way that allows for good airflow around items; avoid overcrowding.

---

### MAINTENANCE AND CLEANING
Before cleaning, disconnect the appliance from the electricity supply. Use a damp cloth and mild cleaners for surfaces; avoid harsh chemicals that may damage the interior.

To defrost, monitor frost thickness and follow specified defrosting procedures. Ensure that the appliance is unplugged during cleaning.

### Installation Instructions 

**Warning:** If disposing of an old appliance with a door lock or catch, ensure it is rendered unusable to prevent young children from becoming trapped inside.

The installation site for the appliance should be chosen carefully, as mentioned in the accompanying user manual. It should accommodate the following temperature ranges:
- SN: +10°C to +32°C
- N: +16°C to +32°C
- ST: +18°C to +38°C
- T: +18°C to +43°C

The appliance must be positioned away from direct sunlight and heat sources. If the ambient temperature exceeds these ranges, it may not function properly. 

**Warning:** This appliance must be properly earthed. Make sure to connect it to an appropriate power supply, and always follow safety guidelines. The power cord should correspond with the following wiring colors:
- Earth: Yellow/Green
- Neutral: Blue
- Live: Brown/Red

Only use components that meet BSI362 ASTA or BSI standards. This appliance meets the E.E.C. Directive requirements concerning electrical interference and safety.

### Operation and Setup

Ensure the appliance is safely positioned. The operational setup requires wiring to be correctly connected from the appliance's rear. Each connection should click securely into place. For the built-in installation, follow the provided instructions to ensure that all screws and fittings are correctly assembled.

### Building-In Instructions 

Make sure the appliance fits correctly within the recessed space specified in the user manual. Use the appropriate screws in the top and front corners as shown. The electricity supply should be appropriately positioned to align with the dimensions of the recess. 

The height without a plinth should be A=820mm, with a total height including the plinth of B=890mm. Ensure the plinth is set at the correct height to leave adequate space above for proper placement.

For any further instructions or information, refer to the user manual or contact the Service Force directly.
