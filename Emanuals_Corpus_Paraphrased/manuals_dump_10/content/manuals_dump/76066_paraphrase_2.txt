**IMPORTANT SAFEGUARDS**  
Adhere to all precautions, including: READ ALL INSTRUCTIONS BEFORE OPERATING THIS VACUUM CLEANER. 

**WARNING**  
To minimize risks of fire, electric shock, or injury: 
- Turn off the switch before unplugging or maintaining the vacuum. 
- Allow components to cool before handling. 
- Keep out of reach of children and use only for intended purposes. 
- Avoid using with damaged cords or plugs, and ensure proper placement away from heat sources. 
- Regularly check for obstructions in the hose, as this may lead to overheating.  
- If malfunctions occur, discontinue use and contact an Authorized Service Center.

**THERMAL PROTECTION**  
This appliance features a thermostat to prevent overheating. In the event of overheating, the on/off switch will turn off automatically. 

**SERVICING DOUBLE-INSULATED APPLIANCES**  
Double insulation eliminates the need for a grounding conductor. Only qualified personnel should conduct repairs to maintain safety.

**ASSEMBLY INSTRUCTIONS**  
The assembly package includes screws necessary for attaching the handle to the vacuum. First, ensure the on/off switch is off. Attach the handle securely using a screwdriver to tighten the screws. Connect the hose to the specified inlet on the vacuum cleaner.

**HANDLE RELEASE & HEIGHT ADJUSTMENT**  
To adjust the vacuum height, utilize the handle release. Ensure the setting matches the type of flooring for optimal cleaning performance.  

**POWER SWITCH**  
The power switch is located at the front of the unit. Press back to turn on (I) or forward to turn off (O). 

**EMPTYING THE DUST CUP**  
To empty the dust cup, twist counterclockwise and remove it from the cleaner. Clean it periodically for sustained performance. 

**CLEANING THE DUST CUP AND FILTERS**  
Clean the dust cup and filter as necessary to maintain optimal performance. Dishwasher-safe parts can be cleaned in a dishwasher. 

**REINSTALLING COMPONENTS**  
Ensure components are reassembled correctly with proper orientation and engagement.

**HEPA FILTER MAINTENANCE**  
Check and clean the pre-motor filter and HEPA filter regularly. Follow the manufacturer’s instructions to ensure effective performance.

**REPLACING THE BRUSH ROLL BELT**  
Before replacing the belt, turn off the vacuum and unplug it. Access the screws indicated to remove the base. If the belt is stretched or damaged, replace it following the outlined steps.
