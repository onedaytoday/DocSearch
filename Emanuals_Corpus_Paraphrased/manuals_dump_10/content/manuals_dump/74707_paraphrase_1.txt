**IMPORTANT SAFETY INFORMATION**

This manual should be kept for future reference. If the appliance is transferred to another owner, it is essential that this manual accompanies it. Users should familiarize themselves with the operation and safety features of the appliance as described herein. Adhering to the guidelines in this manual is crucial to prevent any hazards. 

This appliance is heavy; it’s important to follow the specifications and service recommendations carefully. Avoid exposing the appliance to heat sources, and always ensure that the rear of the unit is ventilated as per the instructions provided. For safety, turn off the appliance before any maintenance. Young children should not be allowed to play with or near the appliance.

The cooling and recharging of components should be performed only by authorized technicians. Avoid prolonged exposure to direct sunlight, and ensure that food is stored properly to prevent freezer burn and other damages. Follow manufacturer guidelines strictly.

**OPERATION**

Ensure familiarity with the controls, including the thermostat. The appliance's efficiency may vary based on environmental factors, such as overall temperature and the amount of food stored. During defrosting, only perform this when necessary. 

Different food types have specific storage requirements, and items such as thawed cooked food should not be refrozen. Proper food management and organization are critical to maintaining quality and safety.

While cleaning, disconnect the appliance from the electricity supply. Use non-abrasive, low-odor cleaning products only, to avoid damaging surfaces. For light replacement or defrosting, refer to the proper procedures in the manual.

**TROUBLESHOOTING**

If there are operational issues, check the following: Ensure the light is functional, verify thermostat settings, and make sure that food items are not obstructing door closures.

**INSTALLATION:**
**Warning:** When disposing of an old appliance with a locking mechanism, ensure it is rendered unusable to prevent accidents involving children. The appliance should be installed in a location that meets the recommended temperature range based on the ratings provided. Ideal operational temperatures are specified as follows:
- SN: +10°C to +32°C
- N: +16°C to +32°C
- ST: +18°C to +38°C
- T: +18°C to +43°C

Avoid locations near heat sources or extreme climates. Follow the instructions carefully during installation, particularly for built-in models.

**Warning:** This appliance must be earthed. To ensure safety, the appropriate wiring must be connected. Earth connections should be identified, and the terminal connections are standardized as follows:
- Earth: Green/Yellow
- Neutral: Blue
- Live: Brown/Red

Ensure compliance with local regulations regarding electrical installations and modifications as mandated by EEC directives.

**Instructions for Built-in Models:** Follow the assembly instructions closely for installation. Unscrew relevant fittings to secure the appliance in the designated recess. Verify that all clearances are maintained for proper operation. Should adjustments be necessary, ensure appropriate clearance is achieved for optimal functionality.
