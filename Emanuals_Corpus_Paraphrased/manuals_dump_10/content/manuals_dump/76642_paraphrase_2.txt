The CSW200UL Heater G6518CSW should be connected to the power supply with the white and ground wires as illustrated. To operate the heater, turn the switch ON; it is secured in place with screws. CAUTION: Do not touch the heater when it is ON, as it will become hot! Ensure the black (live), white (neutral), and ground wires are correctly connected. The heater is installed below the reducer.
