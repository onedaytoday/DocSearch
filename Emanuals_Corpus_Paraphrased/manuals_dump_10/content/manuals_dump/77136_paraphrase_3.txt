Congratulations on your acquisition of this high-resolution LCD touchmonitor. **Installation and Setup**
This section provides installation guidance for the LCD touchmonitor, including connecting AC/DC power. 

1. Connect the touchmonitor to the power supply, ensuring it is powered off during the setup. Attach one end of the power cord to the touchmonitor and the opposite end to an AC outlet.
2. For touchscreen functionality, connect the RS-232 port on the back of the monitor to the corresponding port on your computer.

Consult the specifications for proper power supply requirements.
