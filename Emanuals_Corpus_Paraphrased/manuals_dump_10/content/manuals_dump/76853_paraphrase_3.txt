### Specifications and General Features

**Product Overview:**
This model features three top-mount countertop thickness options and a height designed to accommodate a range of supply connections with IPS Warranty or straight configurations. The available finishes are Brushed (LKS6465NK, LKX6465NK) and Chrome (LKS6465CR, LKX6465CR). The flow rates are specified as GPM at 9.5 P.S.I.G. and GPM at 8.3 P.S.I.G. 

**CODES/STANDARDS:**
The product adheres to ASME/ANSI A112.18.1, ANSI/NSF, and IAPMO/UPC standards and complies with ADA regulations.

**Warranty:**
Elkay offers a warranty to the purchaser ensuring the faucet is free from defects in material and workmanship. This warranty is applicable as long as the purchaser retains ownership of the faucet. The warranty will cover any defects under normal use but excludes damages caused by misuse, corrosive cleaners, or alterations. 

**Exclusions:**
This warranty does not cover any loss or damages resulting from installation errors or improper maintenance practices. It also does not include costs associated with service or installations, nor does it cover damages caused by external factors. 

**Additional Conditions:**
This warranty is limited to the original purchaser and excludes certain implied warranties, such as merchantability, unless explicitly stated otherwise. 

**Service Claims:**
