### USER MANUAL PRO Z910

**Z910 Service Information**

Congratulations on selecting the Z910 model, which is designed with quality and service in mind. This cleaner should be used by trained service personnel only. It must be connected to a grounded outlet to ensure safety. Do not operate the cleaner in environments with sharp objects, ash, or debris, as this can void the warranty.

For concerns regarding service or repairs, please contact an authorized professional to ensure continued coverage under the warranty. Always refer to local guidelines for recycling and disposal of household items.

**Technical Specifications**  
- Sound Level: IEC 704-3 (A)  
- Type: Synthetic  
- Heat Protection: Compliance with EMC 89/336/EEC, 73/23/EEC, and Directive 93/68/EEC.

**Troubleshooting Instructions**

If the cleaner is not functioning correctly, verify that it is properly connected to a power source. Check for any clogs in the hose or attachments; clean as necessary. If overheating occurs, the motor will shut off automatically. Please ensure proper operation to maintain warranty eligibility.
