### ELMO ESD-CC2X Specifications

The ELMO ESD-CC2X includes the following features: 

- RS-485 communication compatibility 
- LCD interface for PTZ control 
- Built-in protection functions 
- RJ11 joy connector 
- RS232 and RS485 communication rates 
- Parity specification 
- Graphic LCD integrated with RTC-IC 
- Power source: DC 12V, 1.5A
