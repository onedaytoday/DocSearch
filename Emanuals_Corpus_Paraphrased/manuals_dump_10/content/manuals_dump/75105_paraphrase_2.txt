**XEM-10 Counter Planetary Overview**

The XEM-10 Counter (XEM10S) is a compact planetary mixer designed with advanced self-lubricating features, ensuring reliable operation. It includes mechanical safeguards for operational safety when the mixing bowl is lowered, preventing incorrect engagement. For more details, visit Dough and Professional, Inc. at www.electroluxusa.com/professional.

The mixer is equipped with OFF and ON buttons for easy control and features a NEMA 5-15P plug. It is ETL and CETL listed, ensuring compliance with safety standards. Optional attachments, such as various beaters for dough mixing, are available.

The XEM-10 Counter Planetary has the capacity to handle multiple applications, including shortcrust pastry, cold brioche, meat, and puree. Electrolux reserves the right to modify product specifications without prior notice.
