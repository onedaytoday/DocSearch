### ELMO ESD-CC1X Specifications

The ELMO ESD-CC1X features RS-232 communication capabilities, enabling software-controlled backlighting for the LCD display along with various interface and protection functions. 

**Connectivity Details:**
- **Joy Connector:** RJ11
- **Communication Protocols:** RS-232, RS-485
- **Interface Rate:** Parity

**Additional Features:**
- **LCD:** Integrated LCD with RTC IC
- **Power Supply:** DC 12V, 1A

**Compatibility:** Designed exclusively for use with ELMO domes and multiplexers, compatible across multiple platforms.

**Manufacturer:** ELMO
**Location:** Brampton, Plainview, New York, L6T 3T6
