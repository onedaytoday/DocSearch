### Customer Instructions

Dear Customer, please refer to the following operating instructions for important information regarding the operation and maintenance of your appliance. These instructions provide essential guidance, warnings, and notes related to potential malfunctions. If you encounter issues, please consult the "Something Not Working" section for troubleshooting steps.

If you require further assistance, please contact AEG Service Force as described in Chapter 2.

#### Appliance Information

- **Starting the Appliance**: Upon initializing your appliance, ensure food items are appropriately placed on the shelves for optimal cooling.
- **Food Preparation**: Properly prepare and store food items, including defrosting if necessary. Always switch off the appliance before cleaning.
- **Location Considerations**: Make sure the appliance is placed in a suitable location according to building regulations.
- **Safety Warnings**: Avoid using the appliance in ways inconsistent with these instructions, as it may cause damage or safety hazards, particularly in the presence of children or flammable materials.

#### Important Notices

- **Food Storage**: When storing food, ensure it is wrapped securely to prevent contamination or spoilage. Bottled items should be stored upright.
- **Cooling Recommendations**: Keep the appliance's temperature at or below 0°C for optimal food preservation. If the temperature rises above +16°C, the appliance may not function properly.

#### Cleaning Instructions

- Regularly clean the interior and exterior of the appliance and ensure it is disconnected from the power supply during maintenance to prevent electrical shock.
- Use appropriate cleaning agents that will not damage the appliance materials. Avoid mixing cleaning products that could pose hazards.

#### Troubleshooting Guide

If your appliance fails to operate correctly, please refer to the "Something Not Working" section. Common issues and their solutions are detailed here, along with information on how to contact AEG Service Force for more serious malfunctions.

**Warning: This Appliance Requires Earthing**

Ensure that the appliance is properly earthed. The electrical supply should adhere to the rating specified, which is indicated on the appliance. Use ASTA (BS) approved fittings for compliance. The wires are color-coded for connection to the appropriate terminals:

- **Earth (E):** Green/Yellow
- **Neutral (N):** Blue
- **Live (L):** Brown/Red

Be cautious of possible electrical shock hazards associated with disconnection from the power source. 

**Location**  
Install the appliance in a well-ventilated area, away from direct sunlight and heat sources. It should be unobstructed to allow for proper airflow.

**Installation Instructions**  
Ensure the appliance is installed according to the specified guidelines. Attach using the provided screws, ensuring that the housing aligns properly with the hinges. If necessary, adjustments should be made for clearance during operation.

If re-installing, fasten the fixing screws as indicated in the instructions. Compliance with relevant household and electrical regulations (GSG, VBG, and VDE) is mandatory. This appliance conforms to EU directives 73/23/EWG and 89/336/EWG (including 92/31/EWG).
