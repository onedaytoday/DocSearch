**SPECIFICATIONS**

**GENERAL:** High-quality self-rimming bearing. 

**CLASSROOM/INSTITUTIONAL SINK PACKAGE:** Includes models DRKR(Q)2217LC and DRKR(Q)2217RC, featuring scratch- and crack-resistant surfaces with lead compliance. Specifications include LK208513L or LK208513 with high lead content and LK1141A lead compliant with ANSI and ADA standards. Additional models DRKR(Q)2217LVRC or DRKR(Q)2217RVRC include LKVR208513L or LKVR208513, as well as LKVR1141A and LKVR18, with perforated options. 

**OTHER:** The suffix 'L' or 'R' indicates left or right mounting. 

**NOTE:** Unless specified otherwise, products will be provided as shown on the back. All items meet ANSI A112.19.3M standards and are certified by NSF/ANSI61 and UL. 

**SINK DIMENSIONS (INCHES):** Quick-Clip countertop model with back and corner locations is available in various configurations (e.g., DRKR2217, DRKRQ2217, DRKR2217LC). Please refer to the specified dimensions. 

**ADA COMPLIANCE:** The construction of this product adheres to ADA requirements. However, the necessary unobstructed knee space for ADA compliance may not align with functional design preferences. 

All products are printed in the USA with model numbers such as DRKR2217, DRKRQ2217, DRKAD2217, and DRKADQ2217. For product dimensions, see the reverse side. All dimensions are in inches; to convert to millimeters, multiply by 25.4.
